【是什么 / What it is】
香港铁路有限公司（MTR Corp）2024年净利润下降6.9%至146.8亿港元，主要因中国内地及海外铁路项目贡献减少，以及杭州地铁1号线3.8亿港元减值损失。尽管物业开发利润增长至110.8亿港元，公司仍面临资产维护、升级和网络扩张带来的重大财务挑战。
Hong Kong's MTR Corp reported a 6.9% drop in 2024 net profit to HK$14.68 billion, driven by reduced contributions from mainland China and overseas rail projects, plus an unexpected HK$380 million impairment loss on Hangzhou Metro Line 1. While property development profits rose to HK$11.08 billion, the company faces considerable financial challenges from asset maintenance, upgrades, and network expansion.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
此案例反映了后疫情时代公共交通运营商的结构性困境：核心铁路业务受跨境客流恢复缓慢和内地项目回报下滑拖累，同时资本开支压力持续加大。MTR"铁路+物业"模式的可持续性受到考验，也为其他依赖地产补贴交通基建的城市提供了预警信号。
This case illustrates the structural challenges facing public transport operators in the post-pandemic era: core rail operations are dragged down by slow cross-border passenger recovery and declining returns from mainland projects, while capital expenditure pressures mount. The sustainability of MTR's "rail plus property" model is being tested, serving as a warning signal for other cities relying on real estate to subsidize transport infrastructure.
