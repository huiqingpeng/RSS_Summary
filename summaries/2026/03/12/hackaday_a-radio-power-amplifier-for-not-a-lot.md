【是什么 / What it is】

这是一篇关于低成本QRP（低功率）射频功率放大器的制作指南。作者[Guido]基于[Harry Lythall]的电路设计，使用廉价晶体管和洞洞板搭建了一个覆盖1-30 MHz频段、输出约1瓦功率的C类放大器，适用于CW（莫尔斯电码）发射。

This article describes a low-cost QRP (low-power) RF power amplifier project. [Guido] built a Class C amplifier based on [Harry Lythall]'s circuit design, using inexpensive transistors and stripboard to deliver about 1 watt across 1-30 MHz for CW (Morse code) transmission.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

该项目证明了业余无线电制作仍然可以低成本实现，无需大量资金投入。C类放大器虽然非线性且产生谐波，但效率极高，非常适合电池供电的便携QRP设备，体现了业余无线电社区持续的技术共享精神。

This project demonstrates that amateur radio construction remains accessible without significant financial investment. While Class C amplifiers are non-linear and generate harmonics, their high efficiency makes them ideal for battery-powered portable QRP operations, reflecting the ongoing spirit of technical sharing in the amateur radio community.

可以复用此电路设计作为个人QRP电台的功率放大级，或将其改造为其他HF频段项目的基础模块。需要注意添加低通滤波器抑制谐波，并确认当地法规对发射功率和杂散辐射的限制要求。

You can replicate this circuit as a power amplifier stage for personal QRP stations, or adapt it as a building block for other HF band projects. Be sure to add a low-pass filter to suppress harmonics, and verify local regulations regarding transmit power limits and spurious emission requirements.
