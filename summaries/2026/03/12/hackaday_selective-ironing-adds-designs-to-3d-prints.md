【是什么 / What it is】

选择性熨烫（Selective Ironing）是一种创新的3D打印后处理技术，由Julius Curt开发，用于在打印件顶面创建图案而不增加任何高度。该技术结合了CAD建模和自定义脚本，将传统熨烫（ironing）从整面处理改进为局部图案化处理。

Selective Ironing is an innovative post-processing technique for 3D printing developed by Julius Curt, which creates designs on the top surface of prints without adding any height. It combines CAD modeling with custom scripting to transform traditional ironing—normally applied to entire surfaces—into a localized pattern-based process.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

这项技术突破了传统3D打印表面装饰的局限，实现了零高度增加的嵌入式标识、图案或零件编号，为精密超薄打印件的功能集成开辟了新可能。它代表了3D打印后处理从"被动优化"向"主动设计"的演进趋势，有望在电子产品、精密机械等高度敏感领域获得应用。

This technique overcomes the limitations of conventional surface decoration in 3D printing, enabling embedded logos, patterns, or part numbers with zero height addition—opening new possibilities for functional integration in ultra-thin precision prints. It represents an evolution from "passive optimization" to "active design" in post-processing, with promising applications in height-sensitive fields like electronics and precision machinery.
