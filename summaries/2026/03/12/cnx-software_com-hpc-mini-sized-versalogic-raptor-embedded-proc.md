【是什么 / What it is】
VersaLogic Raptor (VL-EPU-5530) 是一款采用 COM HPC Mini 规格（95×80×36mm）的嵌入式处理单元，搭载第13代 Intel Core i5-1345URE 或 U300E 处理器，专为恶劣环境下的高性能计算应用设计，支持 -40°C 至 +85°C 宽温运行并通过 MIL-STD-202H 军规冲击振动认证。

The VersaLogic Raptor (VL-EPU-5530) is a COM HPC Mini form factor (95×80×36mm) embedded processing unit powered by 13th Gen Intel Core i5-1345URE or U300E processors, designed for high-performance computing in harsh environments with -40°C to +85°C operating range and MIL-STD-202H shock/vibration qualification.
