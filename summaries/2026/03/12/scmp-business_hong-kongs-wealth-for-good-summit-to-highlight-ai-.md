【是什么 / What it is】
香港将于3月23日至24日举办第四届"裕泽香江"（Wealth for Good）高峰论坛，汇聚全球家族资本代表，重点探讨人工智能、机器人技术和生物科技领域的投资机会。该峰会于2023年3月首次举办，旨在吸引全球家族办公室和高净值人士，今年将聚焦跨代投资、影响力投资、慈善事业及家族治理等议题。

Hong Kong will host its fourth annual Wealth for Good Summit on March 23-24, bringing together global family capital representatives to explore investment opportunities in artificial intelligence, robotics and biotechnology. Launched in March 2023 to attract global family offices and high-net-worth individuals, this year's event will focus on intergenerational investing, impact investing, philanthropy and family governance.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
在地缘政治不确定性上升的背景下，香港正凭借前瞻性政策、稳定制度及与国家长期战略的紧密衔接，凸显其作为财富避风港和首选枢纽的地位。香港政府设定的目标是到2028年再吸引220家家族办公室落户，此次峰会是实现该目标的关键举措，同时也反映了全球富裕投资者正重新评估资产配置，倾向于将更多财富转移至香港寻求更稳定的避风港。

Amid rising geopolitical uncertainty, Hong Kong is positioning itself as a stable safe haven and preferred hub through forward-looking policies, stable systems and alignment with national long-term strategy. With a government goal of attracting 220 additional family offices by 2028, the summit serves as a crucial step toward this target, while also reflecting a broader trend of wealthy investors reassessing global asset allocation and increasingly channeling wealth into Hong Kong for stability.
