【是什么 / What it is】
香港金融管理局预计将在两周内向汇丰银行（HSBC）和渣打银行（Standard Chartered）颁发首批稳定币牌照，这标志着香港《稳定币条例》自去年八月生效以来的重要监管进展。此次审批将涵盖港元锚定稳定币，首批从36份申请中筛选出少数机构获得许可。

The Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA) is expected to grant the first stablecoin licences to HSBC and Standard Chartered within two weeks, marking a significant regulatory milestone since the Stablecoin Ordinance took effect last August. The approvals will cover Hong Kong dollar-pegged stablecoins, with a small number selected from 36 applications.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这是香港打造全球数字资产中心的关键一步，通过引入受严格监管的传统银行发行稳定币，既防范区块链技术加速资金流动可能带来的金融系统风险，又增强国际投资者对香港数字资产市场的信心。传统大型银行的参与也表明稳定币正从边缘加密创新走向主流金融体系整合。

This is a crucial step in Hong Kong's bid to become a global digital asset hub, using strictly regulated traditional banks to issue stablecoins—balancing financial system stability risks from faster blockchain-enabled money flows with enhanced international investor confidence. The involvement of major incumbent banks signals stablecoins' shift from fringe crypto innovation toward mainstream financial system integration.
