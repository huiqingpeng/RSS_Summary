【是什么 / What it is】
这是一篇介绍 AERIS-10 开源相控阵雷达项目的文章。该雷达工作在 10.5 GHz 频段，由 [Nawfal] 开发，旨在降低传统昂贵的相控阵雷达技术门槛，提供 3 公里和 20 公里两种探测距离版本。
This article introduces the AERIS-10, an open-source phased-array radar project. Operating at 10.5 GHz and developed by [Nawfal], it aims to lower the barrier to traditionally expensive phased-array radar technology, offering two detection range options of 3 km and 20 km.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
相控阵雷达长期被国防、航空等高端领域垄断，成本高昂；开源硬件方案使电子波束扫描等核心能力民主化，让研究者、爱好者和小型团队也能接触先进雷达技术。近期海事、防空、气象等领域关注度上升，低成本雷达方案具有现实意义。
Phased-array radars have long been monopolized by high-end sectors like defense and aviation due to high costs; this open-source hardware democratizes core capabilities like electronic beam steering, making advanced radar accessible to researchers, hobbyists, and small teams. With rising attention to maritime, air defense, and weather domains lately, low-cost radar solutions carry practical significance.

可基于 STM32+FPGA 的硬件架构学习雷达信号处理与相控阵原理，或将其用于无人机避障、区域监控、气象观测等场景；也可扩展代码实现俯仰角扫描，或与其他开源项目结合开发融合感知系统。
You can study radar signal processing and phased-array principles using its STM32+FPGA-based hardware architecture, or apply it to scenarios like drone obstacle avoidance, area surveillance, and weather observation; you can also extend the code to enable elevation scanning, or integrate it with other open-source projects to develop fused perception systems.
