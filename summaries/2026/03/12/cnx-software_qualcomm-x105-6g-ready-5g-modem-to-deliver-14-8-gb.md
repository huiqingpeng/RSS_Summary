【是什么 / What it is】

高通X105是全球首款支持3GPP Release 19标准的调制解调器-射频系统，峰值下载速度达14.8 Gbps、上传达4.2 Gbps，同时支持6G研发和测试。该芯片采用6nm射频收发器，功耗比前代X85降低30%，板级面积缩小15%，并集成第五代AI处理器实现智能流量优化。

The Qualcomm X105 is the world's first 3GPP Release 19-ready modem-RF system delivering peak data speeds of 14.8 Gbps download and 4.2 Gbps upload while enabling 6G development and testing. It features a 6nm RF transceiver with 30% lower power consumption and 15% smaller board footprint than the X85, plus an integrated fifth-generation AI processor for intelligent traffic optimization.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

这标志着5G技术向6G演进的关键里程碑，Release 19标准为未来网络架构奠定基础。其AI驱动的智能优化能力将显著提升移动游戏、视频通话等实时应用的体验，同时卫星通信集成（NR-NTN）扩展了全球连接覆盖范围，对偏远地区和应急通信具有战略意义。

This marks a critical milestone in the evolution from 5G toward 6G, with Release 19 standard laying groundwork for future network architectures. Its AI-driven intelligent optimization significantly enhances real-time applications like mobile gaming and video calling, while integrated satellite communication (NR-NTN) extends global connectivity coverage—strategically important for remote areas and emergency communications.
