【是什么 / What it is】
这篇文章介绍了[PlasmatronX]开发的一种可视化技术，用于捕捉声学悬浮装置内部的声场。该技术结合了Schlieren光学成像和计算机断层扫描（CT）技术，将传统的二维空气密度变化图像重建为三维声场模型，作者称之为"计算机声学断层扫描（computerized acoustical tomography）"。

This article describes a visualization technique developed by [PlasmatronX] to capture the sound field inside an acoustic levitation device. The method combines Schlieren optical imaging with computed tomography (CT) techniques to reconstruct traditional 2D air density variation images into a 3D sound field model, which the author calls "computerized acoustical tomography."

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
声学悬浮装置的校准 traditionally 依赖试错法，因为超声波产生的驻波节点无法直接观察。这项技术首次实现了声场的三维可视化，为精密操控微小颗粒（如药物微胶囊、电子元件）提供了直观的调试工具，同时也展示了低成本CT技术在物理场测量中的创新应用。

Acoustic levitation device calibration traditionally relies on trial-and-error since the standing wave nodes generated by ultrasound cannot be directly observed. This technique enables 3D visualization of sound fields for the first time, providing intuitive debugging tools for precisely manipulating tiny particles (such as drug microcapsules, electronic components), while also demonstrating innovative applications of low-cost CT techniques in physical field measurement.
