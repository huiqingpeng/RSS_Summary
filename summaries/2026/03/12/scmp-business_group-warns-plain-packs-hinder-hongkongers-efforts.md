【是什么 / What it is】
香港政府计划于2027年第二季度推行香烟平装政策，要求移除品牌标志并以统一字体和颜色显示名称。然而，一个关注烟草政策的团体调查发现，88%的吸烟者在新设计下无法区分走私烟与已完税香烟，并呼吁当局扩展烟草印花税系统的功能，让消费者可通过扫描二维码验证香烟是否已完税。

The Hong Kong government plans to introduce plain cigarette packaging by Q2 2027, requiring removal of brand logos and displaying names only in standardized fonts and colors. However, a survey by an advocacy group found that 88% of smokers cannot distinguish smuggled from duty-paid cigarettes under the new design, urging authorities to expand the tobacco stamp duty system to allow consumers to verify tax status via QR code scanning.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这项政策可能产生 unintended consequences（意外后果）：原本旨在减少吸烟吸引力的平装措施，反而可能削弱公众识别非法走私烟的能力，助长走私市场。同时，这也反映出公共健康政策与执法实效之间的张力——政策设计若未充分考虑验证机制，可能损害税收征管并让消费者暴露在质量无保障的走私产品中。

This policy may create unintended consequences: the plain packaging measure originally designed to reduce smoking appeal could instead weaken the public's ability to identify illegal smuggled cigarettes, fueling the black market. It also highlights the tension between public health policy and enforcement effectiveness—if policy design fails to consider verification mechanisms, it may harm tax collection and expose consumers to unregulated smuggled products.
