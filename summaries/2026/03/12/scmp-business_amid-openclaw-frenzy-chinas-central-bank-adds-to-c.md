【是什么 / What it is】
中国人民银行在OpenClaw AI智能体引发全国热潮之际，发布网络安全警告，要求金融行业以"积极而审慎、安全有序"的方式部署人工智能。这是央行年度科技工作会议的一部分，旨在平衡金融科技创新与风险防控。

The People's Bank of China (PBOC) issued cybersecurity warnings amid the nationwide buzz surrounding the OpenClaw AI agent, calling for "proactive yet prudent, safe and orderly" AI deployment in the financial sector. This was part of the central bank's annual technology work conference, aiming to balance fintech innovation with risk prevention and control.

这标志着全球主要经济体对AI金融应用的监管态度正从鼓励创新转向风险防范与高质量发展并重。央行的双重信号——既呼吁"深度融银技融合"释放数字化潜力，又强调"预判极端风险"完善监管框架——反映了AI技术快速迭代与金融系统稳定性之间的深层张力。

This signals a regulatory shift among major global economies from encouraging innovation alone to balancing risk prevention with high-quality development in AI financial applications. The PBOC's dual messaging—calling for deeper banking-technology integration to unleash digital potential while emphasizing "pre-empting extreme risks" to refine regulatory frameworks—reflects the underlying tension between rapid AI iteration and financial system stability.
