【是什么 / What it is】
Turris 推出了一款名为 Omnia NG Wired 的无线路由器，该版本移除了内置 Wi-Fi 7 功能以降低成本。它基于高通 IPQ9574 四核处理器，配备双 10GbE SFP+ 接口和四 2.5GbE RJ45 端口，运行 OpenWrt 基础的 Turris OS 系统，并支持可选的 Wi-Fi 7 升级套件和 4G/5G 蜂窝网络模块。

Turris has introduced the Omnia NG Wired, a Wi-Fi-less router variant that removes built-in Wi-Fi 7 connectivity to reduce costs. It features a Qualcomm IPQ9574 quad-core processor, dual 10GbE SFP+ cages, four 2.5GbE RJ45 ports, runs OpenWrt-based Turris OS, and supports optional Wi-Fi 7 upgrade kits and 4G/5G cellular modules.
