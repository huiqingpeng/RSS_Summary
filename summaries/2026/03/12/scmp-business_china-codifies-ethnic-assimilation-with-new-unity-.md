【是什么 / What it is】
中国通过了一项新的《民族团结法》，以法律形式确立了促进民族融合的政策框架。该法案以2756票赞成、3票反对、3票弃权的压倒性多数获得通过，体现了习近平关于构建"共有精神家园"的号召，旨在为少数民族同化政策提供法定依据。

China has passed a new "Law on Ethnic Unity" that codifies policies promoting ethnic assimilation into a legal framework. The law was adopted by an overwhelming vote of 2,756 in favor, with only three against and three abstentions, reflecting Xi Jinping's call to foster a "shared spiritual home" and providing statutory backing for assimilating minority groups.

This law marks a significant shift in China's ethnic policy from multiculturalism toward assimilation, with profound political and social implications.
