This is an entrepreneurial story about Liu Huiping, a 49-year-old entrepreneur from Anhui province, China. A secondary school dropout who launched his first steamed bun shop in Shanghai in 1998 with a 4,000-yuan (US$580) loan, he built a bun empire generating 180 million yuan annually after surviving failure, violent attacks, and bankruptcy.
