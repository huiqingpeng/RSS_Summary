【是什么 / What it is】
中国船员刘艺文（Liu Yiwen）在霍尔木兹海峡利用公共海事无线电建立了一个"家庭之声电台"，帮助因美以伊冲突而滞留的船员向家人传递平安信息。他所在的商船原计划前往波斯湾装载液化石油气，却因2月28日爆发的战争被困在阿联酋霍尔法坎港附近水域。

A Chinese sailor Liu Yiwen has created a "family's voice station" using public maritime radio to help stranded seamen relay safety messages to their families amid the US-Israel-Iran conflict. His Singapore-registered commercial vessel, originally bound for the Persian Gulf to load LPG, has been stuck near Khor Fakkan harbour since the war broke out on February 28.

这一案例凸显了现代战争中平民通信基础设施的脆弱性，以及个体在危机时刻自发构建应急通信网络的创造力。同时，它也反映了全球化航运业中跨国船员（尤其是中国籍海员）在地缘政治冲突中的困境与互助需求。

This case highlights the fragility of civilian communication infrastructure during modern warfare and the ingenuity of individuals in building emergency networks. It also reflects the plight of multinational seafarers—particularly Chinese sailors—in global shipping when caught in geopolitical conflicts, demonstrating grassroots mutual aid under extreme conditions.
