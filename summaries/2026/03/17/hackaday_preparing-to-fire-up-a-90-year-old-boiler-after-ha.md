【是什么 / What it is】
这篇文章记录了英国Claymills泵站志愿者团队修复一台90年历史、已停用50年的兰开夏锅炉（#1号）的最后阶段工作。修复内容包括重新组装砖砌结构、处理卡死的阻尼器轴、安装铸铁灰箱和自动撒煤机，以及更换炉门等关键部件。

This article documents the final stage of restoration work by volunteers at Claymills Pumping Station in the UK on a 90-year-old Lancashire boiler (#1) that has been out of service for 50 years. The restoration includes reassembling brickwork, addressing a seized damper shaft, installing cast-iron ash boxes and automatic sprinkler stokers, and replacing furnace doors.

This project demonstrates both the technical complexity of industrial heritage preservation and the value of community volunteerism, while providing a physical case study for understanding early 20th-century boiler automation technology. Successfully restarting this boiler will offer invaluable living heritage resources for historical engineering education, steam power research, and industrial tourism.
