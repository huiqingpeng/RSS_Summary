【是什么 / What it is】
根据英国知识产权律所Mathys & Squire的报告，中国在全球反无人机技术专利申请中处于领先地位，去年提交了82项专利，远超美国（22项）和韩国（6项）。反无人机技术专利整体激增27%，主要涵盖激光、微波和干扰等定向能技术，以应对无人机在现代战争和民用安全领域带来的新威胁。

According to a report by UK intellectual property law firm Mathys & Squire, China leads the world in anti-drone technology patent applications, filing 82 patents last year—far ahead of the US (22) and South Korea (6). Overall anti-drone patent filings surged 27%, primarily covering directed energy technologies such as lasers, microwaves, and jamming systems to address emerging threats from drones in modern warfare and civilian security.
