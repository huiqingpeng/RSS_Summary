【是什么 / What it is】

This is a Spring 2026 analysis report on the open-source AI ecosystem on Hugging Face, tracking changes over the past year (2025) across competitive landscape, geographic distribution, technical trends, and emerging communities based on platform data. The report compares the dynamics between the U.S. and China in open-source AI and explores key topics including sovereign AI, corporate participation, and community evolution.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
开源AI正从"消费预训练模型"转向"主动创造衍生产物"的参与式生态，Hugging Face用户、模型和数据集数量接近翻倍，但下载量高度集中于0.01%的头部模型。中国已在月下载量和总下载量上超越美国，占41%份额，同时独立开发者占比从17%升至39%，显示权力结构从大型工业实验室向个人和小型集体转移。

Open-source AI is shifting from "consuming pre-trained models" to a participatory ecosystem of "actively creating derivative artifacts," with Hugging Face users, models, and datasets nearly doubling—yet downloads are highly concentrated in just 0.01% of top models. China has surpassed the U.S. in monthly and total downloads, capturing 41% share, while independent developers rose from 17% to 39% of downloads, indicating a power shift from large industry labs to individuals and small collectives.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
开发者可关注细分领域的专业化社区而非追逐头部模型，利用量化、适配和再分发等中间环节参与生态；企业可借鉴财富500强中30%以上的做法，将开源模型作为默认组件以降低封闭系统的成本和灵活性限制；政策制定者和机构可探索开源权重模型在主权AI中的应用，实现本地数据微调、国产硬件部署和监管透明化。

Developers can focus on specialized communities in niche domains rather than chasing headliner models, participating in the ecosystem through intermediation like quantization, adaptation, and redistribution; enterprises can follow the 30%+ of Fortune 500 companies by adopting open models as default components to reduce costs and flexibility constraints of closed systems; policymakers and institutions can explore open-weight models for sovereign AI, enabling local data fine-tuning, domestic hardware deployment, and regulatory transparency.
