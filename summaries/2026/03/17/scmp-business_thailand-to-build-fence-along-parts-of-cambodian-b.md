【是什么 / What it is】
泰国军方计划下月开始在柬泰边境的尖竹汶府修建1.3公里长的边境围栏，包括实体围墙和配备传感器及监控摄像头的电子围栏。这一举措源于两国去年因长期紧张局势爆发致命冲突后的安全需求，且已获得柬埔寨方面的同意。

Thailand's military plans to build a 1.3km border fence in Chanthaburi province starting next month, comprising both physical walls and electronic fences with sensors and CCTV cameras. This follows deadly clashes between the two countries last year due to long-standing tensions, and has received approval from Cambodia.
