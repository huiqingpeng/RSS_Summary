【是什么 / What it is】
Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang announced that the company is restarting production of its H200 AI chip, a China-specific variant designed to comply with US export restrictions. The chip, based on older Hopper technology, had its manufacturing halted last year due to escalating regulatory challenges from both the US and Chinese governments.
