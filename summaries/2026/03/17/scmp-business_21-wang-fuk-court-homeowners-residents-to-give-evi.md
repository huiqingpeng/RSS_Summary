【是什么 / What it is】
香港独立调查委员会公布了一份包含37名"涉事方"的名单，其中包括14名前业主立案法团成员和7名居民，他们将在针对去年11月大埔王富阁致命火灾的八场证据听证会上作证。这场火灾是香港数十年来最严重的火灾事故，造成168人死亡、近5000名居民流离失所，政府成立独立机构调查火灾原因及大型建筑维修翻新工程中的系统性问题。

An independent committee investigating Hong Kong's deadliest fire in decades has released a list of 37 "involved parties," including 14 members of the former owners' corporation and 7 residents, who will testify at eight evidential hearings. The blaze at Wang Fuk Court in Tai Po last November killed 168 people and displaced nearly 5,000 residents, prompting the government to establish the body to examine the cause and identify systemic problems in large-scale building maintenance and renovation works.
