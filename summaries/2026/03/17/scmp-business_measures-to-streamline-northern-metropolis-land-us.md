【是什么 / What it is】
香港发展局提出一项专项立法，旨在大幅简化北部都会区土地用途变更程序，将审批时间从通常的九个月缩短至两个月。该立法包含六项附属法规，涵盖成立法定机构、加快土地收回补偿、采用新型建筑技术、简化噪音许可程序、促进跨境人员流动以及延长临时土地用途有效期等措施。

The Development Bureau of Hong Kong has proposed designated legislation to drastically streamline land-use change procedures for the Northern Metropolis project, reducing approval time from the typical nine months to just two months. The legislation comprises six subsidiary laws covering the establishment of statutory firms, expedited land resumption payments, adoption of new building technologies, simplified noise permission procedures, facilitation of cross-border people flow, and extended validity for temporary land use.
