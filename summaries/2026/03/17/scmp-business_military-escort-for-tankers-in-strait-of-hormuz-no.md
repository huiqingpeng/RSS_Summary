【是什么 / What it is】
国际海事组织（IMO）秘书长Arsenio Dominguez表示，在霍尔木兹海峡为油轮提供军事护航并非可持续的解决方案。此前美国总统特朗普呼吁组建国际海军行动以保护伊朗沿海的石油运输，但遭到德国、英国、西班牙等多个国家的冷淡回应。

The IMO Secretary General Arsenio Dominguez stated that military escorts for tankers in the Strait of Hormuz are not a sustainable solution. This comes after US President Donald Trump called for an international naval operation to secure oil shipments off the Iranian coast, but received a lukewarm response from countries including Germany, the UK, and Spain.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
霍尔木兹海峡是全球最重要的石油运输通道之一，任何中断都会对全球能源市场造成重大冲击。军事护航虽能降低风险，但无法从根本上解决地区冲突，反而可能加剧紧张局势；同时，被困海湾的船员安全和港口设施遭袭问题也日益严峻。

The Strait of Hormuz is one of the world's most critical oil transit chokepoints, and any disruption would severely impact global energy markets. While military escorts may reduce risks, they cannot address the root causes of regional conflicts and may escalate tensions; meanwhile, the safety of stranded crews and attacks on port facilities are growing concerns.
