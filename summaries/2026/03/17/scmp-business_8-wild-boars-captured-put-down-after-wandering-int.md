【是什么 / What it is】
香港渔农自然护理署（AFCD）于周二在黄大仙竹园（北）邨附近捕获并人道毁灭了八头野猪（两头大野猪和六头小野猪）。事件源于居民报案，当局出动兽医使用麻醉枪将野猪麻醉后移除，理由是野猪在市区游荡对公共安全构成威胁。

Hong Kong's Agriculture, Fisheries and Conservation Department (AFCD) captured and humanely dispatched eight wild boars (two large adults and six smaller ones) near Chuk Yuen (North) Estate in Wong Tai Sin on Tuesday. The operation followed a resident's report, with veterinarians deployed to sedate the animals using tranquilliser guns, citing public safety threats posed by wild pigs roaming in urban areas.

此事件反映了香港野生动物管理政策的重大转变——当局于2021年底终止了"捕捉后迁移"政策，改为对进入市区的野猪实施人道毁灭。这凸显了高密度城市中人与自然冲突的加剧，以及政府在公共安全与动物福利之间寻求平衡时所面临的争议性抉择。

This incident reflects a significant shift in Hong Kong's wildlife management policy—authorities terminated the "capture and relocate" approach in late 2021, replacing it with humane dispatch for urban wild boars. It highlights intensifying human-nature conflicts in high-density cities and the controversial trade-offs governments face between public safety and animal welfare.
