This article discusses the impact of the US pharmaceutical industry's "reshoring" trend on Chinese contract drug makers (such as WuXi AppTec, WuXi Biologics, and WuXi XDC). While order backlogs are sufficient for 2026-2027, analysts warn of uncertain demand beyond 2028 as major US pharma companies plan to build in-house capacity and shift to alternative markets like India and Singapore.
