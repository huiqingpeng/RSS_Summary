【是什么 / What it is】
KiCad 10 是开源电子设计自动化（EDA）软件的最新主版本，于 2025 年 2 月 KiCad 9 发布后由数百名贡献者完成 7,609 次提交而推出。该版本带来了深色模式、图形化 DRC 规则编辑器、Allegro/PADS/gEDA 文件导入器以及原理图和 PCB 编辑器的多项功能改进。

KiCad 10 is the latest major release of the open-source electronic design automation (EDA) software, developed by hundreds of contributors with 7,609 unique commits following KiCad 9's release in February 2025. This version introduces dark mode, a graphical DRC rule editor, importers for Allegro/PADS/gEDA files, and numerous enhancements to both the Schematic and PCB editors.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
作为开源硬件生态的核心工具，KiCad 10 的发布降低了专业 PCB 设计的门槛，其新增的商业 EDA 文件导入功能有助于打破工具锁定、促进设计迁移。深色模式和图形化 DRC 编辑器则显著提升了用户体验，使复杂规则配置更直观，有望加速开源硬件在工业和商业领域的采用。

As a cornerstone tool in the open-source hardware ecosystem, KiCad 10 lowers the barrier to professional PCB design, while its new commercial EDA file importers help break vendor lock-in and facilitate design migration. Dark mode and the graphical DRC editor significantly improve usability, making complex rule configuration more intuitive and potentially accelerating open-source hardware adoption in industrial and commercial sectors.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
硬件开发者可立即通过官方 PPA 或下载页升级至 KiCad 10，利用新导入器迁移遗留项目；设计团队可尝试 Variants 功能管理多 BOM 配置，或使用 Tuning Profiles 优化高速信号时序。建议先在非关键项目上验证兼容性，关注社区反馈以规避潜在的库文件迁移问题。

Hardware developers can upgrade to KiCad 10 immediately via official PPA or download pages, using the new importers to migrate legacy projects; design teams can explore the Variants feature for multi-BOM management or Tuning Profiles for high-speed signal optimization. It's recommended to validate compatibility on non-critical projects first and monitor community feedback to avoid potential library migration issues.
