【是什么 / What it is】
Radxa AICore DX-M1M 是一款基于 DeepX DX-M1M 神经处理单元（NPU）的紧凑型 M.2 2242 边缘 AI 加速模块，在仅 3W 功耗下可提供高达 25 TOPS（INT8）的 AI 算力。该模块采用 PCIe Gen3 x2 接口，兼容 x86 和 Arm 系统（包括 Raspberry Pi 5 和 Radxa 单板计算机），专为工业机器人臂、自主移动机器人、边缘服务器、无人机和 AIoT 设备设计。

The Radxa AICore DX-M1M is a compact M.2 2242 edge AI acceleration module built around the DeepX DX-M1M neural processing unit (NPU), delivering up to 25 TOPS (INT8) AI performance at just 3W power consumption. Featuring a PCIe Gen3 x2 interface, it works with both x86 and Arm systems (including Raspberry Pi 5 and Radxa SBCs) and is designed for industrial robot arms, autonomous mobile robots, edge servers, drones, and AIoT devices.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
该模块代表了边缘 AI 硬件的重要演进方向：在极低功耗（3W）下实现高性能（25 TOPS），解决了传统 AI 加速器功耗过高、难以嵌入电池供电或散热受限场景的核心痛点。其 M.2 标准化形态和即插即用特性大幅降低了 AI 硬件部署门槛，使开发者无需复杂的定制硬件设计即可快速集成 AI 能力。

This module represents a significant evolution in edge AI hardware: achieving high performance (25 TOPS) at extremely low power (3W), solving the core pain point of traditional AI accelerators consuming too much power for battery-operated or thermally constrained scenarios. Its standardized M.2 form factor and plug-and-play nature dramatically lower the barrier to AI hardware deployment, allowing developers to quickly integrate AI capabilities without complex custom hardware design.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
开发者可利用 DEEPX DXNN SDK 将 PyTorch、ONNX、TensorFlow 和 Keras 模型编译为 DXNN 格式，在 Windows 或 Ubuntu 系统上快速部署；也可通过 DX-All Suite 一键安装完整环境。典型应用场景包括：为 Raspberry Pi 5 添加实时视频分析能力（如人脸检测、姿态估计）、在无人机上实现低功耗目标追踪、或为工业边缘网关构建轻量级 AI 推理节点。

Developers can use the DEEPX DXNN SDK to compile PyTorch, ONNX, TensorFlow, and Keras models into DXNN format for rapid deployment on Windows or Ubuntu systems, or use the DX-All Suite for one-click environment setup. Typical application scenarios include: adding real-time video analytics capabilities (face detection, pose estimation) to Raspberry Pi 5, implementing low-power object tracking on drones, or building lightweight AI inference nodes for industrial edge gateways.
