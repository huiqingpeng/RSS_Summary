【是什么 / What it is】

研华（Advantech）推出了一款名为 EKI-6333BE-4GD 的工业级 Wi-Fi 7 无线接入点，专为自主系统（如 AMR 车队、机器人协作和 XR 远程操作）设计。该设备支持三频段（2.4/5/6 GHz）、冗余 12V-48V DC 电源输入，并能在 -40°C 至 75°C 的极端温度下运行，符合工业网络安全标准 IEC 62443。

Advantech has launched the EKI-6333BE-4GD, an industrial-grade Wi-Fi 7 tri-band wireless access point designed for autonomous systems such as AMR fleets, robotics collaboration, and XR remote operations. It features redundant 12V-48V DC power inputs, operates in temperatures from -40°C to 75°C, and complies with the IEC 62443 industrial cybersecurity standard.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

Wi-Fi 7 的 Multi-Link Operation (MLO) 和高达 5.8 Gbps 的吞吐量，为工业 4.0 场景中的低延迟、高可靠无线通信提供了关键基础设施，解决了传统 Wi-Fi 在移动机器人和实时控制中的瓶颈。随着自主移动机器人（AMR）和扩展现实（XR）远程操作的普及，工业级网络设备正从"可选"变为"必需"，网络安全合规（IEC 62443、WPA3）也成为工厂数字化的硬性门槛。

Wi-Fi 7's Multi-Link Operation (MLO) and throughput up to 5.8 Gbps provide critical infrastructure for low-latency, high-reliability wireless communication in Industry 4.0 scenarios, addressing bottlenecks of traditional Wi-Fi in mobile robotics and real-time control. As AMR fleets and XR remote operations proliferate, industrial-grade networking equipment is shifting from "optional" to "essential," with cybersecurity compliance (IEC 62443, WPA3) becoming a mandatory threshold for factory digitalization.
