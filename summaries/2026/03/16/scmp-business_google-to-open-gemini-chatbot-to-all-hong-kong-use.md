【是什么 / What it is】
Google宣布将分阶段向香港所有用户开放其生成式AI服务Gemini，首先推出网页版，随后推出移动版聊天机器人和虚拟助手。此前，香港用户因OpenAI、Anthropic等开发商基于监管合规不确定性而实施的地理封锁，不得不依赖VPN或第三方平台访问AI服务。

Google announced it will gradually roll out its generative AI service Gemini to all users in Hong Kong, starting with the web app before introducing a mobile version for the chatbot and virtual assistant. Previously, Hong Kong users had to rely on VPNs or third-party platforms to access AI services due to geofencing implemented by developers like OpenAI and Anthropic over regulatory and compliance uncertainties.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
此举标志着香港正式融入全球主流AI生态，消除了技术访问壁垒，有望激发本地创意产业和生产力发展。同时，这也反映出Google对香港市场的长期承诺，以及在区域监管环境变化下科技巨头重新评估市场策略的趋势。

This move signifies Hong Kong's formal integration into the global mainstream AI ecosystem, removing technical access barriers and potentially stimulating local creative industries and productivity. It also reflects Google's long-term commitment to the Hong Kong market and the broader trend of tech giants reassessing market strategies amid evolving regional regulatory environments.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
创作者和学生可直接利用Gemini的多模态能力（文本、图像、音频交互及多媒体生成）进行内容创作和学习辅助。企业和专业人士可将其整合到工作流程中提升效率，同时关注Google后续构建的完整AI生态系统以把握更多本地化AI应用机会。

Creators and students can directly leverage Gemini's multimodal capabilities (text, image, audio interaction and multimedia generation) for content creation and learning assistance. Businesses and professionals can integrate it into workflows to boost efficiency, while keeping an eye on Google's subsequent full-stack AI ecosystem development to seize more localized AI application opportunities.
