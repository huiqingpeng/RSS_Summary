【是什么 / What it is】
香港政府推出新措施，鼓励18岁青年登记成为选民。年轻人在换领成人身份证时，可选择以自我声明方式豁免提交地址证明，并可在入境事务处办事处即场进行宣誓。
The Hong Kong government has launched a new initiative to encourage young people aged 18 to register as voters. When replacing their juvenile identity cards with adult ones, young people can opt for self-declarations to be exempted from producing proof of address and take oaths on-site at Immigration Department offices.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
此举降低了青年选民登记的门槛，解决了许多年轻人因缺乏地址证明而无法登记的障碍，有望提升年轻群体的政治参与度。这也反映出政府对扩大选民基础、增强选举代表性的重视。
This measure lowers the barrier for young voter registration, addressing the obstacle many young people face due to lack of address proof, and is expected to boost political participation among the youth demographic. It also reflects the government's emphasis on expanding the voter base and enhancing electoral representation.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
若你是符合资格的香港青年，可在4月底至6月初期间前往入境事务处办理身份证时同步完成选民登记。政策制定者可参考此"一站式"服务模式，将行政流程整合以提升公共服务效率。
If you are an eligible Hong Kong youth, you can complete voter registration when visiting the Immigration Department for identity card renewal between late April and early June. Policymakers can reference this "one-stop" service model, integrating administrative processes to improve public service efficiency.
