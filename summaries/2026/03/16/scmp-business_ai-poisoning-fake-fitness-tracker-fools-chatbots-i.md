【是什么 / What it is】

这是一篇关于中国央视315晚会曝光"AI投毒"（AI poisoning）现象的报道。报道揭示了不良商家如何利用生成式引擎优化（GEO）技术，通过批量发布虚假的专家评测、行业排名和用户评论，操纵AI聊天机器人的推荐结果，让虚构产品"Apollo-9"健身手环被AI推荐为优质选择。

This article reports on China Central Television's 315 Gala exposé of "AI poisoning," revealing how bad actors exploit Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) techniques to manipulate AI chatbot recommendations. The investigation demonstrated how a system called Liqing automatically posted fabricated expert reviews, industry rankings, and user feedback to make a fictional fitness tracker "Apollo-9" appear in AI-generated top recommendations.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

这一事件暴露了AI信息生态系统的新漏洞：当AI模型从开放网络抓取训练数据时，恶意制造的虚假信息会污染其知识库，导致"垃圾进、垃圾出"的恶性循环。随着生成式AI成为越来越多人获取信息的主要入口，GEO操纵可能演变为比传统SEO更具破坏性的信息操控手段，亟需监管介入。

This incident exposes a critical vulnerability in AI information ecosystems: when models scrape training data from the open web, maliciously fabricated content contaminates their knowledge bases, creating a "garbage in, garbage out" cycle. As generative AI becomes the primary information gateway for more users, GEO manipulation could evolve into a more destructive form of information control than traditional SEO, urgently requiring regulatory intervention.
