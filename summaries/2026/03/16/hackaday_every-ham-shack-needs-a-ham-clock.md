【是什么 / What it is】
Ham Clock CYD 是一款专为业余无线电（ham radio）操作员设计的独立时钟设备，基于廉价的 ESP32 彩色触摸屏（CYD）开发，配备温湿度传感器和电池备份的实时时钟模块。它无需互联网即可运行，但连接网络时可显示太阳活动预报和短波/VHF 频段传播条件等专业数据。
Ham Clock CYD is a standalone clock device designed for amateur radio operators, built around the affordable ESP32-based color touchscreen display (CYD), with additional temperature/humidity sensor and battery-backed RTC module. It functions without internet access but can display specialized data like solar forecasts and HF/VHF band conditions when connected.
