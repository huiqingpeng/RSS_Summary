【是什么 / What it is】
美国联邦上诉法院以2比1的投票结果，允许特朗普政府临时恢复"第三国驱逐"政策，即可以将无证移民驱逐到非其原籍国的其他国家。此前，地区法官Brian Murphy曾裁定该政策违法并予以阻止，但上诉法院解除了这一禁令，允许政府在诉讼期间继续执行驱逐。

A US federal appeals court ruled 2-1 to allow the Trump administration to temporarily resume "third-country deportations," which send undocumented migrants to countries other than their home nations. This overturned a district court block, permitting deportations to continue while the case is litigated.

This ruling marks a significant legal victory for the Trump administration's immigration enforcement strategy, potentially expanding operational capacity for deporting undocumented migrants. The policy bypasses traditional repatriation processes, raising widespread concerns about due process and human rights protections, while reflecting deep judicial divisions on immigration issues.
