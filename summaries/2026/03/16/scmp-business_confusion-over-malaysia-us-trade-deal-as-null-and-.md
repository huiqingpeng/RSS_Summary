【是什么 / What it is】
马来西亚政府因贸易部长 Johari Abdul Ghani 撤回"美马贸易协议已无效"的言论而陷入尴尬境地。该协议于 2024 年 10 月签署，承诺以 2400 亿美元投资和采购换取 19% 的优惠关税税率，但美国最高法院 2 月裁定特朗普总统滥用紧急权力加征关税的裁决，使协议的法律基础受到质疑。

The Malaysian government has been put on the defensive after Trade Minister Johari Abdul Ghani retracted his claim that the Malaysia-US trade deal was "null and void." The agreement, signed in October 2024, promised US$240 billion in investments and purchases in exchange for a preferential 19% tariff rate, but its legal foundation was questioned following a US Supreme Court ruling in February that found President Trump had unconstitutionally used emergency powers to impose tariffs.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
此事暴露了小国在与美国进行双边贸易谈判时面临的结构性脆弱性——协议可能因美国国内政治和法律变动而瞬间失效。同时，这也反映了特朗普 2.0 时代贸易政策的高度不确定性，以及各国政府在"交易式外交"中难以预测承诺稳定性的困境。

This incident exposes the structural vulnerability of smaller nations in bilateral trade negotiations with the US—agreements can become instantly precarious due to American domestic political and legal shifts. It also reflects the high uncertainty of trade policy in Trump 2.0 era, and the dilemma facing governments in predicting the stability of commitments under "transactional diplomacy."

Business decision-makers should include "political risk clauses" or "legal change protection mechanisms" in cross-border agreements, and avoid building core business assumptions on single-market commitments.
