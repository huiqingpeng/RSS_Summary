【是什么 / What it is】
中国研究人员成功开发了一项突破性的低温器官保存技术，能够在零下温度将动物器官冷冻数天后复苏并移植。这项技术旨在大幅延长器官的保存时间，解决当前器官移植中因时间限制导致的严重浪费问题。

Chinese researchers have developed a breakthrough cryopreservation technology that can freeze animal organs at sub-zero temperatures for several days, revive them, and successfully transplant them. This technology aims to dramatically extend organ preservation time to address the severe waste caused by current time constraints in organ transplantation.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
目前心脏离体后仅能保存6小时，导致美国大量移植心脏被丢弃；若将保存时间延长至24小时，可清除全美器官移植等待名单。这项技术不仅能挽救更多生命，还可能建立全球化的器官分配网络，彻底改变器官移植的地理和时间限制。

Currently, hearts can only be preserved for 6 hours after removal, leading to massive discards in the US; extending preservation to 24 hours could clear the entire US transplant waiting list. This technology could not only save more lives but also enable a global organ distribution network, fundamentally transforming the geographical and temporal constraints of organ transplantation.
