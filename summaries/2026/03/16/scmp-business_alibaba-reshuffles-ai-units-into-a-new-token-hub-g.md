【是什么 / What it is】

阿里巴巴集团于周一宣布成立"Token Hub"（ATH）事业群，由CEO吴泳铭直接领导，将旗下所有核心AI团队和产品整合到统一架构下，包括通义实验室、模型即服务业务、Qwen个人AI助手业务单元、钉钉AI企业工作流团队"悟空"以及AI创新业务单元。该组织围绕"token"概念构建——即AI大语言模型处理和生成文本、图像或代码时的基本计算与输出单位。

Alibaba Group announced on Monday the establishment of the "Token Hub" (ATH) Business Group, led directly by CEO Eddie Wu, consolidating all its core AI teams and products under one unified structure—including Tongyi Laboratory, model-as-a-service business, Qwen personal AI assistant unit, Dingtalk AI enterprise workflow team "Wukong," and a separate AI Innovation unit. The organization is built around the concept of the "token," the fundamental unit of computation and output generated by AI large language models when processing and producing text, images, or code.
