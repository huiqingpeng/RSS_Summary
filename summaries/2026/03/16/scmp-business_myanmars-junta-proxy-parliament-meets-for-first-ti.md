【是什么 / What it is】
缅甸军政府代理议会自2021年政变以来首次召开会议。这个新成立的议会由军方盟友和前将军组成，旨在为被联合国和欧盟称为" sham（骗局）"的政权披上合法外衣。在仅覆盖全国三分之一地区的选举中，军方支持的联邦巩固与发展党（USDP）以仅50%的投票率赢得了81%的席位。

Myanmar's junta-proxy parliament convened for the first time since the 2021 coup. The new assembly, stacked with military allies and ex-generals, aims to legitimize a regime branded a sham by the United Nations and EU. In an election covering barely a third of the country, the army-backed Union Solidarity and Development Party (USDP) won 81% of seats on a mere 50% turnout.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这一发展标志着缅甸军方试图通过制度化的民主形式巩固权力，尽管国际社会普遍不承认其合法性。宪法保留25%议席给军队的安排，加上USDP控制的席位，使军方实际上完全掌控立法机构，包括决定总统人选的关键权力。这种模式可能成为其他威权政权"选举式威权主义"的参考案例。

This development marks the military's attempt to consolidate power through institutionalized democratic forms, despite widespread international rejection of its legitimacy. The constitutional reservation of 25% of seats for the military, combined with USDP-controlled seats, gives the armed forces effective control over the legislature, including the crucial power to determine the presidency. This model may serve as a reference case of "electoral authoritarianism" for other authoritarian regimes.
