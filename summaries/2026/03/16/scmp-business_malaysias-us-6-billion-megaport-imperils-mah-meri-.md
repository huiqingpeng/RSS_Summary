【是什么 / What it is】

This article reports on Malaysia's US$6 billion Port Klang expansion plan, a major infrastructure project that threatens the fishing livelihoods of the indigenous Mah Meri community on Pulau Carey in Selangor state. Local fishermen say their catches have already declined steadily over the past five years, and the port development will further encroach upon their traditional fishing grounds.
