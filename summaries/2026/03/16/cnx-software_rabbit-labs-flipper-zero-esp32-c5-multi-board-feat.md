【是什么 / What it is】
这是一款由 Rabbit-Labs EU 设计的 Flipper Zero 扩展板，基于 ESP32-C5 双频 Wi-Fi 6 微控制器，集成了 TI CC1101 次 GHz 收发器、GPS 模块、SD 卡槽和 USB-C 接口。相比同类 bulky 产品（如 ESP32 Marauder 5G Apex 5），它采用更紧凑的设计，定位为简化替代方案。

This is an expansion board for the Flipper Zero designed by Rabbit-Labs EU, built around the ESP32-C5 dual-band Wi-Fi 6 microcontroller with integrated TI CC1101 sub-GHz transceiver, GPS module, SD card slot, and USB-C port. Positioned as a stripped-down, more compact alternative to bulkier competitors like the ESP32 Marauder 5G Apex 5.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
该扩展板将 Wi-Fi 6、蓝牙 5.0、Zigbee/Thread/Matter、次 GHz RF 和 GPS 集成到单一紧凑设备中，大幅扩展了 Flipper Zero 的无线安全测试能力，支持 wardriving、包捕获和位置日志记录。其模块化设计反映了渗透测试工具向多协议、便携化发展的趋势，同时 $125 的定价使专业级 RF 分析工具更易获取。

This board significantly expands the Flipper Zero's wireless security testing capabilities by integrating Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.0, Zigbee/Thread/Matter, sub-GHz RF, and GPS into a single compact device—enabling wardriving, packet capture, and location logging. Its modular design reflects the trend toward multi-protocol, portable penetration testing tools, while the $125 price point makes professional-grade RF analysis more accessible.
