【是什么 / What it is】
尼泊尔选举委员会确认37岁的跨性别女性普里蒂卡·什雷斯塔（Pritika Shrestha）成为该国首位跨性别女性国会议员，她通过比例代表制代表中间派政党"国家独立党"（RSP）进入275人的众议院。这是尼泊尔LGBTQ群体政治代表性的历史性突破，该国约有90万人自认属于性少数群体，且拥有南亚地区最先进的LGBTQ权利法律框架。

Nepal's Election Commission confirmed 37-year-old Pritika Shrestha as the country's first transgender woman lawmaker, entering the 275-member House of Representatives through proportional representation from the centrist Rastriya Swatantra Party (RSP). This marks a historic breakthrough in political representation for Nepal's LGBTQ community, which numbers approximately 900,000 people and operates under some of South Asia's most progressive legal frameworks for LGBTQ rights.
