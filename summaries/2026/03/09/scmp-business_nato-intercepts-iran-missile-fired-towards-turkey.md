【是什么 / What it is】

NATO intercepted a ballistic missile fired from Iran towards Turkey on March 10, marking the second such incident within a week. This occurred amid Iran's retaliatory strikes against multiple nations, with missile debris landing in Gaziantep province, approximately 150km from Incirlik Air Base—where hundreds of US military personnel are stationed and US nuclear weapons are believed to be stored.
