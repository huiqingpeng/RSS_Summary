【是什么 / What it is】
美国能源部官员表示，美国正通过电子废弃物回收、关键矿物精炼和加工技术的创新，试图打破中国在关键矿物领域的主导地位。这些"改变游戏规则"的技术突破包括更高效的电池黑粉回收工艺，以及能够在同一流程中处理多种关键矿物的新型加工技术。

A US Department of Energy official stated that America is attempting to break China's dominance in critical minerals through innovations in electronic waste recycling, refining, and processing technologies. These "game-changing" breakthroughs include more efficient black mass recovery processes and novel processing technologies that can handle multiple types of critical minerals within the same workflow.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
关键矿物是电动汽车、可再生能源设备和国防技术的核心原材料，中国目前在该领域占据战略垄断地位已有30年。美国若能在回收和加工技术上取得突破，不仅能缩短供应链、减少对外依赖，还能在全球绿色能源转型中重获战略主动权，这对国家安全和气候目标都具有深远影响。

Critical minerals are core raw materials for electric vehicles, renewable energy equipment, and defense technologies, with China holding a strategic monopoly in this sector for 30 years. If the US achieves breakthroughs in recycling and processing technologies, it could not only shorten supply chains and reduce foreign dependence but also regain strategic initiative in the global green energy transition—carrying profound implications for both national security and climate goals.
