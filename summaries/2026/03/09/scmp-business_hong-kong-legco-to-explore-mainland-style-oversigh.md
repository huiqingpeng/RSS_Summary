【是什么 / What it is】
香港立法会主席李慧琼提议借鉴中国人大的执法检查制度，建立专门机制监督已获批法案和资金的实际执行情况。这一提议是在国务院副总理丁薛祥呼吁香港立法会"务实有为"之后提出的，李慧琼同时强调香港首个五年计划不会损害"一国两制"原则。

Hong Kong Legislative Council president Starry Lee proposed adopting a mainland-style law enforcement inspection system to scrutinise the implementation of approved bills and funding. The proposal follows Vice-Premier Ding Xuexiang's call for the city's legislature to deliver more "practical outcomes," with Lee also defending Hong Kong's first five-year plan as consistent with the "one country, two systems" principle.

This signals a potential shift of Hong Kong's legislative oversight toward mainland models, reflecting Beijing's higher expectations for governance efficiency and further adjustment of executive-legislative relations under the "patriots administering Hong Kong" framework. If implemented, it would replace the traditional reliance on lawmakers' questions with more substantive constraints on government implementation.
