【是什么 / What it is】
本文讨论中国专家对马斯克"太空数据中心"构想的回应。北京大学计算机科学家高文指出，中国AI数据中心并不存在电力瓶颈，因此无需将计算设施搬入太空；相反，中国应优先发展在轨计算系统，用于直接分析卫星采集的数据。

This article covers Chinese experts' response to Elon Musk's "space-based data centres" concept. Computer scientist Gao Wen from Peking University argues that electricity supply isn't a bottleneck for China's AI data centres, making space relocation unnecessary; instead, China should prioritize orbital computing systems to analyze satellite data directly.

This reflects China's independent judgment in space-AI strategy: avoiding blind following of US commercial space ventures, instead formulating differentiated solutions based on domestic priorities (data transmission efficiency vs. energy shortage). It also reveals orbital computing as an emerging arena for technological competition among major powers.
