【是什么 / What it is】
香港强制性公积金计划管理局（MPFA）提议将MPF供款的最高入息水平从每月30,000港元上调至40,000港元（增幅33%），同时最低入息门槛从7,100港元提高至10,000港元。这是该制度13年来首次调整供款入息上下限，旨在反映薪资增长并符合法定要求。

Hong Kong's Mandatory Provident Fund Schemes Authority (MPFA) has proposed raising the maximum income level for MPF contributions from HK$30,000 to HK$40,000 per month (a 33% increase), while also increasing the minimum threshold from HK$7,100 to HK$10,000. This marks the first adjustment to the contribution income thresholds in 13 years, aiming to reflect rising salaries and meet statutory requirements.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
该提议引发商界与劳工界的分歧：商界担心经济复苏期间增加雇主供款负担会加剧企业经营压力、影响就业市场；而劳工界则认为调整幅度仍不足，要求更大幅度提高上限以保障雇员退休权益。这反映了香港在平衡社会保障完善与经济竞争力之间的政策张力。

The proposal has sparked divergence between business and labour sectors: the business community fears increased employer contribution burdens during economic recovery could worsen operating pressures and hurt the jobs market, while labour representatives argue the adjustments are insufficient and demand bolder increases to protect employees' retirement benefits. This reflects Hong Kong's policy tension between enhancing social security and maintaining economic competitiveness.
