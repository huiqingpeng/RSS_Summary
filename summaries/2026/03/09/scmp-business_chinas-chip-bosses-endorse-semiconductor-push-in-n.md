【是什么 / What it is】

中国芯片行业领袖公开支持"十五五"规划（2026-2030年）草案，该规划将集成电路产业列为国家科技战略的核心支柱，特别强调成熟制程优化、先进工艺提升、关键设备材料自主化，以及宽禁带半导体（碳化硅、氮化镓）和高性能AI芯片的发展。

China's semiconductor industry leaders have publicly endorsed the draft 15th Five-Year Plan (2026-2030), which designates integrated circuits as a cornerstone of national technology strategy—emphasizing mature-node refinement, advanced process capabilities, localization of critical equipment and materials, and development of wide-bandgap semiconductors (SiC, GaN) and high-performance AI chips.

Amid deepening US-China tech decoupling, this blueprint signals Beijing's systematic push to overcome "chokepoint" technologies and build a self-controlled semiconductor supply chain; the endorsement from leading firms like Cambricon and Tongfu Microelectronics reflects deep policy-market alignment, potentially accelerating restructuring of global semiconductor industry dynamics.
