【是什么 / What it is】
中国半导体行业的学者和企业家正在呼吁政府加强对AI芯片和关键材料的国家支持，以加速核心技术突破。他们建议通过调控AI算力价格、建立统一交易市场，以及利用稀土等战略原材料优势来实现科技自立自强。

Scholars and entrepreneurs in China's semiconductor industry are calling for stronger state backing in AI chips and critical materials to accelerate core technology breakthroughs. They propose regulating AI computing power prices, establishing a unified trading market, and leveraging strategic raw materials like rare earth elements to achieve technological self-reliance.

This appeal reflects the urgency China faces amid international tech blockades on advanced chips and the "involutionary" low-price competition plaguing the domestic AI computing market. If implemented, such policies could reshape China's AI infrastructure landscape and impact global semiconductor supply chain dynamics.
