【是什么 / What it is】
这是一篇关于香港大屿山南部贝澳湿地在2024年9月石油泄漏事件后，环保组织绿色和平发布的研究报告。研究显示该保护区内的土壤和水样中总石油烃（TRH）含量比未受影响区域高出40倍，而香港环境保护署被指未调查泄漏源头，仅协调清理工作。

This is a report about a study released by Greenpeace regarding petroleum contamination in Pui O wetland on southern Lantau Island, Hong Kong, following an oil spill in September 2024. The study found total petroleum hydrocarbon (TRH) levels up to 40 times higher than unaffected areas in the protected wetland, while Hong Kong's Environmental Protection Department was accused of only coordinating cleanup without investigating the source.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这一事件暴露了香港环境监管体系的重大漏洞——保护区内的高浓度石油污染可能对当地生态造成长期损害，但主管部门却"大事化小"。同时，这也揭示了现有生物多样性保护计划的局限性，即高生态价值的未受保护区域被排除在优先保护项目之外，导致栖息地退化和破碎化持续威胁大屿山的珍贵自然生态。

This incident exposes critical gaps in Hong Kong's environmental regulatory system—high petroleum concentrations in protected areas may cause long-term ecological damage, yet authorities "swept the matter under the carpet." It also reveals limitations in existing biodiversity conservation plans, where unprotected sites with high ecological value are excluded from priority projects, allowing habitat degradation and fragmentation to threaten Lantau Island's precious natural ecology.
