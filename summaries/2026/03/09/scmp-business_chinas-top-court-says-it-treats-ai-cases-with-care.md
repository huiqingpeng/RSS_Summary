【是什么 / What it is】

中国最高法院在工作报告中表示，法院在审理人工智能相关案件时采取了审慎态度，既保护技术创新空间，又明确对利用AI侵权或扰乱社会秩序的行为进行法律规制。同时，最高检察院披露去年起诉了4,739名涉及人工智能、电商等领域数据安全违规的嫌疑人。

China's Supreme People's Court stated in its work report that courts have adopted a cautious approach in adjudicating AI-related cases, protecting room for technological innovation while clarifying legal regulation against acts that exploit AI to infringe upon rights or disrupt social order. Separately, the Supreme People's Procuratorate disclosed that 4,739 individuals were prosecuted last year for data security breaches in fields including AI and e-commerce.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

这一表态反映了中国在AI治理上的"平衡术"——既要成为全球AI创新领导者，又要防范技术滥用风险，这种司法导向将为AI企业提供相对确定的合规预期。同时，数据安全案件的高起诉量显示出监管执法力度正在加强，企业面临的合规压力显著提升。

This statement reflects China's "balancing act" in AI governance—aiming to be a global AI innovation leader while guarding against technology misuse, with this judicial orientation providing relatively predictable compliance expectations for AI enterprises. Meanwhile, the high prosecution volume for data security cases demonstrates intensifying regulatory enforcement, significantly increasing compliance pressure on companies.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】

AI企业可参考"尽到合理注意义务且无实际损害"的裁判标准，在产品设计阶段嵌入合规审查机制，建立AI输出内容的错误监测与纠正流程。同时应加强数据安全管理，特别是在训练数据采集、用户数据存储等环节建立全生命周期合规体系，避免触碰数据安全刑事红线。

AI enterprises can reference the judicial standard of "exercising due diligence without causing actual harm" to embed compliance review mechanisms at the product design stage, establishing error monitoring and correction processes for AI outputs. They should also strengthen data security management, particularly building full-lifecycle compliance systems for training data collection and user data storage, to avoid crossing criminal red lines for data security.
