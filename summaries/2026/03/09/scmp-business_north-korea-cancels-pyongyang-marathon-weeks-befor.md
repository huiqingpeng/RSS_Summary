【是什么 / What it is】
朝鲜在赛事开始前数周突然取消原定于4月5日举行的2026年平壤国际马拉松，未说明具体原因。该赛事本应是平壤自新冠疫情后恢复举办该活动的第二年，由朝鲜田径协会通过专营朝鲜旅游的高丽旅行社（Koryo Tours）通知取消。

North Korea abruptly cancelled the 2026 Pyongyang International Marathon just weeks before its scheduled date of April 5, without providing specific reasons. The race would have marked the second year since Pyongyang resumed the event after the pandemic, and the cancellation was communicated by the DPR Korea Athletics Association via Koryo Tours, a Beijing-based company specialising in North Korea tours.
