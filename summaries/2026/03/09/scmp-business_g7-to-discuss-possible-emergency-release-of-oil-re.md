【是什么 / What it is】
七国集团（G7）正在考虑协调释放战略石油储备，以应对中东战争升级及伊朗封锁霍尔木兹海峡可能引发的全球能源供应危机。法国总统马克龙作为轮值主席国领导人，于周一宣布了这一计划，同时G7财长也将召开紧急视频会议评估海湾地区的经济形势。
The G7 is considering a coordinated release of strategic oil reserves to address a potential global energy supply crisis caused by escalating war in the Middle East and Iran's blockade of the Strait of Hormuz. French President Emmanuel Macron, whose country holds the rotating presidency, announced the plan on Monday, while G7 finance ministers were also set to hold an emergency video meeting to assess the economic situation in the Gulf region.

The Strait of Hormuz is a critical chokepoint for global energy transportation, and any blockade would severely impact international oil prices and global economic stability. The G7's move signals that major economies are shifting from observation to active intervention in energy markets, reflecting that geopolitical risks have escalated into systemic economic threats, and may also foreshadow strengthened international energy security cooperation mechanisms.
