【是什么 / What it is】
这是一篇关于2025年底华南美国商会（AmCham South China）对400多家企业调查报告的分析文章。报告指出中美贸易关系可能进入"战术性休战"状态，经济脱钩速度放缓但不会停止，双边贸易将更多集中于非敏感领域。

This is an analysis of a survey conducted by the American Chamber of Commerce in South China (AmCham South China) in late 2025, covering over 400 companies. The report suggests China-US trade relations may enter a "tactical truce" phase, with economic decoupling slowing but not stopping, and bilateral trade increasingly focused on non-sensitive sectors.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
企业信心出现回升迹象——39%的受访企业对中美关系前景持积极态度，较2024年上升14个百分点，反映出市场对双边关系稳定的期待。同时，这一"战术性休战"框架表明，尽管地缘政治摩擦持续，但务实经贸合作仍有空间，企业需要重新评估供应链和市场策略。

The uptick in business confidence—39% of respondents expressed a positive outlook, up 14 percentage points from 2024—signals market expectations for stabilized bilateral relations. Meanwhile, this "tactical truce" framework indicates that pragmatic economic cooperation remains viable despite ongoing geopolitical friction, requiring companies to reassess supply chain and market strategies.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
跨国企业可关注非敏感领域的贸易机会，如工业零部件供应和新兴市场合作，同时建立灵活的风险对冲机制。投资者和政策研究者可将此报告作为监测中美关系"温度"的参考指标，并结合中国向新兴市场供应工业部件的转型趋势，调整区域布局策略。

Multinational companies can explore trade opportunities in non-sensitive sectors such as industrial parts supply and emerging market partnerships, while building flexible risk-hedging mechanisms. Investors and policy researchers can use this report as a barometer for China-US relations and adjust regional strategies in line with China's shift toward supplying industrial components to emerging markets.
