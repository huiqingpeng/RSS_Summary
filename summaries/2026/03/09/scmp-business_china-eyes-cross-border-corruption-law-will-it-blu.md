【是什么 / What it is】
中国正计划制定一部跨境反腐败法律，旨在规范中国海外企业的腐败行为，并追捕外逃人员及追回境外非法资产。该立法已于2023年列入全国人大常委会立法规划，并在2024年得到中央委员会第三次全体会议的正式呼吁，但目前尚未公布具体细节和时间表。
China is planning to enact a cross-border anti-corruption law aimed at regulating corrupt practices by Chinese companies operating overseas, as well as pursuing fugitives and recovering illicit assets abroad. The legislation was first included in the NPC Standing Committee's 2023 legislative plan and formally called for by the Communist Party's Central Committee in 2024, though no specific details or timeline have been released.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这部法律标志着中国从被动应对西方"长臂管辖"转向主动构建反腐败国际规则体系，可能削弱美欧在跨境腐败案件中的单边执法优势。同时，这也反映了中国企业全球化进程中合规压力上升，以及北京加强海外资产管控和人员监管的战略意图。
This law signals China's shift from passively responding to Western "long-arm jurisdiction" to proactively building its own international anti-corruption regulatory framework, potentially blunting the unilateral enforcement advantages of the US and EU in cross-corruption cases. It also reflects rising compliance pressures on Chinese companies amid globalization, as well as Beijing's strategic intent to strengthen oversight of overseas assets and personnel.
