【是什么 / What it is】

Quixant 发布了 IQON 3 和 IQON Air 3 两款工业级嵌入式系统，基于 AMD Ryzen Embedded 8000 系列处理器，专为赌场、体育博彩和街机游戏设备设计。两款产品均支持四路独立 4K 显示，配备游戏专用硬件如 4MB 无电池 MRAM 状态存储、11 路物理入侵检测、专用 LED 控制器及赌场标准串行接口（ccTalk、SAS 等）。

Quixant has released the IQON 3 and IQON Air 3, two industrial embedded systems powered by AMD Ryzen Embedded 8000 series processors, specifically designed for casino, sports betting, and arcade gaming machines. Both systems support four independent 4K displays and feature gaming-specific hardware including 4MB battery-free MRAM for state storage, eleven physical intrusion-detection monitors, dedicated LED controllers, and casino-standard serial interfaces (ccTalk, SAS, etc.).
