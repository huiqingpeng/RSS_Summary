【是什么 / What it is】

LooperRobotics Insight 9 是一款专为具身智能、四足机器人和动态移动平台设计的独立空间 AI 相机，搭载 D-Robotics RDK X5 八核处理器和 10 TOPS AI 加速器，可在设备端独立完成视觉 SLAM 和深度映射，无需依赖主机 PC。相比 Intel RealSense D435i 或 Luxonis OAK-D 等典型 USB 深度相机，它采用"三眼感知矩阵"设计，集成 8.4MP Sony Starvis IMX415 RGB 传感器和两个全局快门立体深度传感器，并预装 ROS 2 Humble 环境。

The LooperRobotics Insight 9 is a standalone spatial AI camera designed for embodied intelligence, quadruped robots, and dynamic mobile platforms. It features a D-Robotics RDK X5 octa-core processor with 10 TOPS AI accelerator, enabling on-device Visual SLAM and depth mapping without relying on a host PC. Unlike typical USB depth cameras like Intel RealSense D435i or Luxonis OAK-D, it adopts a "Tri-Eye Perception Matrix" with an 8.4MP Sony Starvis IMX415 RGB sensor and two global shutter stereo depth sensors, with ROS 2 Humble pre-installed.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

这款相机代表了边缘 AI 计算在机器人感知领域的重大进步，将原本需要高性能 PC 的 V-SLAM 和深度处理任务下放到功耗仅 5W 的嵌入式设备上，显著降低了机器人系统的复杂度和成本。其 188° 超广角、0.01 Lux 低光灵敏度、-20°C 至 85°C 宽温工作范围以及 24g 高-G 跟踪能力，使其特别适合高振动、低光照等严苛环境下的 legged robotics 应用。与竞品相比，它在 10 米范围内保持低于 8% 的误差，明显优于 RealSense D435 和 D455 的远距离漂移问题。

This camera represents a significant advancement in edge AI computing for robotic perception, offloading V-SLAM and depth processing tasks from high-performance PCs to a 5W embedded device, dramatically reducing system complexity and cost. Its 188° ultra-wide FOV, 0.01 Lux low-light sensitivity, -20°C to 85°C operating range, and 24g high-G tracking make it particularly suitable for demanding legged robotics applications with high vibration and poor lighting. Compared to competitors, it maintains under 8% error at 10 meters, significantly outperforming RealSense D435 and D455's long-range drift issues.
