【是什么 / What it is】
DFRobot RainbowLink-V2 是一款紧凑型 USB 转串口转换器，集成四个独立通道（隔离 RS-485、隔离 RS-232 及两个 TTL 接口），同时可从 USB 5V 输入转换输出 12V/800mA、5V/2A 和 3.3V/200mA 三种电压，无需额外电源适配器。
The DFRobot RainbowLink-V2 is a compact USB-to-serial converter featuring four independent channels (isolated RS-485, isolated RS-232, and two TTL interfaces), while also converting USB 5V input to 12V/800mA, 5V/2A, and 3.3V/200mA outputs without requiring an external power adapter.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
该设备将多种串口协议与多电压供电集成于单一紧凑模块，大幅降低工业调试、物联网开发和嵌入式系统原型设计的设备复杂度与成本；其隔离设计还能有效保护主机免受工业环境中的电气干扰和接地回路损害。
This device integrates multiple serial protocols and multi-voltage power supply into a single compact module, significantly reducing equipment complexity and cost for industrial debugging, IoT development, and embedded system prototyping; its isolated design also effectively protects the host from electrical interference and ground loop damage in industrial environments.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
可用于同时调试多个 UART 设备（如 GPS 模块）、驱动需要 12V 供电的 RS-485 传感器（如土壤传感器）、或控制工业 PLC；也适合作为 Raspberry Pi 等开发板的扩展串口与电源模块，在实验室或现场快速搭建原型系统。
It can be used to simultaneously debug multiple UART devices (such as GPS modules), drive 12V-powered RS-485 sensors (like soil sensors), or control industrial PLCs; it also serves as an extended serial port and power module for development boards like Raspberry Pi, enabling rapid prototyping in labs or field deployments.
