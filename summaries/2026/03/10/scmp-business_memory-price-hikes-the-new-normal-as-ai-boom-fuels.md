【是什么 / What it is】
本文报道了希捷科技高管的观点，指出人工智能热潮正在推动存储行业进入一个"超级周期"，内存价格上涨可能成为未来几年的新常态。与传统存储行业的供需周期不同，AI数据中心对DRAM等内存芯片的需求激增正在重塑市场格局。

This article reports on an executive from Seagate Technology's view that the AI boom is pushing the storage industry into a "supercycle," with memory price hikes likely becoming the new normal for the coming years. Unlike traditional supply-demand cycles in the storage industry, surging demand from AI data centers for memory chips like DRAM is reshaping the market landscape.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
AI工作负载的爆炸式增长正在迫使存储行业在单盘片上压缩更多数据，以满足云巨头的需求，这标志着基础设施层面的结构性转变。服务器DRAM价格在2026年第一季度预计环比上涨约90%，创下历史最大涨幅，这将直接影响云计算成本和企业IT预算，并可能传导至终端服务和产品定价。

The explosive growth of AI workloads is forcing the storage industry to pack more data onto disks to meet demand from cloud giants, marking a structural shift at the infrastructure level. With server DRAM prices expected to surge approximately 90% quarter-over-quarter in Q1 2026—the steepest increase on record—this will directly impact cloud computing costs and enterprise IT budgets, potentially passing through to end-user service and product pricing.
