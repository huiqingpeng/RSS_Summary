【是什么 / What it is】
本文报道了美国和以色列在对伊朗军事行动中大规模使用人工智能（AI）武器系统的最新情况，包括美军使用Anthropic的Claude模型协助打击约1000个目标，以及以色列使用"Lavender"AI系统识别潜在攻击目标。这些系统通过分析卫星图像、优化目标选择和缩短"杀伤链"时间来提升作战效率。

This article reports on the large-scale deployment of AI weapon systems by the US and Israel in military operations against Iran, including the US military's use of Anthropic's Claude model to assist in striking approximately 1,000 targets, and Israel's "Lavender" AI system for identifying potential attack targets. These systems enhance operational efficiency by analyzing satellite imagery, optimizing target selection, and compressing the "kill chain" timeline.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
AI军事应用的加速暴露了严重的问责漏洞和人道主义风险：算法错误可能导致灾难性平民伤亡（如疑似AI误判导致的伊朗学校轰炸事件），而缺乏具有约束力的国际监管协议使风险持续升级。这标志着战争形态正从"人类决策"向"算法辅助甚至替代决策"转变，引发对自主武器系统伦理边界的紧迫追问。

The accelerated military adoption of AI reveals critical accountability gaps and humanitarian risks: algorithmic errors can lead to catastrophic civilian casualties (such as the suspected AI-misidentified bombing of an Iranian school), while the absence of binding international regulatory agreements continues to escalate these dangers. This marks a transformation in warfare from "human decision-making" to "algorithm-assisted or even substituted decision-making," raising urgent questions about the ethical boundaries of autonomous weapons systems.
