【是什么 / What it is】

China's Ministry of Commerce summoned global shipping giants Maersk and MSC on Tuesday for talks on "shipping operations" without providing specific reasons. This follows the Panamanian government's seizure of two ports from Hong Kong-based CK Hutchison, which has since launched legal proceedings seeking at least US$2 billion in damages.
