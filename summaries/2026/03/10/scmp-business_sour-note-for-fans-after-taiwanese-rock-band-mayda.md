【是什么 / What it is】
台湾摇滚乐队五月天原定于3月24日在香港的演唱会因"内部评估"被取消，主办方改为在3月29日加演一场，引发已购票粉丝（尤其是专程从外地赴港的观众）不满并向香港消费者委员会投诉。Taiwanese rock band Mayday cancelled their originally scheduled March 24 concert in Hong Kong due to an "internal assessment," replacing it with an additional show on March 29, which sparked complaints from ticket holders—particularly fans who had travelled from outside the city—to Hong Kong's Consumer Council.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
此事件凸显了大型演艺活动取消/改期时消费者权益保护的漏洞，以及跨境追星粉丝面临的时间与经济损失风险；同时反映出演唱会主办方在危机沟通与补偿机制上的不足，可能损害艺人品牌形象。This incident highlights gaps in consumer protection for large-scale entertainment events and the financial risks faced by cross-border fans; it also reflects organisers' shortcomings in crisis communication and compensation mechanisms, potentially damaging the artist's brand reputation.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
作为活动参与者，购票前应仔细阅读退款/改期条款，优先选择可退改的渠道；作为活动主办方，应建立完善的应急预案与粉丝沟通机制，必要时提供交通住宿补偿方案。As an attendee, read refund/rescheduling terms carefully before purchasing and prioritise flexible booking channels; as an event organiser, establish robust contingency plans and fan communication systems, with compensation options for travel and accommodation when necessary.
