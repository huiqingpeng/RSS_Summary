【是什么 / What it is】

NXP i.MX 93W 是恩智浦首款集成无线功能的微处理器系统级封装（SiP），将双核 Arm Cortex-A55 应用处理器（i.MX 93）与 iW610 三模无线芯片（WiFi 6、蓝牙 LE、802.15.4）整合在单一 14.2×12 mm 封装内，并内置所有射频外围元件。该芯片于 Embedded World 2026 发布，预计 2027 年初上市。

The NXP i.MX 93W is the company's first integrated wireless MPU System-in-Package (SiP), combining a dual-core Arm Cortex-A55 application processor (i.MX 93) with an iW610 tri-radio chip (WiFi 6, Bluetooth LE, 802.15.4) in a single 14.2×12 mm package that includes all RF external components. Unveiled at Embedded World 2026, it is expected to be available in early 2027.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

高度集成化设计可替代 PCB 上多达 60 颗分立元件，显著缩减板级面积、简化硬件设计与认证流程，加速产品上市周期。同时支持 Matter over Wi-Fi/Thread 等新兴物联网标准，并内置 Arm Ethos-U65 NPU 实现边缘 AI 推理，契合智能家居、医疗传感、能源设备等低功耗边缘计算场景的技术演进趋势。

The highly integrated design replaces up to 60 discrete PCB components, significantly reducing board area, simplifying hardware design and regulatory certification, and accelerating time-to-market. It also supports emerging IoT standards like Matter over Wi-Fi/Thread, with an integrated Arm Ethos-U65 NPU for edge AI inference—aligning with the technology evolution toward low-power edge computing in smart home, medical sensing, and energy grid applications.
