【是什么 / What it is】
这篇文章介绍了中国未来五年的基础设施规划，重点是在新疆和西藏地区建设两条横贯全部九个陆地边境省份的高速公路，同时推进东部沿海国家高速公路建设，并将三条通往西藏的公路升级。这些项目包括1970年代为提升1962年中印边境战争后军事机动性而建的战略公路的平行路线，以及2024年9月开工、预计2032年完工的独山子-库车高速公路。

This article outlines China's infrastructure plans for the next five years, focusing on constructing two highways spanning all nine land-border provinces in Xinjiang and Tibet regions, advancing the National Coastal Highway along the east coast, and upgrading three highways into Tibet. These projects include routes parallel to a strategic road built in the 1970s for military mobility following the 1962 Sino-India border war, and the Dushanzi-Kuqa Highway which began construction in September 2024 with expected completion in 2032.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这些公路建设具有双重战略意义：一方面大幅缩短天山山脉穿越时间、实现全年通行，强化对边疆地区的控制与经济发展；另一方面平行于既有军事战略公路的新线路，显著提升中国在敏感边境地带的军事投送能力和快速反应能力。这反映了中国通过基础设施"硬实力"巩固领土主权、应对地缘政治紧张局势的长期战略。

These highway constructions carry dual strategic significance: they dramatically reduce travel time across the Tianshan Mountains and enable year-round access, strengthening control and economic development in frontier regions; simultaneously, new routes parallel to existing military strategic roads significantly enhance China's military projection and rapid response capabilities in sensitive border areas. This reflects China's long-term strategy of consolidating territorial sovereignty and addressing geopolitical tensions through infrastructure "hard power."
