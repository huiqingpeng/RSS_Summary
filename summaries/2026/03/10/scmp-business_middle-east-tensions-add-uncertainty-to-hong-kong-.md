【是什么 / What it is】
本文报道了香港主要商业地产开发商九龙仓置业（Wharf REIC）对2026年香港零售租金前景的预警。该公司指出，尽管旗下海港城和时代广场的客流量有所回升，但消费转化疲软，同时中东地缘政治紧张局势可能加剧经济不确定性。

This article reports Wharf REIC's warning about Hong Kong's retail rent outlook for 2026. The major commercial property developer notes that while visitor traffic at its flagship malls Harbour City and Times Square has improved, spending conversion remains weak, and rising Middle East geopolitical tensions may heighten economic uncertainty.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这反映了香港零售业"旺丁不旺财"的结构性困境——人流恢复未能带动实际消费增长，同时外部风险正从俄乌、加沙冲突转向更直接影响香港经济的中东局势。此外，利率下行空间消失甚至反转的可能性，将进一步压制零售物业的估值和租金定价能力。

This reflects Hong Kong retail's structural dilemma of "busy traffic but weak spending"—footfall recovery hasn't translated into actual consumption growth. Meanwhile, external risks are shifting from Ukraine and Gaza to Middle East tensions with more direct impact on Hong Kong's economy. The potential disappearance or reversal of interest rate cuts would further suppress retail property valuations and rent pricing power.
