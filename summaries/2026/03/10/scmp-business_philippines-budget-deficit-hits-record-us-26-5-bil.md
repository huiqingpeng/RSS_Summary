【是什么 / What it is】
菲律宾2025年财政预算赤字创下265亿美元的历史新高，达到1.58万亿比索，主要原因是针对防洪工程腐败丑闻的调查导致政府支出放缓和税收下降。尽管政府加强了公共工程项目的审查，赤字仍超出了1.56万亿比索的法定上限。

The Philippines' 2025 budget deficit hit a record US$26.5 billion (1.58 trillion pesos), driven by a corruption investigation into flood-control projects that slowed government spending and reduced revenue. Despite tighter scrutiny of public works, the deficit still breached the government's 1.56 trillion-peso ceiling.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

This case highlights the unintended consequences of anti-corruption drives: short-term fiscal strain from project suspensions that reduce spending efficiency rather than achieve real savings, plus dampened investor confidence. The deficit at 5.63% of GDP, though slightly improved from 2024, remains above target, illustrating the tension between governance reform and economic stability.
