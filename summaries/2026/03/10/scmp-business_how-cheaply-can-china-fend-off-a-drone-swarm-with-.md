【是什么 / What it is】

中国官方媒体报道，中国已推出一款面向城市警务的低成本微波反无人机集群防御系统。该系统由国有081电子集团研制，将雷达、光电和无线电探测系统与大型平板天线集成于八轮卡车平台，可通过干扰实现"软杀伤"或利用高功率微波（HPM）瞬间过热熔化无人机内部芯片和金属电路实现"硬杀伤"。

Chinese state media reported that China has launched a low-cost urban police microwave anti-drone swarm defence system. Developed by state-owned 081 Electronics Group, it integrates radar, electro-optical and radio detection systems with a large flat-panel antenna mounted on an eight-wheel truck, capable of "soft kill" through jamming or "hard kill" using high-power microwaves to instantly overheat and melt drones' internal chips and metal circuitry.
