【是什么 / What it is】
这是一篇关于海地安全部队使用爆炸无人机打击帮派武装的报道。人权观察组织（Human Rights Watch）指出，自2024年3月以来，海地政府及其安全部队在美国私人军事公司Vectus Global的支持下，使用绑有炸药的四旋翼无人机在首都太子港人口密集区开展反帮派行动，已造成超过1200人死亡，其中包括60名平民（43名成人、17名儿童）。

This is a report on Haitian security forces using explosive drones to target criminal gangs. Human Rights Watch stated that since March 2024, the Haitian government and its security forces, supported by US private military firm Vectus Global, have conducted anti-gang operations using quadcopter drones strapped with explosives in densely populated areas of Port-au-Prince, resulting in over 1,200 deaths including 60 civilians (43 adults, 17 children).
