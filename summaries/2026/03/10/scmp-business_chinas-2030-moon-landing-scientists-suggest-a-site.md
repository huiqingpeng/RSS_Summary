【是什么 / What it is】
中国科学家团队在《自然·天文学》期刊发表研究，建议将月球正面低纬度的Rimae Bode区域作为2030年载人登月任务的着陆点。该区域曾被NASA星座计划列为兴趣点，以平坦地形和丰富火山地质样本著称。

A Chinese research team published a study in *Nature Astronomy* proposing the Rimae Bode region on the lunar nearside as the landing site for China's 2030 crewed moon mission. This area, previously flagged by NASA's Constellation program, is known for its flat terrain and rich volcanic geological samples.

---

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这一选址建议凸显了中国登月计划与NASA历史研究的高度重合，反映月球优质着陆点的稀缺性。Rimae Bode的火山熔岩流和沉积物对研究月球火山活动具有独特科学价值，同时平坦地形保障宇航员安全作业。

This site selection highlights the significant overlap between China's lunar ambitions and NASA's historical research, reflecting the scarcity of prime landing locations on the Moon. The volcanic features offer unique scientific value for studying lunar volcanism, while the flat terrain ensures safe astronaut operations.
