【是什么 / What it is】

香港城市大学（CityUHK）开设的工商管理博士（DBA）项目专为资深高管设计，采用"课程+论文"模式，帮助学员将实际工作中的复杂问题转化为可辩护、可分享、可落地的应用型研究。该项目每年严格控制招生规模，确保一对一指导，学制3-6年，每月仅需一个周末集中授课。

The City University of Hong Kong's Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) programme is designed for senior executives, combining coursework with a thesis to transform real workplace problems into defensible, shareable, and practical applied research. With strictly limited annual intake for one-to-one supervision, the programme spans 3-6 years with monthly weekend-only sessions.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

在AI快速生成答案的时代，真正的领导力稀缺的是"深度"而非"广度"——能够经得起审视、解决真实复杂问题的能力。该项目通过严格的博士级训练，让高管从知识消费者转变为知识创造者，这种 rigor 带来的自信无法通过"头衔光学"来伪装，且校友网络形成的信任纽带能转化为长期的商业合作机会。

In an era where AI rapidly generates answers, true leadership lacks "depth" rather than "breadth"—the ability to withstand scrutiny and solve real complex problems. Through rigorous doctoral training, the programme transforms executives from knowledge consumers into knowledge creators; this rigor-built confidence cannot be faked through "title optics," and the trust bonds formed within cohorts convert into lasting business partnerships.
