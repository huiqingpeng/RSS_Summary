【是什么 / What it is】
香港离岛区议员反对六条主要渡轮航线（包括前往长洲、南丫岛、梅窝及坪洲等）票价上调最高12.8%的提案。该提案由运输署提出，预计与公共交通补贴计划改革同步于4月1日生效，届时60岁以上长者将不再享受统一2港元票价优惠。

District councillors representing Hong Kong's outlying islands have opposed a proposal to raise ferry fares by up to 12.8% on six key routes serving Cheung Chau, Lamma Island, Mui Wo and Peng Chau. The plan, presented by the Transport Department, is scheduled to take effect on April 1 alongside an overhaul of the public transport subsidy scheme that will eliminate the flat HK$2 fare for elderly passengers aged 60 or above.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
此举将加重离岛长者出行负担，可能抑制其外出意愿，同时损害依赖游客消费的本地经济。此外，议员担忧燃油价格飙升可能导致未来进一步加价，形成恶性循环，凸显离岛社区在交通成本与可及性之间的深层矛盾。

The fare hikes will impose greater financial burdens on elderly island residents, potentially discouraging travel and harming local economies dependent on tourist spending. Councillors also raised concerns that soaring fuel prices could trigger additional increases, highlighting a systemic tension between transportation costs and accessibility for outlying island communities.
