【是什么 / What it is】
香港红十字会为2024年1月大埔太和邨王福阁大火的灾民发起募捐活动，三个月内筹得5.3亿港元善款，并于3月底正式结束筹款。这场火灾造成168人死亡、近5000人受灾，是该机构近年来处理的最大规模灾难救援项目之一。

The Hong Kong Red Cross raised HK$530 million through a three-month donation drive for victims of the January 2024 Wang Fuk Court fire in Tai Po, which killed 168 people and displaced nearly 5,000 residents. The charity concluded its fundraising campaign in late March, marking one of its largest disaster relief operations in recent years.
