This article describes how maker Eric Strebel replicated hard-to-source clear dust cover parts for IBM Selectric typewriters using resin casting techniques. He employed degassed tin-cure silicone to create a two-part mold, combined with tinted clear epoxy resin cured under heat and pressure to achieve high-fidelity reproductions.
