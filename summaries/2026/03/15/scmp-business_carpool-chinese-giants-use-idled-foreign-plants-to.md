【是什么 / What it is】

Chinese automakers (such as Great Wall Motors, Geely, and Chery) are accelerating overseas expansion through an "asset-light" strategy: utilizing idled foreign plants to produce EVs rather than building new facilities. This approach stems from domestic overcapacity and weak demand, while avoiding geopolitical risks and capital expenditure pressures.
