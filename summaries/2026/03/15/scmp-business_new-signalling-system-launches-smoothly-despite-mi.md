【是什么 / What it is】
香港地铁（MTR）于周日清晨6点正式在荃湾线启用新信号系统，这是港铁老化铁路网络现代化升级的一部分，旨在提升自动化水平和运营可靠性。尽管首日出现列车与月台屏蔽门对位不准等轻微故障，但整体运营基本顺利，约200名额外职员被派驻沿线协助。

Hong Kong's MTR Corporation launched a new signalling system on the Tsuen Wan line at 6am on Sunday, as part of a modernization upgrade for the city's ageing rail network to improve automation and operational reliability. Despite minor glitches such as train-platform screen door misalignment on the first day, overall operations proceeded largely as planned, with approximately 200 additional staff deployed along the line to assist.
