【是什么 / What it is】

This is an exclusive interview with Zhao Tong, senior fellow at Carnegie's Nuclear Policy Programme, analyzing the future trajectory of Iran's nuclear programme and Middle East security architecture after US-Israel military strikes. Despite successful "decapitation" operations, the war risks prolongation, with Iran potentially exhausting US-Israeli forces through a war of attrition.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
美以行动传递的"丛林法则"信号可能刺激更多国家追求核武器，引发亚洲核军备竞赛。同时，中东局势动荡将直接冲击中国在该地区的能源安全和战略利益，重塑大国博弈格局。

The "law of the jungle" signaled by US-Israeli actions could spur more countries to pursue nuclear weapons, triggering an Asian nuclear arms race. Meanwhile, Middle East turmoil will directly impact China's energy security and strategic interests in the region, reshaping great power competition.
