【是什么 / What it is】
巴拿马政府正在敦促中国国有航运巨头中远集团（Cosco）重新考虑其暂停在巴尔博亚港运营的决定。此前，中远集团在当地报纸发布通知，宣布暂停使用该港口，但未说明具体原因，此举正值巴拿马运河地缘政治紧张局势加剧以及香港长江和记实业出售港口资产引发争议的背景下。

The Panamanian government is urging Chinese state-owned shipping giant Cosco to reconsider its decision to suspend operations at the Balboa port. Cosco had published a notice in a local newspaper announcing the suspension without stating reasons, amid heightened geopolitical tensions surrounding the Panama Canal and the controversy over Hong Kong-based CK Hutchison Holdings' port asset sales.
