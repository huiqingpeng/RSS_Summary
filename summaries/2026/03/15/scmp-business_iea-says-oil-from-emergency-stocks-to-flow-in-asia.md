【是什么 / What it is】
国际能源署（IEA）宣布，由于中东战争导致霍尔木兹海峡被封锁，创纪录规模的紧急石油储备将立即向亚洲和大洋洲投放，而欧洲和美洲则需等到3月底才能获得。这是历史上最大规模的石油供应中断事件，IEA已收到成员国的具体实施计划。
The International Energy Agency (IEA) announced that a record release of emergency oil stocks will be made available immediately to Asia and Oceania due to the Strait of Hormuz being blocked by war in the Middle East, while Europe and the Americas must wait until end-March. This follows the biggest supply disruption in history, with implementation plans now received from member states.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
亚洲买家高度依赖中东石油供应，因此储备释放的速度对该地区尤为关键；此举旨在缓解油价突破每桶100美元后的市场紧张情绪，并应对美国周末袭击伊朗主要石油出口设施后可能加剧的地缘政治动荡。这反映了全球能源供应链的脆弱性以及紧急协调机制在危机中的实际作用。
The speed of reserve releases is particularly critical for Asia given its heavy reliance on Middle Eastern oil flows; this move aims to ease market tensions after oil prices surged above $100/barrel and brace for heightened geopolitical turmoil following US strikes on Iran's key oil export facility. It highlights the fragility of global energy supply chains and the practical role of emergency coordination mechanisms during crises.
