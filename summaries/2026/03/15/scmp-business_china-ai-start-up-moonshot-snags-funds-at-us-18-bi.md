【是什么 / What it is】
中国AI初创公司Moonshot AI正在寻求新一轮高达10亿美元的融资，估值达到180亿美元，较三个月前增长超过四倍。该公司是Kimi聊天机器人的开发商，此前已获得阿里巴巴、腾讯等科技巨头的投资支持。
Chinese AI start-up Moonshot AI is seeking to raise up to US$1 billion in a new funding round at a valuation of US$18 billion, more than quadrupling its value in just three months. The company, developer of the Kimi chatbot, has previously secured backing from tech giants including Alibaba and Tencent.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
Moonshot的快速融资反映了中国AI初创企业正加速追赶OpenAI和Anthropic等硅谷领导者的全球竞争态势。同时，竞争对手如智谱和MiniMax的估值已达300-400亿美元，显示中国AI赛道正经历资本热潮，开源智能体OpenClaw的爆发更推动了行业创新加速。
Moonshot's rapid fundraising reflects intensifying global competition as Chinese AI start-ups race to catch up with Silicon Valley leaders like OpenAI and Anthropic. Meanwhile, rivals such as Zhipu and MiniMax have reached US$30-40 billion valuations, signaling a capital frenzy in China's AI sector further fueled by the breakout of open-source agent OpenClaw.
