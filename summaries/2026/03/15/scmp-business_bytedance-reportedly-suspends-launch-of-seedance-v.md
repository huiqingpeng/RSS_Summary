ByteDance has suspended the global launch of its latest video-generation model, Seedance 2.0, due to copyright disputes with Disney and other Hollywood studios. Officially unveiled in February and positioned as a multimodal AI tool for professional film, e-commerce, and advertising, the model had gained industry attention for its generation capabilities and was even dubbed "the DeepSeek of video."
