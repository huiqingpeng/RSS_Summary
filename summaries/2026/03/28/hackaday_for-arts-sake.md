This article explores artistic expressions emerging from hacker/maker culture, particularly focusing on "circuit sculpture" and other forms that combine electronic engineering with aesthetic pursuits. This "technical art" breaks down disciplinary barriers between engineering and art, demonstrating that functionality and beauty can coexist in a single creation, offering a new paradigm for cross-domain innovation.
