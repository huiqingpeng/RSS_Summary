【是什么 / What it is】

阳台太阳能（Balcony Solar）是一种即插即用的小型光伏系统，通常由1-2块太阳能板和微型逆变器组成，可直接安装在公寓阳台或户外区域，无需专业电工安装，通过普通家用插座即可并网发电。这一概念在德国率先流行，正逐步扩展到欧洲其他国家及美国部分州。

Balcony solar is a plug-and-play mini photovoltaic system, typically consisting of 1-2 solar panels and micro-inverters, that can be directly installed on apartment balconies or outdoor areas without professional electricians, feeding power into the grid through standard household sockets. Originating in Germany, this concept is now spreading to other European countries and select US states.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

阳台太阳能打破了传统屋顶太阳能对房产所有权的限制，让租房者和公寓住户首次能够参与能源转型，填补了太阳能普及中的市场空白。其爆发式增长（德国已超100万套）反映出大众对清洁能源的强烈需求，同时也为城市可持续发展开辟了新的分布式能源路径。

Balcony solar breaks the property ownership barrier of traditional rooftop solar, enabling renters and apartment dwellers to participate in the energy transition for the first time, filling a critical market gap. Its explosive growth (over 1 million units in Germany alone) demonstrates strong public appetite for clean energy while opening new distributed energy pathways for urban sustainability.

若身处已开放阳台太阳能的地区（如德国、西班牙、法国、比利时或美国犹他州/弗吉尼亚州），可直接从大型零售商购买成套设备DIY安装，并通过配套APP监控发电情况；若所在地区尚未开放，可关注本地政策动态或向能源监管部门提议简化小型光伏系统的并网审批流程，推动类似"即插即用"框架落地。

If you're in regions where balcony solar is approved (Germany, Spain, France, Belgium, or US states like Utah/Virginia), you can purchase ready-to-install kits from major retailers for DIY setup and monitor generation via companion apps; if not yet available locally, track policy developments or advocate to energy regulators for streamlined grid-connection approvals for small-scale plug-in solar systems.
