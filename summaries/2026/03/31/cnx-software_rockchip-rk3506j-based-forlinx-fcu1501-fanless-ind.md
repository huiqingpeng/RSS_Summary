【是什么 / What it is】

飞凌科技（Forlinx Technology）推出了FCU1501，一款基于瑞芯微RK3506J处理器的无风扇工业级嵌入式计算机和物联网网关。该处理器采用三核Cortex-A7加单核Cortex-M0的异构架构，专为恶劣环境下的高可靠性数据采集和协议转换而设计。

Forlinx Technology has introduced the FCU1501, a fanless industrial embedded computer and IoT gateway powered by the Rockchip RK3506J processor featuring a tri-core Cortex-A7 and single Cortex-M0 heterogeneous architecture. It is designed for high-reliability data acquisition and protocol conversion in harsh industrial environments.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

这款网关以约134美元的价格提供了工业级可靠性（-40°C至+85°C工作温度、3级EMC认证）和丰富的工业接口（多路RS485/RS232/CAN FD），在成本敏感的工业自动化和边缘计算场景中极具竞争力。RK3506J作为瑞芯微面向工业物联网的专用SoC，标志着国产芯片在工业网关领域的成熟应用。

This gateway delivers industrial-grade reliability (-40°C to +85°C operating range, Level 3 EMC certification) and rich industrial interfaces (multiple RS485/RS232/CAN FD channels) at an aggressive ~$134 price point, making it highly competitive for cost-sensitive industrial automation and edge computing scenarios. The RK3506J, as Rockchip's dedicated SoC for industrial IoT, signals the maturation of domestic Chinese chips in the industrial gateway segment.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】

可用于构建工厂自动化中的Modbus TCP/RTU协议转换网关、轨道交通数据采集节点或智能楼宇的边缘计算终端；其Web管理界面和预装Linux 6.1系统降低了部署门槛。开发者可参考其硬件设计评估RK3506J在自有项目中的适用性，或利用其双网口+4G LTE架构实现高可用性的远程设备监控方案。

It can be deployed as a Modbus TCP/RTU protocol conversion gateway in factory automation, a data acquisition node for rail transit, or an edge computing terminal for smart buildings; the web-based management interface and pre-installed Linux 6.1 lower deployment barriers. Developers can evaluate RK3506J's suitability for their own projects based on this hardware design, or leverage its dual Ethernet plus 4G LTE architecture to implement highly available remote equipment monitoring solutions.
