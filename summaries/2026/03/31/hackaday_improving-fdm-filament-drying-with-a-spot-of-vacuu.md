【是什么 / What it is】

This article explores a technical approach to improving FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) filament drying using vacuum technology. While traditional hot-air dryers are inefficient, the author proposes reducing ambient pressure to lower water's boiling point, enabling efficient moisture removal at lower temperatures—even at room temperature.
