【是什么 / What it is】
汇丰银行于3月24日在香港发行25亿美元AT1（额外一级资本）债券，这是自伊朗冲突导致市场冻结以来的首笔AT1债券发行。该债券为永续次级或有可转换证券，分为5年期（6.75%）和10年期（7%）两档，旨在满足监管资本要求。

HSBC launched a US$2.5 billion AT1 (Additional Tier 1) bond issuance in Hong Kong on March 24, marking the first AT1 bond sale since the market froze due to the Iran conflict. The perpetual subordinated contingent convertible securities are split into two tranches—5-year at 6.75% and 10-year at 7%—to meet regulatory capital requirements.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
此次发行标志着AT1债券市场从地缘政治冲击中复苏，香港凭借其金融稳定性成为首选发行地。对于汇丰而言，这不仅强化了资本基础，也反映了国际投资者对亚洲金融中心的持续信心。

This issuance signals the recovery of the AT1 bond market from geopolitical shocks, with Hong Kong's financial stability making it the preferred venue. For HSBC, it not only strengthens its capital base but also reflects international investors' sustained confidence in the Asian financial hub.
