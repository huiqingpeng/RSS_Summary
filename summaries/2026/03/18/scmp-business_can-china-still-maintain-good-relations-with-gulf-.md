This article examines how Beijing's long-standing diplomatic strategy of maintaining friendly relations simultaneously with all six Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) states and Iran is being severely tested by the recent escalation, where Iran struck targets in all GCC members in retaliation for US and Israeli attacks on Iran.
