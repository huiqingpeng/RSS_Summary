【是什么 / What it is】

这篇文章回顾了1990年代末至2000年代初免费拨号上网服务的兴衰历程，重点介绍了NetZero、Juno等公司如何通过广告支持模式为用户提供免费互联网接入，以及这一商业模式如何随着宽带普及而衰落。

This article reviews the rise and fall of free dial-up Internet services from the late 1990s to early 2000s, focusing on how companies like NetZero and Juno provided free Internet access through ad-supported models, and how this business model declined with the proliferation of broadband.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

这一模式开创了"免费+广告"和"免费增值"（freemium）的互联网商业模式先河，为后来Google、Facebook等巨头的免费服务奠定了基础；同时它也揭示了早期互联网基础设施从按分钟计费到扁平化定价的转型，以及广告市场泡沫对初创企业的致命影响。

This model pioneered the "free + ads" and freemium business models that laid groundwork for later giants like Google and Facebook; it also reveals the transition of early Internet infrastructure from per-minute billing to flat-rate pricing, and how advertising market bubbles could fatally impact startups.
