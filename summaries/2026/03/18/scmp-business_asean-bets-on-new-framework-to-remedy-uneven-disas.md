【是什么 / What it is】
东盟正在推进一项名为"ASPECT"（东盟紧急与全面转型战略协议）的新框架，旨在解决该地区灾害响应不均衡的问题。该倡议由2025年轮值主席国菲律宾主导，于3月5日在马尼拉举行的第35届东盟社会文化共同体理事会上提出，作为对现有《东盟灾害管理与应急响应协定》（AADMER）的补充机制。

ASEAN is advancing a new framework called "ASPECT" (Asean Strategic Protocol for Emergency and Comprehensive Transformation) to address uneven disaster response across the region. Proposed by 2025 chair the Philippines at the 35th Asean Socio-Cultural Community Council Meeting in Manila on March 5, this initiative serves as a complementary mechanism to the existing Asean Agreement on Disaster Management and Emergency Response (AADMER).

---

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
ASPECT的核心价值在于推动"预判性行动"，使东盟能够在成员国正式提出请求之前就启动集体响应，从而大幅提升大规模或演变中灾害的应对速度。这标志着东盟从被动救灾向主动防灾的战略转型，强化了"一个东盟，一个响应"的区域团结承诺，尤其对保护脆弱群体具有重要意义。

ASPECT's core value lies in enabling "anticipatory action," allowing ASEAN to initiate collective responses even before formal requests from member states, significantly improving response speed to large-scale or evolving disasters. This marks a strategic shift from reactive relief to proactive disaster prevention, reinforcing the regional solidarity commitment of "One ASEAN, One Response" with particular significance for protecting vulnerable populations.
