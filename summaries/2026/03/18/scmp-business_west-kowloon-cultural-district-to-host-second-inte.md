【是什么 / What it is】

第二届香港国际文化峰会将于2025年3月22日至23日在西九文化区举行，主题为"新时代：通过艺术重新构想社区"，汇聚来自14个国家和地区的约30位博物馆馆长、艺术机构管理者及文化领袖，探讨博物馆、表演艺术机构和文化区的未来发展。

The second Hong Kong International Cultural Summit will take place on March 22-23, 2025, at the West Kowloon Cultural District under the theme "A New Era: Reimagining Community through the Arts," bringing together approximately 30 museum directors, arts administrators, and cultural leaders from 14 countries and regions to discuss the future of museums, performing arts institutions, and cultural districts.

---

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

此次峰会与香港艺术周同期举办，标志着香港持续强化其作为国际文化艺术交流中心的地位，政府已将文化旅游和创意产业列为优先发展领域。峰会聚焦文化机构与社区的关系、公共学习及社区参与，反映了全球文化机构从"展示空间"向"社区枢纽"转型的行业趋势，同时西九文化区2027年表演艺术中心的即将落成也为此增添了战略意义。

Timed with Hong Kong Art Week, the summit underscores the city's ongoing efforts to strengthen its position as an international hub for arts and cultural exchange, with cultural tourism and creative industries identified as government priorities. The focus on community engagement and public learning reflects a global shift from cultural institutions as mere exhibition spaces to community hubs, while the upcoming 2027 opening of the WestK Performing Arts Centre adds strategic significance to this edition.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】

文化从业者可关注峰会线上直播及后续报告，借鉴多学科艺术区运营、博物馆教育创新及表演艺术中心社区转型等议题的实践案例；投资者与政策研究者可将此作为观察亚洲文化基础设施发展趋势的窗口，特别是西九文化区与全球顶级机构的合作模式；普通公众则可利用艺术周期间的相关展览和导览活动，深入体验香港作为国际文化枢纽的多元生态。

Cultural professionals can follow the live-streamed sessions and reports to learn from case studies on multidisciplinary arts district operations, museum education innovation, and performing arts center community transformation. Investors and policy researchers may use this as a window to observe Asia's cultural infrastructure trends, particularly WestK's collaboration models with world-class institutions. The general public can take advantage of exhibitions and guided tours during Art Week to experience Hong Kong's diverse cultural ecosystem as an international hub.
