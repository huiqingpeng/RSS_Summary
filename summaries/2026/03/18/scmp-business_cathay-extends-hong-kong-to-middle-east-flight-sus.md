【是什么 / What it is】
国泰航空宣布将香港往返中东（迪拜和利雅得）的航班停飞期限延长至4月底。该航线自2月28日美以联合袭击伊朗后首次停飞，此后已多次延期。

Cathay Pacific announced the extension of flight suspensions between Hong Kong and the Middle East (Dubai and Riyadh) until the end of April. The route was first suspended on February 28 following a joint US-Israeli attack on Iran and has been extended multiple times since.

This incident reflects the direct impact of geopolitical conflicts on the global aviation industry, where airlines are forced to prioritize safety over commercial interests. It also highlights the vulnerability of Middle East routes as critical hubs connecting Asia and Europe, and the ongoing impact of international uncertainty on cross-border travel and supply chains.
