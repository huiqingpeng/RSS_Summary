【是什么 / What it is】
中国高超音速武器专家、中国科学院院士方岱宁于2月27日因病去世，享年68岁。此前他曾因2022年的一场"视频会议亲吻门"丑闻被免去北京理工大学副校长职务，但仍继续从事科研工作。其讣告照片上周在中文社交媒体流传，引发关于其死因的争议性猜测。

Fang Daining, a key scientist in China's hypersonic weapons programme and member of the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), died of illness on February 27 at age 68. He had previously been removed as vice president of Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT) following a 2022 "kissing scandal" during a video conference, but continued his research work. An image of his obituary notice circulated on Chinese social media last week, sparking controversial speculation about the cause of his death.
