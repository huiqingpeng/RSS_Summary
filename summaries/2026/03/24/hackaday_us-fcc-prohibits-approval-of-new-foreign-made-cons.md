【是什么 / What it is】
美国联邦通信委员会（FCC）将外国制造的消费级路由器列入"覆盖清单"（Covered List），禁止新设备获得销售批准，除非获得明确的条件性豁免。这一决定源于跨机构评估认定此类路由器对美国国家安全构成"不可接受的风险"。

The US Federal Communications Commission (FCC) has added foreign-made consumer routers to its "Covered List," prohibiting approval of new devices for sale unless granted explicit conditional exemption. This decision follows an interagency determination that such routers pose "unacceptable risks" to US national security.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
此举标志着美国通信基础设施"供应链安全"政策的进一步收紧，从华为等特定企业扩展到整个外国制造路由器类别；然而文章质疑其实际效用，指出真正威胁来自软件漏洞（如输入验证缺陷）而非硬件后门，且美国本土制造能力尚属"虚构"状态，政策可能流于形式。

This signals further tightening of US "supply chain security" policy for communications infrastructure, expanding from specific companies like Huawei to entire categories of foreign-made equipment; however, the article questions its practical effectiveness, noting that real threats stem from software vulnerabilities (e.g., input validation flaws) rather than hardware backdoors, and domestic US manufacturing capabilities remain largely "fictional," potentially rendering the policy symbolic.
