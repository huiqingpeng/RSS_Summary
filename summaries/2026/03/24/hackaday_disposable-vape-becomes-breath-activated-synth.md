这篇文章介绍了一个将一次性电子烟（ElfBars BC5000）改造为呼吸激活合成器的硬件黑客项目。制作者Becky Stern拆除了电子烟的外壳，保留了锂电池、USB充电板和低压传感器，并将其与一个基于555定时器的合成器电路结合，通过吸气触发声音，利用光敏电阻控制音高。

This article showcases a hardware hacking project that repurposes a disposable vape (ElfBars BC5000) into a breath-activated synthesizer. Creator Becky Stern gutted the vape, retaining the LiPo battery, USB charging board, and low-pressure sensor, then combined these with a 555 timer-based synthesizer circuit triggered by inhalation, with pitch controlled by photoresistors.
