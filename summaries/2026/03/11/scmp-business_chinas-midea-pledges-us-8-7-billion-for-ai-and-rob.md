【是什么 / What it is】

中国家电巨头美的集团宣布将在未来三年投入600亿元人民币（87亿美元）用于人工智能和机器人技术研发，重点聚焦"具身智能"等前沿领域。这一投资规模与其过去五年的总研发投入相当，标志着传统制造业向自动化转型的加速。Midea Group, a Chinese home appliance giant, announced it will invest 60 billion yuan (US$8.7 billion) over the next three years in AI and robotics R&D, with a focus on "embodied intelligence" and other cutting-edge areas—matching its total spending over the past five years and signaling an accelerated shift from traditional manufacturing to automation.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

此举反映了中国制造业将机器人技术和物理AI视为下一代效率竞争的核心战场，传统家电企业正积极拥抱未来技术以保持竞争力。美的自2017年收购德国库卡机器人公司以来持续加码布局，其六臂人形机器人Miro U已在工厂实现产线换型效率提升30%的实际应用。This move reflects how Chinese manufacturers view robotics and physical AI as the next battleground for efficiency competition, with traditional appliance makers actively embracing futuristic technologies to stay competitive. Since acquiring German robotics giant Kuka in 2017, Midea has steadily expanded its footprint, with its six-armed humanoid robot Miro U already achieving a 30% improvement in production-line changeover efficiency in real factory deployments.
