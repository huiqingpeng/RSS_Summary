这一政策转向反映了华盛顿的普遍共识：美国过去在帮助中国融入全球经济时缺乏足够的保障措施，最终催生了一个强大的战略竞争对手。美国如今对印度采取"交易性方式"，标志着全球化时代无条件经济合作的终结，也预示着大国竞争时代美国对外经济政策的根本性调整——经济关系将越来越从属于地缘政治考量。

This policy shift reflects a widespread consensus in Washington that the US helped integrate China into the global economy without adequate safeguards, ultimately creating a formidable strategic rival. The "transactional approach" toward India signals the end of unconditional economic cooperation in the globalization era and foreshadows a fundamental adjustment in US foreign economic policy for the age of great power competition—where economic ties will increasingly be subordinated to geopolitical considerations.
