【是什么 / What it is】
德国和日本宣布将释放战略石油储备，以应对中东战争导致的原油价格飙升。此前国际能源署（IEA）呼吁成员国释放4亿桶石油储备，日本决定于6月16日率先行动，G7领导人也正讨论美以与伊朗战争带来的广泛经济影响。
Germany and Japan announced they will release strategic oil reserves to address surging crude prices caused by the Middle East war. The IEA had requested member states to release 400 million barrels, with Japan deciding to take the lead by acting as early as June 16, while G7 leaders were set to discuss the widespread economic fallout from the US-Israeli war with Iran.

This move reflects major economies' heightened alertness to energy security and the immediate impact of geopolitical conflicts on global energy markets. Japan's decision to take the lead, given its exceptionally high dependence on Middle Eastern oil, sets a precedent and signals that the G7 may adopt more coordinated collective interventions to stabilize markets.
