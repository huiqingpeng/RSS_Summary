【是什么 / What it is】
这篇文章报道了2025年初中国人工智能人才市场的爆发式增长。据职场社交平台脉脉的数据显示，1-2月AI相关职位招聘量同比增长约12倍，占新经济领域招聘总数的26.23%，远高于医疗健康、可再生能源和半导体等其他新兴产业。

This article reports on the explosive growth of China's AI talent market in early 2025. According to data from professional social networking platform Maimai, AI-related job postings grew approximately 12-fold year-on-year in January-February, accounting for 26.23% of all new-economy job listings—far outpacing other emerging industries such as healthcare, renewable energy, and semiconductors.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这一趋势标志着中国企业正将AI从边缘实验快速转向核心业务整合，反映出国家战略层面对AI产业的高度重视。同时，高性能计算工程师等关键岗位供需比低至0.15（7个岗位竞争1人），揭示了高端AI人才的严重结构性短缺，这可能成为制约行业发展的关键瓶颈。

This trend signals Chinese companies' rapid shift from experimental AI adoption to core business integration, reflecting national strategic prioritization of the AI industry. Meanwhile, critical positions like high-performance computing engineers face severe supply shortages with a demand-supply ratio as low as 0.15 (7 positions competing for 1 candidate), potentially becoming a key bottleneck constraining industry development.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
技术从业者应评估自身技能与高性能计算、算法开发等紧缺岗位的匹配度，考虑通过专项培训或项目经验积累进入这一高需求领域。企业管理者则需重新审视人才战略，通过更具竞争力的薪酬包、远程工作政策或产学研合作等方式争夺有限的顶尖人才。

Technical professionals should assess their skill alignment with shortage areas like high-performance computing and algorithm development, considering specialized training or project experience to enter this high-demand field. Business leaders need to revisit talent strategies, competing for limited top-tier talent through more competitive compensation packages, remote work policies, or industry-academia partnerships.
