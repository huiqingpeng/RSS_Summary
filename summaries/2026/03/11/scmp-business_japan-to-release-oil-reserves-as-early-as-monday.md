【是什么 / What it is】
日本决定最早于周一（6月16日）释放战略石油储备，包括15天的民间储备和1个月的国家储备，以应对国际能源市场紧张局势。此举是在未等待国际能源署（IEA）协调决定的情况下单方面采取的紧急措施。
Japan has decided to release strategic oil reserves as early as Monday (June 16th), including 15 days of private reserves and one month of national reserves, to address tensions in the international energy market. This is a unilateral emergency measure taken without waiting for a coordinated decision from the International Energy Agency (IEA).

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
作为全球第四大经济体和第五大原油进口国，日本95%的石油依赖中东、70%需经霍尔木兹海峡运输，其能源安全对全球经济具有重要影响。此次单方面行动表明主要消费国可能加速脱离多边协调框架，转向更激进的单边能源政策以应对地缘政治风险。
As the world's fourth-largest economy and fifth-biggest crude importer, with 95% of oil from the Middle East and 70% transiting the Strait of Hormuz, Japan's energy security has significant global implications. This unilateral move signals that major consumers may increasingly bypass multilateral coordination in favor of more aggressive unilateral energy policies to address geopolitical risks.
