Hong Kong's Pop Culture Festival will open next month, featuring a newly restored 4K version of Leslie Cheung's 1999 film *The Kid* and the 25th anniversary of the Hong Kong Film Archive. However, attendance is expected to drop from 800,000 to 600,000 due to a shortened programme from three months to two.
