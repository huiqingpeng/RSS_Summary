【是什么 / What it is】
香港赛马会首次成为LIV Golf香港站"M"品牌赛事的官方社区合作伙伴，通过"赛马会社区高尔夫球计划"推动高尔夫运动的普及与长远发展。该计划涵盖校园活动、体验课程、校际比赛，并为弱势群体提供免费门票，同时安排国际球手探访学校及专业教练指导青少年球员。

For the first time, the Hong Kong Jockey Club served as Official Community Partner of the LIV Golf Hong Kong "M" Mark tournament, launching the Jockey Club Community Golf Programme to promote golf participation and long-term development. The programme includes school-based events, experiential courses, inter-school competitions, complimentary tickets for underprivileged communities, school visits by international golfers, and professional coaching for young players of diverse abilities.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这一合作体现了大型体育机构从单纯赞助转向深度社区共建的趋势，将高端赛事资源转化为普惠性社会价值。通过2024/25年度支持八项"M"品牌赛事、惠及逾81,000人的经验，赛马会正助力香港巩固国际体育盛事之都的地位，同时以独特商业模式实现税收、慈善与就业的多元社会贡献。

This partnership reflects a trend where major sports institutions shift from pure sponsorship to deep community engagement, converting premium event resources into inclusive social value. Building on its 2024/25 experience supporting eight "M" Mark events benefiting over 81,000 people, the Club is helping Hong Kong strengthen its status as a premier sports destination while generating tax revenue, charitable support, and employment through its unique integrated business model.

组织者可借鉴"赛事+教育+弱势群体关怀"的三层架构设计社区项目；企业可参考赛马会的整合商业模式，将核心业务收益系统性地转化为社会影响力；个人则可关注此类官方合作伙伴计划，获取免费参与高端赛事的机会或成为志愿者（如CARE@HKJC志愿者团队）深入体验大型活动运营。

Organizers can adopt a three-tier framework of "event + education + underprivileged care" for community programmes; corporations may reference the Club's integrated business model to systematically convert core revenue into social impact; individuals can follow such official partnership programmes to secure complimentary access to premium events or volunteer opportunities (like the CARE@HKJC Volunteer Team) for hands-on major event experience.
