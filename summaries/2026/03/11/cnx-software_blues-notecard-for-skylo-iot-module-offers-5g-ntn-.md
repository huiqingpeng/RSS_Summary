【是什么 / What it is】

Blues Notecard for Skylo 是一款集成式物联网模块，将 5G NTN 卫星、NB-IoT/LTE-M 蜂窝网络、WiFi 和 GNSS 定位功能整合于 42×30mm 的单一硬件中，专为运输物流、能源和商用设备等资产追踪场景设计。该模块采用 Quectel BG95-S5 调制解调器，内置嵌入式 SIM 卡，并配备硬件安全元件，无需额外硬件或卫星订阅合同即可实现全球连接。

The Blues Notecard for Skylo is an integrated IoT module that combines 5G NTN satellite, NB-IoT/LTE-M cellular, WiFi, and GNSS positioning in a single 42×30mm hardware unit, designed for asset tracking applications in transportation/logistics, energy, and commercial equipment. It features a Quectel BG95-S5 modem, embedded SIM, hardware security elements, and enables global connectivity without requiring additional hardware or satellite subscription contracts.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

该模块的核心创新在于智能连接优先级机制——默认 WiFi、其次蜂窝、最后按需计费的卫星通信，这种"连接降级"策略显著降低了卫星数据成本，同时确保近乎 100% 的在线率。随着 3GPP Release 17 标准推动 5G NTN（非地面网络）技术成熟，此类多模融合方案正成为卫星物联网普及的关键推手，打破了传统卫星终端高昂成本和复杂部署的壁垒。

The module's core innovation lies in its intelligent connectivity hierarchy—defaulting to WiFi, falling back to cellular, and finally to pay-as-you-go satellite—this "graceful degradation" strategy significantly reduces satellite data costs while ensuring near-100% uptime. As 3GPP Release 17 drives 5G NTN (Non-Terrestrial Network) technology maturation, such multi-mode converged solutions are becoming critical enablers for satellite IoT mass adoption, breaking the cost and deployment complexity barriers of traditional satellite terminals.

开发者可直接购买 $89 的模块配合 $25 的 Notecarrier-F 开发板快速原型验证，或利用官方提供的云模拟器和浏览器终端零硬件评估平台能力；设计量产产品时，可将其 42×30mm 紧凑尺寸和 I2C/UART/USB 多接口直接嵌入追踪设备，利用内置加速度计实现运动感知节电，并通过 Blues 的 JSON 式 "Note" 数据协议简化云端集成。建议优先评估目标区域的蜂窝覆盖情况，合理配置 500MB/10 年蜂窝流量与 10KB 卫星数据的混合使用策略。

Developers can purchase the $89 module with $25 Notecarrier-F board for rapid prototyping, or use the cloud simulator and browser terminal for zero-hardware platform evaluation; for mass production designs, its compact 42×30mm form factor and I2C/UART/USB interfaces allow direct embedding into tracking devices, leveraging the built-in accelerometer for motion-aware power saving and Blues' JSON-style "Note" data protocol to simplify cloud integration. It's recommended to evaluate cellular coverage in target regions first and strategically configure the hybrid usage of 500MB/10-year cellular data and 10KB satellite allocation.
