A Chinese defence technology company claims its "Jingqi" war monitoring system successfully intercepted B-2 stealth bomber radio signals during the US military strike on Iran in February, reconstructing weeks of American military buildup. The system integrates satellite imagery, aviation trajectory data, and public military records to analyze transport routes, reconnaissance patterns, base deployments, and carrier strike group movements.
