【是什么 / What it is】

Award-winning memory chip expert Shi Guojun has left the United States to join DK Electronic Materials, a top Chinese semiconductor firm, as chief strategic scientist and director of the Future Industry Research Institute. He will lead the company's strategic planning and development in emerging industries, with a particular focus on advancing its semiconductor memory business.
