【是什么 / What it is】

Google 正在推行一项新的 Android 未验证应用安装流程，取代原有的"允许未知来源"开关。用户现在需要深入开发者选项，启用"允许未验证软件包"设置，并在设备重启后等待 24 小时的"安全延迟"才能最终激活该功能。

Google is implementing a new process for installing unverified Android apps, replacing the old "allow unknown sources" toggle. Users must now dig into Developer Options to enable "Allow Unverified Packages" and wait for a 24-hour "security delay" after device restart before the setting can finally be activated.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

这一变化表面上以用户安全为由，实则大幅提高了侧载（sideloading）的门槛，同时强制第三方应用商店和独立开发者遵守与 Play 商店相同的验证规则。这不仅可能扼杀第三方应用生态，还因 20 台设备的免费开发者账户限制而增加了小型开发者的成本。

While publicly framed as a security measure, this change significantly raises the barrier for sideloading and forces third-party app stores and indie developers to comply with the same verification rules as the Play Store. It threatens to cripple alternative app ecosystems and imposes additional costs on small developers due to the 20-device limit on free developer accounts.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】

Android 用户和开发者应提前熟悉新的"高级流程"设置路径，为即将到来的推广做好准备；独立开发者可考虑评估付费开发者账户的投入产出比，或探索 PWA 等替代分发方案。同时，关注社区对 24 小时延迟机制的反馈，以及可能出现的绕过方案或政策调整。

Android users and developers should familiarize themselves with the new "advanced flow" settings path in advance of the wider rollout; indie developers may evaluate the ROI of paid developer accounts or explore alternative distribution methods like PWAs. Also, monitor community feedback on the 24-hour delay mechanism and potential workarounds or policy adjustments that may emerge.
