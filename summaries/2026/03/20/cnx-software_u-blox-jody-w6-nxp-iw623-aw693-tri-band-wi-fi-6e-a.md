【是什么 / What it is】

u-blox JODY-W6 是一系列基于 NXP IW623/AW693 芯片组的无线通信模块，在单一封装中集成三频 Wi-Fi 6E（2.4/5/6 GHz）和蓝牙 5.4（含 LE Audio）。该系列包含七种产品变体，分为专业级（工业应用）和汽车级（AEC-Q104 认证）两大类别，采用 15.6 × 19.8 mm 的 LGA 封装形式。

The u-blox JODY-W6 is a series of wireless communication modules based on NXP's IW623/AW693 chipsets, integrating tri-band Wi-Fi 6E (2.4/5/6 GHz) and Bluetooth 5.4 (including LE Audio) in a single package. The lineup includes seven product variants across professional-grade (industrial applications) and automotive-grade (AEC-Q104 certified) categories, housed in a compact 15.6 × 19.8 mm LGA form factor.
