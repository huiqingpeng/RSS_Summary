【是什么 / What it is】
美国贸易代表办公室（USTR）本周依据《1974年贸易法》第301条款，对包括新加坡在内的16个经济体发起调查，指控其存在结构性制造业产能过剩，并声称新加坡2024年对美贸易顺差达270亿美元。新加坡贸工部随即反驳，指出美国自身数据显示新加坡对美实际为贸易逆差约270亿美元，双方数据存在根本分歧。

The US Trade Representative's Office launched Section 301 investigations into 16 economies including Singapore, alleging structural manufacturing overcapacity and claiming Singapore had a US$27 billion trade surplus with the US in 2024. Singapore's Ministry of Trade and Industry immediately contested this, citing US Bureau of Economic Analysis data showing Singapore actually ran a US$27 billion trade deficit with the US—a fundamental discrepancy between the two sides' figures.
