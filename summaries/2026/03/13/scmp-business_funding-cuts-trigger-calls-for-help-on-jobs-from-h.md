【是什么 / What it is】
香港物理治疗协会向政府发出警告，指出尽管政府持续资助物理治疗师培训，但社会福利机构的资金削减已导致招聘冻结和裁员，预计到2040年本地物理治疗师将出现超过2,000人的过剩。这一现象形成了"培养人才却无处就业"的矛盾局面。
The Hong Kong Physiotherapy Association has warned the government that funding cuts to social welfare organisations have triggered hiring freezes and job losses, despite continued government subsidies for physiotherapy training, creating a projected surplus of over 2,000 practitioners by 2040. This creates a paradox where public investment develops skilled professionals who cannot be deployed due to budget constraints.

This reflects a common policy coordination failure in public services—disconnect between workforce planning and fiscal budgeting—leading to wasted educational resources and brain drain risks. With Hong Kong's aging population driving rising demand for physiotherapy, funding constraints will weaken the primary healthcare system's capacity to address future health challenges.
