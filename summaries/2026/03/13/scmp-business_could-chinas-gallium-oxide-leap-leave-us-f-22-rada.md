【是什么 / What it is】

中国北京邮电大学吴振平团队于2025年2月11日在《Science Advances》发表突破性研究，首次通过实验证实κ-相氧化镓（kappa-gallium oxide）在室温下具有稳定铁电性，可同时实现数据存储和高功率发射功能，将功率器件与存储器合二为一。这一发现标志着第四代半导体材料氧化镓（Ga₂O₃）的关键技术突破，可能使中国在军用雷达等高端电子领域实现跨代领先。

A Chinese team led by Wu Zhenping at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications published a breakthrough study in *Science Advances* on February 11, 2025, experimentally confirming that kappa-gallium oxide exhibits stable ferroelectricity at room temperature—enabling simultaneous data storage and high-power transmission in a single device. This discovery marks a critical advance in fourth-generation semiconductor material gallium oxide (Ga₂O₃), potentially allowing China to leapfrog generations in military radar and other high-end electronics.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

该技术可能使中美军用雷达技术差距扩大至两代：中国现役歼-10、歼-20、歼-35已采用第三代氮化镓（GaN）雷达，而美国F-22仍依赖老旧的砷化镓（GaAs）系统；氧化镓作为第四代半导体，具有更高击穿电压、更低损耗和更小体积优势，结合存算一体特性可大幅压缩雷达等设备的尺寸与功耗。这不仅关乎军事装备代差，更意味着中国在半导体材料原创创新领域从"追赶"转向"领跑"，可能重塑全球高端芯片产业格局。

This technology could widen the Sino-US military radar gap to two generations: China's active J-10, J-20, and J-35 fighters already employ third-generation GaN radars, while the US F-22 still relies on aging GaAs systems; gallium oxide as a fourth-generation semiconductor offers higher breakdown voltage, lower losses, and smaller size, with integrated memory functionality further reducing device footprint and power consumption. Beyond military implications, this signals China's shift from "catch-up" to "leadership" in original semiconductor material innovation, potentially reshaping the global high-end chip industry landscape.
