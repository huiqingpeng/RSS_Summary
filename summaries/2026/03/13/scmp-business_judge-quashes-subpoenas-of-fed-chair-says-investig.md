【是什么 / What it is】

美国联邦法官James Boasberg于周五撤销了美国司法部向美联储发出的传票，裁定这项针对美联储主席杰罗姆·鲍威尔的调查"出于政治动机"。该调查由特朗普任命的美国检察官Jeanine Pirro于1月发起，法官认为大量证据表明传票的真实目的是向鲍威尔施压，要求其服从特朗普总统反复提出的降息要求。

A federal judge, James Boasberg, quashed US Justice Department subpoenas issued to the Federal Reserve on Friday, ruling that the investigation into Fed Chair Jerome Powell was "politically motivated." The probe was launched in January by Trump-appointed US Attorney Jeanine Pirro, but the judge found that abundant evidence suggested the subpoenas' true purpose was to pressure Powell into complying with President Trump's repeated demands for interest rate cuts.
