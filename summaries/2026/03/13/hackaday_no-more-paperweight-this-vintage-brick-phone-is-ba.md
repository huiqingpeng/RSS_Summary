This article describes a project by tech enthusiast Alan Boris who retrofitted a 1990s Motorola DynaTAC 8000 Classic 2 "brick phone" into a modern functional device. By embedding a Raspberry Pi Pico 2, a small LCD screen, and a BM10 mini cellphone module, he brought this obsolete 1G network device back onto modern cellular networks.
