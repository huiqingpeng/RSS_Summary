【是什么 / What it is】
摩根大通（JPMorgan Chase）已签约成为新鸿基地产（SHKP）西九龙"艺方广场"（Artist Square Towers）办公项目的 anchor tenant（主力租户），将租用约25万平方英尺办公空间，租期10年，预计2028年下半年迁入。该项目位于香港西九龙文化区，总楼面面积约70万平方英尺，预计2027年竣工。

JPMorgan Chase has signed on as the anchor tenant for Sun Hung Kai Properties' (SHKP) Artist Square Towers (AST) office development in Hong Kong's West Kowloon district, committing to lease approximately 250,000 sq ft for 10 years with a planned move in the second half of 2028. The 700,000 sq ft mixed-use project, located in Hong Kong's main cultural hub, is targeted for completion in 2027.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这笔交易标志着国际金融机构对香港作为国际金融中心地位的持续信心，同时印证了西九龙作为"中环2.0"（Central 2.0）的崛起——从传统文化区转型为新兴商业枢纽。此举也可能引发其他跨国企业的跟随效应，加速香港商业格局从单一核心（中环）向多中心发展的演变。

This deal signals continued international confidence in Hong Kong as a premier financial centre while validating West Kowloon's emergence as "Central 2.0"—transforming from a cultural district into a new commercial hub. It may also trigger a follow-the-leader effect among other multinationals, accelerating Hong Kong's evolution from a single-core business district (Central) to a multi-centre commercial landscape.
