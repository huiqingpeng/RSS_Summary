【是什么 / What it is】

本文报道了香港最具影响力的政府建筑师之一、公营房屋先驱廖本怀（Donald Liao Poon-huai）于96岁逝世的消息。他是香港首位房屋署署长（1973-1980），也是首位在英治时期担任政务司的华人官员（1985-1989），主导设计了马头围、华富等早期标志性公共屋邨，并开创了居者有其屋计划。

This article reports the death of Donald Liao Poon-huai, one of Hong Kong's most influential government architects and public housing visionaries, at age 96. He served as Hong Kong's first director of housing (1973-1980) and became the first Chinese official under British rule to serve as secretary for district administration (1985-1989), leading the design of landmark early housing estates including Ma Tau Wai and Wah Fu, and pioneering the Home Ownership Scheme.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

廖本怀的职业生涯横跨香港公共房屋发展的关键时期，其设计理念和政策创新深刻影响了数百万香港市民的居住条件，塑造了现代香港的城市面貌。作为首位担任高级政务职位的华人，他也象征着殖民地时期香港公务员体系中华人地位的提升，具有重要的历史和社会意义。

Liao's career spanned a critical period in Hong Kong's public housing development, and his design philosophy and policy innovations profoundly affected living conditions for millions of Hong Kong residents, shaping the modern urban landscape of the city. As the first Chinese person to hold senior administrative positions, he also symbolized the rising status of Chinese officials in Hong Kong's colonial civil service, carrying significant historical and social meaning.
