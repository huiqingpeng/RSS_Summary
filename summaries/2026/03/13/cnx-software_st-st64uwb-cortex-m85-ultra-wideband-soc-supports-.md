【是什么 / What it is】

意法半导体（STMicroelectronics）发布了 ST64UWB 系列 SoC，这是一款基于 Arm Cortex-M85 的超宽带（UWB）系统级芯片，采用 18nm FD-SOI 工艺制造，同时支持现行的 IEEE 802.15.4z 和即将推出的 IEEE 802.15.4ab UWB 标准。该系列包含三款产品：面向汽车入门的 A100、高端汽车的 A500（带雷达感知功能）以及面向工业/智能家居的 C100。

STMicroelectronics has released the ST64UWB series SoC, an Arm Cortex-M85-based ultra-wideband (UWB) system-on-chip manufactured on an 18nm FD-SOI process, supporting both current IEEE 802.15.4z and upcoming IEEE 802.15.4ab UWB standards. The family includes three variants: the A100 for automotive entry, the A500 for premium automotive (with radar sensing), and the C100 for industrial/smart home applications.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

UWB 技术正成为数字车钥匙、无感门禁和精准定位的核心基础设施，而 IEEE 802.15.4ab 新标准的引入将链路预算提升高达 18dB，使安全测距距离延长约 8 倍，同时 Kaiser 脉冲形状和 1.3GHz 带宽可将空间精度提升约两倍。这预示着 UWB 将从"精准定位"向"长距离雷达感知"演进，拓展儿童存在检测、脚踢传感器等汽车安全场景。

UWB technology is becoming core infrastructure for digital car keys, hands-free access, and precise positioning. The new IEEE 802.15.4ab standard increases link budget by up to 18 dB, extending secure ranging distance by roughly 8×, while Kaiser pulse shaping and 1.3 GHz bandwidth deliver approximately double the spatial accuracy. This signals UWB's evolution from "precise positioning" toward "long-range radar sensing," expanding into automotive safety scenarios like child presence detection and kick sensors.

It is recommended to monitor ecosystem developments from CCC/ICCE/Aliro alliances and proactively architect multi-mode secure authentication systems combining UWB with Bluetooth/NFC.
