【是什么 / What it is】
中国各地苹果Mac Mini电脑因"OpenClaw"（一款自主AI工具）热潮而售罄，经销商纷纷加价销售。这款配备16GB内存和256GB存储的基础型号原本售价4,499元，现因供不应求等待时间已延长至至少一个月。

Apple's Mac Mini computers are selling out across China amid a surge in demand driven by "OpenClaw," an autonomous AI tool. The basic model with 16GB memory and 256GB storage, originally priced at 4,499 yuan, now has wait times of at least a month at official Apple Stores while third-party dealers mark up prices by hundreds of yuan.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这反映了AI工具正在从云端向本地设备迁移的趋势，用户对数据隐私和安全的重视程度显著提升。同时，性价比高的专用设备成为AI普及的关键基础设施，可能重塑个人电脑市场的消费逻辑。

This reflects a growing trend of AI tools migrating from cloud to local devices, with users increasingly prioritizing data privacy and security. Affordable dedicated hardware is becoming critical infrastructure for AI adoption, potentially reshaping consumer logic in the personal computer market.
