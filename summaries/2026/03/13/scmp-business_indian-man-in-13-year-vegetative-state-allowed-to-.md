【是什么 / What it is】
印度最高法院于2025年裁定，允许处于植物人状态13年的工程学生Harish Rana停止生命维持治疗，这是印度宪法中"有尊严地死亡权"原则迄今最明确的实际应用案例之一。Rana自2012年从宿舍四楼坠落后四肢瘫痪，完全卧床并依赖饲管生存。

The Indian Supreme Court ruled in 2025 to allow Harish Rana, an engineering student in a vegetative state for 13 years, to have life support withdrawn—marking one of the clearest real-world applications of the constitutional "right to die with dignity" in India. Rana became quadriplegic after falling from his hostel building in 2012 and had been bedridden with feeding tube dependency ever since.

This case establishes a clear judicial stance in India on weighing quality of life against mere prolongation of existence, providing important precedent for similar long-term vegetative state cases. It also reflects the ongoing global societal debate on the complex balance between medical ethics, patient autonomy, and the burden of family caregiving.
