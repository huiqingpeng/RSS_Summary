【是什么 / What it is】
SparkFun 推出了 Thing Plus – ESP32-C5 开发板，这是首批基于 ESP32-C5 芯片的 Adafruit Feather 兼容硬件之一。该板搭载双频 WiFi 6、蓝牙 5.0 LE 和 802.15.4 无线协议（支持 Zigbee/Thread/Matter），并配备 LiPo 电池管理与 Qwiic 扩展接口。

SparkFun has launched the Thing Plus – ESP32-C5 board, one of the first Adafruit Feather-compatible hardware based on the ESP32-C5 chip. It features dual-band WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.0 LE, and 802.15.4 radio (Zigbee/Thread/Matter support), along with LiPo battery management and Qwiic expansion connector.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
ESP-IDF v6.0 的发布标志着 ESP32-C5 从"预览版"进入"稳定版"，新增 Safe Bootloader OTA 等关键功能，使远程部署和 Matter 智能家居应用变得可靠。这标志着 RISC-V 架构在 IoT 领域的成熟，双频 WiFi 6 与多协议无线融合为边缘设备提供了更高带宽和更低功耗的连接方案。

The release of ESP-IDF v6.0 marks ESP32-C5's transition from "preview" to "stable" with critical features like Safe Bootloader OTA, enabling reliable remote deployments and Matter smart home applications. This signals the maturation of RISC-V architecture in IoT, where dual-band WiFi 6 combined with multi-protocol wireless offers higher bandwidth and lower power connectivity for edge devices.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
可用于构建 Matter 兼容的智能家居中枢或传感器节点，利用双频 WiFi 6 实现低延迟无线传输；借助预装的 MicroPython 或 Arduino IDE 快速原型开发，并通过 Qwiic 生态系统扩展传感器。对于电池供电项目，可利用内置的 MAX17048 电量计实现精确的电源管理。

Ideal for building Matter-compatible smart home hubs or sensor nodes leveraging dual-band WiFi 6 for low-latency wireless; rapid prototyping via pre-installed MicroPython or Arduino IDE with Qwiic ecosystem for sensor expansion.
