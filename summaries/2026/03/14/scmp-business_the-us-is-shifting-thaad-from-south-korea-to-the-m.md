The US is redeploying components of its Terminal High Altitude Area Defence (THAAD) anti-missile system from South Korea to the Middle East to support its military operations against Iran. This cross-theatre movement of advanced missile defence assets also includes sending Patriot systems to Saudi Arabia and the UAE. South Korea has indirectly confirmed the moves, with President Lee Jae Myung acknowledging that Seoul's opinions "would not be heeded."
