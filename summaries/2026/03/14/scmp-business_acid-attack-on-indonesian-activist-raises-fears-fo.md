【是什么 / What it is】
印度尼西亚人权组织 KontraS 的副协调员 Andrie Yunus 在雅加达遭遇泼酸袭击，导致面部、一只眼睛、双手和胸部严重烧伤，全身24%面积受损。这起事件发生在 Prabowo Subianto 就任总统后不久，引发了对印尼民主状况和人权活动人士安全的广泛担忧。

Indonesian human rights activist Andrie Yunus, deputy coordinator of the KontraS rights group, was attacked with acid in Jakarta, sustaining serious burns to his face, an eye, hands and chest—covering 24% of his body. The incident occurred shortly after Prabowo Subianto assumed the presidency, sparking widespread concerns about Indonesia's democratic conditions and the safety of human rights defenders.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
此次袭击被 KontraS 视为"压制社会批评声音的企图"，且受害者此前已因 activism 活动收到过威胁，显示出针对人权工作者的系统性暴力风险。联合国人权事务高级专员 Volker Turk 已对此表示"深切关注"，反映出国际社会对印尼人权状况恶化的警觉。

The attack was characterized by KontraS as "an attempt to silence critical voices," and the victim had previously received threats for his activism, indicating systemic violence risks against human rights workers. UN High Commissioner for Human Rights Volker Turk's expression of "deep concern" reflects international alarm over deteriorating human rights conditions in Indonesia.
