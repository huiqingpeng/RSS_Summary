【是什么 / What it is】

Air China, one of China's largest state-owned airlines, will resume direct flights between Beijing and Pyongyang starting March 30, 2025, marking the first revival of this route since its suspension in January 2020 due to the Covid-19 pandemic. The service currently operates weekly on Mondays, with economy fares starting at 2,630 yuan (US$381) for the nearly two-hour journey.
