【是什么 / What it is】
小鹏汽车旗下飞行汽车公司Aridge（前身为AeroHT）完成2亿美元融资，累计股权融资达10亿美元，成为亚洲融资规模最大的飞行汽车开发商。该公司正加速推进"陆地航母"飞行汽车的量产制造，并计划最快于今年在香港上市，已聘请摩根大通和摩根士丹利担任承销商。

Xpeng's flying car affiliate Aridge (formerly AeroHT) raised US$200 million, bringing total equity financing to about US$1 billion and making it Asia's most-funded flying car developer. The company is ramping up production of its "Land Aircraft Carrier" vehicle and pursuing an IPO on the Hong Kong stock exchange as soon as this year, with JPMorgan Chase and Morgan Stanley as underwriters.
