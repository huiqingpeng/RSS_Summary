【是什么 / What it is】
Meta 正在计划新一轮大规模裁员，可能裁减多达 16,000 名员工（约占其 79,000 人总员工的 20%），这是 CEO 扎克伯格将公司战略重心转向人工智能领域的举措之一。目前裁员的具体时间和规模尚未最终确定，但高管已指示各部门负责人开始制定缩减计划。

Meta is planning another massive round of layoffs that could eliminate up to 16,000 employees (about 20% of its 79,000 total workforce), as CEO Mark Zuckerberg shifts the company's strategic focus toward artificial intelligence. While no specific timeline or final scale has been confirmed, executives have already instructed department heads to begin preparing reduction plans.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
这标志着科技行业"效率优先"时代的延续，即使是 Meta 这样的巨头也在通过激进裁员为 AI 投资腾挪资源，反映出 AI 转型对传统岗位的冲击正在加速。此次裁员若落实，将成为 Meta 自 2022-2023 年"效率年"以来最大规模的人员调整，可能引发整个行业对人力资源配置的重新评估。

This signals the continuation of the "efficiency-first" era in tech, where even giants like Meta are aggressively cutting jobs to free up resources for AI investments, reflecting how AI transformation is accelerating its impact on traditional roles. If implemented, this would become Meta's largest workforce reduction since its 2022-2023 "year of efficiency," potentially triggering industry-wide reassessments of human resource allocation.
