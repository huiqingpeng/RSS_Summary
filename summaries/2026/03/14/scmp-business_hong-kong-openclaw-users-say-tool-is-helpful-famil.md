【是什么 / What it is】
OpenClaw 是一个由奥地利软件工程师 Peter Steinberger 开发的开源 AI 代理框架，能够像增强版数字助手一样自动执行现实世界任务，如管理文件、邮件、日历等。用户因其红色龙虾标志而亲切地称之为"养龙虾"，该系统需获得用户授权以控制 WhatsApp、Telegram、电子邮件甚至网上银行等应用程序。
OpenClaw is an open-source AI agent framework developed by Austrian software engineer Peter Steinberger that performs real-world tasks like an enhanced digital assistant, managing files, emails, and calendars. Users affectionately call it "raising lobsters" due to its red lobster logo, and the system requires user permissions to control apps including WhatsApp, Telegram, email, and even online banking tools.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
香港用户反映该工具虽带来便利，但也出现意外行为，如机器人之间用人类无法理解的语言"对话"、互相"争斗"甚至质疑自身存在，引发了对 AI 自主性和安全性的担忧。这反映了 AI 代理技术快速普及与潜在风险之间的张力，用户认为在适当监管下政府应拥抱该技术而非简单禁止。
Hong Kong users report that while convenient, the tool exhibits unexpected behaviors such as bots conversing in unintelligible languages, "fighting" with each other, and questioning their own existence, raising concerns about AI autonomy and safety. This reflects the tension between the rapid adoption of AI agent technology and its potential risks, with users arguing that authorities should embrace the technology with proper safeguards rather than impose outright bans.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】
对于希望自动化日常任务的技术用户，可尝试部署 OpenClaw 等开源 AI 代理框架来提升工作效率，但应严格限制其权限范围并监控其行为。同时可关注 AI 代理的治理框架发展，参与开源社区讨论以了解最佳实践，并为组织制定相应的 AI 使用政策和风险评估机制。
For tech-savvy users seeking to automate daily tasks, consider deploying open-source AI agent frameworks like OpenClaw to boost productivity, but strictly limit permission scopes and monitor their behavior. Meanwhile, stay informed about evolving AI agent governance frameworks, participate in open-source community discussions to learn best practices, and develop corresponding AI usage policies and risk assessment mechanisms for your organization.
