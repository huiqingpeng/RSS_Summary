【是什么 / What it is】

于尔根·哈贝马斯（Jürgen Habermas）是德国最具影响力的当代哲学家之一，以"交往行为理论"和公共领域中的理性共识构建闻名。他于2025年6月21日去世，享年96岁，其七十余年的学术生涯与公共介入深刻塑造了战后德国的道德话语与政治反思。

Jürgen Habermas was one of Germany's most influential contemporary philosophers, renowned for his "theory of communicative action" and rational consensus-building in the public sphere. He died on June 21, 2025, at age 96, leaving behind a seven-decade academic and public career that profoundly shaped post-war Germany's moral discourse and political self-reflection.

Habermas's death comes at a critical juncture in German history: post-war pacifism is waning while the far-right AfD has become the second-strongest parliamentary party, making his critical legacy—persistent vigilance against fascism, nationalism, and militarism—urgently relevant again. He demonstrated that intellectuals can engage in and guide a society's democratic self-understanding through public intervention, not merely academic research.
