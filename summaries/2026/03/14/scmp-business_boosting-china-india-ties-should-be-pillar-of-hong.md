【是什么 / What it is】
本文主张将促进中印关系作为香港下一个五年规划的重要支柱。文章分析了中印两国在经济结构上的互补性——中国领先先进制造业，印度则是全球服务枢纽，并强调香港凭借普通法制度、双语优势和历史人文纽带，可在两国关系中发挥独特桥梁作用。

This article argues that boosting China-India ties should be a key pillar of Hong Kong's upcoming five-year plan. It analyzes the economic complementarity between the two Asian powers—China's strength in advanced manufacturing versus India's position as a global services hub—and highlights Hong Kong's unique bridging role through its common law system, bilingualism, and historical people-to-people connections.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】
到21世纪下半叶，中印关系有望成为超越中美关系的最重要地缘政治关系，两国更可能共同塑造非西方主导的国际秩序。香港若不能把握这一战略机遇，将削弱其作为"中国最国际化城市"的定位；反之，则可借助印度 diaspora 的既有网络，在金融服务、物流和供应链管理等关键领域深化合作。

By the latter half of the 21st century, the Sino-Indian relationship is projected to become the world's most significant geopolitical dynamic, potentially surpassing the China-US rivalry. For Hong Kong, failing to seize this strategic opportunity would weaken its positioning as "China's most international city"; conversely, leveraging the existing Indian diaspora network could deepen cooperation in finance, logistics, and supply chain management.
