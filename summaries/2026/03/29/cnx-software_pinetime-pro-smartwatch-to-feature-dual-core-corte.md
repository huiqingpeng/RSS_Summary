【是什么 / What it is】

Pine64 正在开发 PineTime Pro 智能手表，这是 2019 年推出的开源 PineTime 的重大升级版本。该设备采用双核 Cortex-M33 微控制器、2.13 英寸 AMOLED 显示屏、GPS 和更丰富的传感器，定位为面向开发者和开源社区的硬件平台。

Pine64 is developing the PineTime Pro smartwatch, a major upgrade to the open-source PineTime launched in 2019. The device features a dual-core Cortex-M33 microcontroller, 2.13-inch AMOLED display, GPS, and enhanced sensors, positioned as a hardware platform for developers and the open-source community.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

PineTime Pro 代表了开源硬件在可穿戴设备领域的显著进步——更大的内存（800KB SRAM + 8MB PSRAM）和闪存使更复杂的固件成为可能，同时保持了开源生态的兼容性。这也反映了 Pine64 与中国大型智能手表制造商合作获取定制芯片的趋势，可能降低开源硬件的成本门槛并加速创新。

The PineTime Pro represents significant advancement for open-source hardware in wearables—larger memory (800KB SRAM + 8MB PSRAM) and flash enable more complex firmware while maintaining open-source ecosystem compatibility. It also reflects Pine64's trend of partnering with major Chinese smartwatch manufacturers for custom chips, potentially lowering cost barriers and accelerating innovation in open-source hardware.
