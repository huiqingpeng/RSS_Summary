【是什么 / What it is】

The Mekotronics R57-5S is a Rockchip RK3576-powered mini PC designed for kiosk and digital signage applications, featuring a distinctive built-in inclined 5-inch touchscreen. It comes with a 6 TOPS AI accelerator, flexible storage options (eMMC/UFS/SSD), and rich industrial interfaces including RS232/RS485, dual Gigabit Ethernet, and HDMI input/output.
