【是什么 / What it is】

英特尔在CES 2026上低调发布了Wildcat Lake系列入门级处理器，采用全新的1P+4LPE五核架构（1个性能核+4个低功耗效率核），基于Intel 18A工艺制造，并首次在入门级产品中集成Xe3架构GPU和NPU 5 AI加速器。这是英特尔面向低成本迷你PC和边缘计算设备的下一代低功耗平台。

Intel discreetly launched the Wildcat Lake series entry-level processors at CES 2026, featuring a new 1P+4LPE penta-core architecture (1 Performance core + 4 Low Power Efficiency cores), manufactured on Intel 18A process, and debuting Xe3-architecture GPU and NPU 5 AI accelerator in the entry-level segment. This represents Intel's next-generation low-power platform for cost-effective mini PCs and edge computing devices.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

Wildcat Lake标志着英特尔18A工艺首次量产，同时也是Xe3 GPU和NPU 5的首次亮相，单核性能较上一代Alder Lake-N提升近两倍，AI算力达到15-17 TOPS，为入门级设备带来显著的图形和AI能力跃升。然而，由于LPDDR5x内存成本较高，2026年"平价"迷你PC的入门价格可能从往年的150-200美元上涨至约400美元。

Wildcat Lake marks Intel 18A's first mass production, alongside the debut of Xe3 GPU and NPU 5, delivering nearly double the single-core performance over Alder Lake-N with 15-17 TOPS AI compute, bringing substantial graphics and AI capabilities to entry-level devices. However, due to costly LPDDR5x memory, "affordable" mini PCs in 2026 may see entry prices rise from the historical $150-200 to around $400.
