【是什么 / What it is】

Digi IX25 是 Digi International 推出的一款坚固型工业级 5G 蜂窝路由器，基于 1.2 GHz 四核 ARM Cortex-A53 处理器，提供 5G eMBB（增强移动宽带）、5G RedCap（精简能力）和 LTE 三种蜂窝网络变体，专为公用事业、油气、交通、自助服务终端等关键企业应用设计。

The Digi IX25 is a rugged industrial 5G cellular router from Digi International built around a 1.2 GHz quad-core ARM Cortex-A53 processor. It comes in three cellular variants—5G eMBB (Enhanced Mobile Broadband), 5G RedCap (Reduced Capability), and LTE—designed for critical enterprise applications in utilities, oil & gas, transportation, and kiosks.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

该设备首次将 5G RedCap 技术引入工业路由器领域，填补了高速 eMBB 与低速 LPWA 之间的市场空白，使中带宽物联网应用（如远程监控、智能计量）能以更低成本、更低功耗获得 5G 网络优势；同时其 −40°C 至 75°C 宽温运行、ATEX/C1D2/MIL-STD-810H 认证，满足了恶劣工业环境对可靠性的严苛要求。

This device brings 5G RedCap technology to the industrial router market for the first time, filling the gap between high-speed eMBB and low-power LPWA. It enables medium-bandwidth IoT applications like remote monitoring and smart metering to benefit from 5G with lower cost and power consumption. Its wide operating temperature (−40°C to 75°C) and ATEX/C1D2/MIL-STD-810H certifications meet stringent reliability requirements for harsh industrial environments.

【我能用什么 / How I can use it】

若您从事工业物联网或边缘计算项目，可根据带宽需求选择合适变体：高带宽视频流选 eMBB 版，中等数据量的遥测/监控选 RedCap 版以节省成本；利用其 LXC 容器和 Python 支持在本地运行边缘分析，减少云端依赖；通过 Digi Remote Manager 实现大规模设备的远程配置、OTA 更新和生命周期管理，特别适用于分布式基础设施场景。

For industrial IoT or edge computing projects, select the appropriate variant based on bandwidth needs: choose eMBB for high-bandwidth video streaming, or RedCap for medium-data telemetry and monitoring to reduce costs. Leverage its LXC containers and Python support to run edge analytics locally, reducing cloud dependency. Use Digi Remote Manager for remote configuration, OTA updates, and lifecycle management of large-scale deployments, particularly suited for distributed infrastructure scenarios.
