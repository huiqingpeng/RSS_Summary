【是什么 / What it is】

Raspberry Pi 推出了一款配备 3GB RAM 的新型 Raspberry Pi 4 单板计算机，售价 83.75 美元，这是通过使用两个 1.5GB LPDDR4 芯片实现的非标准内存配置。同时，由于 LPDDR4 DRAM 价格在过去一年上涨了七倍，Raspberry Pi 宣布对其 4GB 及以上内存的 Raspberry Pi 4/5、Compute Module 4/5 及 AI HAT+ 2 等产品进行第三轮全面涨价。

Raspberry Pi has launched a new Raspberry Pi 4 single-board computer with 3GB RAM priced at $83.75, achieved through an unconventional configuration using two 1.5GB LPDDR4 chips. Simultaneously, due to a seven-fold increase in LPDDR4 DRAM prices over the past year, Raspberry Pi announced its third round of price hikes across the board for 4GB+ RAM variants of Raspberry Pi 4/5, Compute Module 4/5, and AI HAT+ 2.

【为什么重要 / Why it matters】

这标志着单板计算机市场正面临严重的内存成本压力，3GB 配置的推出反映了供应链约束下的产品策略调整——在芯片稀缺时利用非标准组合填补价格空白。涨价幅度显著（4GB 型号涨 25 美元，16GB 型号最高涨 100-150 美元），可能重塑边缘 AI、物联网和教育市场的硬件选型格局，同时加剧与 Orange Pi、Radxa 等竞品的价格竞争。

This signals severe memory cost pressures in the SBC market, with the 3GB configuration reflecting a supply-chain-driven product strategy—using non-standard chip combinations to fill pricing gaps during component scarcity. The substantial price increases ($25 for 4GB models, up to $100-150 for 16GB variants) may reshape hardware choices in edge AI, IoT, and education markets, while intensifying price competition with alternatives from Orange Pi, Radxa, and others.
