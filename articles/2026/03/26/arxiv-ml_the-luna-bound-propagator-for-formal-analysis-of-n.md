Title:The Luna Bound Propagator for Formal Analysis of Neural Networks
View PDF HTML (experimental)Abstract:The parameterized CROWN analysis, a.k.a., alpha-CROWN, has emerged as a practically successful bound propagation method for neural network verification. However, existing implementations of alpha-CROWN are limited to Python, which complicates integration into existing DNN verifiers and long-term production-level systems. We introduce Luna, a new bound propagator implemented in C++. Luna supports Interval Bound Propagation, the CROWN analysis, and the alpha-CROWN analysis over a general computational graph. We describe the architecture of Luna and show that it is competitive with the state-of-the-art alpha-CROWN implementation in terms of both bound tightness and computational efficiency on benchmarks from VNN-COMP 2025.
