Title:IncreRTL: Traceability-Guided Incremental RTL Generation under Requirement Evolution
View PDF HTML (experimental)Abstract:Large language models (LLMs) have shown promise in generating RTL code from natural-language descriptions, but existing methods remain static and struggle to adapt to evolving design requirements, potentially causing structural drift and costly full regeneration. We propose IncreRTL, a LLM-driven framework for incremental RTL generation under requirement evolution. By constructing requirement-code traceability links to locate and regenerate affected code segments, IncreRTL achieves accurate and consistent updates. Evaluated on our newly constructed EvoRTL-Bench, IncreRTL demonstrates notable improvements in regeneration consistency and efficiency, advancing LLM-based RTL generation toward practical engineering deployment.
