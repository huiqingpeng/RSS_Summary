Mekotronics is known for its unusual Rockchip devices, and the R57-5S is a Rockchip RK3576 mini PC for kiosks and digital signage applications with a built-in, inclined 5-inch touchscreen display.
The system ships with up to 16GB LPDDR5, up to 128GB eMMC flash, or up to 1TB UFS flash. It also features an M.2 socket for storage or an AI accelerator, HDMI 2.1 and USB-C DP video outputs, a 4K-capable HDMI input port, dual GbE, WiFi 5 and Bluetooth 5.1, optional 4G LTE, a few USB ports, and a terminal block with RS232 and RS485 interfaces.
MekotronicsR57-5S specifications:
- SoC – Rockchip RK3576
- CPU
- 4x Cortex-A72 cores @ 2.2GHz, four Cortex-A53 cores @ 1.8GHz
- Arm Cortex-M0 MCU at 400MHz
- GPU – ARM Mali-G52 MC3 GPU with support for OpenGL ES 1.1, 2.0 and 3.2, OpenCL up to 2.0 and Vulkan 1.1
- NPU – 6 TOPS (INT8) AI accelerator with support for INT4, INT8, INT16, BF16, and TF32 mixed operations.
- VPU
- Video Decoder: H.264, H.265, VP9, AV1, and AVS2 up to 8K @ 30fps or 4K @ 120fps
- Video Encoder: H.264 and H.265 up to 4K @ 60fps, (M)JPEG encoder/decoder up to 4K @ 60fps
- CPU
- System Memory – 4GB, 8GB, or 16GB LPDDR5
- Storage
- 32GB, 64GB, or 128GB eMMC flash
- 32GB, 64GB, 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, or 1TB UFS flash
- M.2 NVMe 2280 SSD via M.2 M-Key socket
- Display – 5-inch touchscreen display
- Video Output
- HDMI 2.1 port up to 4Kp120 or 8Kp30 with support for HDR10, HDR HLG, HDR ViVid
- USB Type-C port with DisplayPort 1.4
- Three displays supported with built-in touch display
- Video Input – HDMI port up to 4Kp60
- Audio – 3.5mm audio jack, digital audio output via HDMI
- Networking
- 2x Gigabit Ethernet RJ45 ports
- Dual-band WiFi 5 and Bluetooth 5.1 via AP6256 module
- Optional 4G LTE and GPS via mini PCIe module + Nano SIM card slot
- USB – 1x USB 3.0 port, 2x USB 2.0 ports, 1x USB Type-C port with DisplayPort 1.4 alt mode
- Expansion
- M.2 M-Key (PCIe 3.0) socket for NVMe storage or AI accelerator
- Mini PCIe socket for 4G LTE/GPS
- Terminal block with RS232, RS485, and GPIO + H/L switch
- Misc
- Power button
- RTC
- Power Supply – 12V/2A DC via power barrel jack
- Dimensions – 162 x 111 x 46mm (aluminium housing)
The hardware is somewhat similar to the company’s R57 mini PC introduced in 2024, but adds a touchscreen display. The design reminds me of Intel-based mini PCs from PIPO and GOLE that integrate a similarly inclined touchscreen display, allegedly to work around Windows license requirements at the time.
The Mekotronics R57-5S targets different applications as an Arm-based system with support for Android 14, Buildroot, Debian, and Armbian. It’s clearly not a consumer product, seeing the huge bezel in the photo below, but it could be made part of kiosks or digital signage solutions with the small 5-inch touch display acting as an interface to control one or two larger displays connected over HDMI and/or USB-C.
The Mekotronics R57-5S is listed on Made-in-China for $260 (4GB/32GB model), but the price is also shown to be negotiable, so go figure. I’ve just come across it today, but it looks to have been launched last year, and Leepsvideo had a quick look at the mini PC in the video hands-on below.
