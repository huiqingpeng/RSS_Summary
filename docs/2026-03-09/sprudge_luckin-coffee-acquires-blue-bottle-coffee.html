Luckin Coffee Acquires Blue Bottle Coffee

Sprudge 2026-03-09
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

瑞幸咖啡以4亿美元从雀巢手中收购了精品咖啡品牌蓝瓶咖啡（Blue Bottle Coffee），获得其全球100多家门店。这笔交易使瑞幸在保持平价快咖啡定位的同时，成功进入高端精品咖啡市场。
Luckin Coffee has acquired Blue Bottle Coffee from Nestle for $400 million, gaining over 100 global locations. This deal allows Luckin to enter the premium specialty coffee market while maintaining its original fast-service, budget-friendly brand positioning.

为什么重要 / Why it matters

这笔收购标志着中国咖啡品牌首次反向收购美国标志性精品咖啡品牌，反映了中国咖啡企业的全球扩张野心。同时，雀巢保留零售业务、剥离"不盈利"门店的做法，也揭示了精品咖啡实体店模式的困境与转型趋势。
This acquisition marks the first time a Chinese coffee brand has reverse-acquired an iconic American specialty coffee brand, reflecting Chinese coffee companies' global expansion ambitions. Meanwhile, Nestle's strategy of keeping retail while divesting "unprofitable" physical stores reveals the challenges and transformation trends in the specialty coffee brick-and-mortar model.

我能用什么 / How I can use it

关注瑞幸如何将蓝瓶咖啡引入中国市场，尤其是在星巴克臻选店关闭的商场选址策略，这可能重塑国内高端咖啡竞争格局。对于从业者而言，可研究这种"双品牌战略"——用独立高端品牌拓展新客群，同时保护主品牌定位——是否适用于其他消费品类。
Watch how Luckin introduces Blue Bottle to China, particularly its mall location strategy targeting spaces vacated by Starbucks Reserve stores, which may reshape the domestic premium coffee competitive landscape. For industry practitioners, study whether this "dual-brand strategy"—using a separate premium brand to expand customer base while protecting the main brand's positioning—can apply to other consumer categories.
