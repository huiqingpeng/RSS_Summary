是什么 / What it is

全球市值最大的制药公司礼来（Eli Lilly）宣布投资30亿美元扩大其在中国的供应链布局，重点包括建立口服固体制剂的本土化生产体系，以及与本土企业药明康德（Pharmaron Beijing）等建立战略合作。这是礼来在华累计投资近60亿美元的重要组成部分，核心目标是为其首创口服小分子GLP-1受体激动剂orforglipron（用于治疗2型糖尿病和肥胖症）建设大规模产能。
Eli Lilly, the world's largest pharmaceutical company by market capitalization, announced a US$3 billion investment to expand its supply chain footprint in China, focusing on establishing a domestic production system for oral solid preparations and forming strategic partnerships with local leaders like Pharmaron Beijing. This brings its total cumulative investment in China to nearly US$6 billion, with the centerpiece being high-volume manufacturing capacity for orforglipron, its first-in-class oral small-molecule GLP-1 receptor agonist for type 2 diabetes and obesity. ---

这一投资反映了跨国药企在中国市场的"本土化竞争"加剧——在GLP-1类药物（如司美格鲁肽、替尔泊肽）全球需求爆发的背景下，谁能率先实现中国本土生产和供应链稳定，谁就能在定价、供应保障和市场准入上占据优势。同时，礼来选择与本土CDMO（合同研发生产组织）深度合作，也体现了中国医药制造能力的国际认可度提升，以及外资药企"在中国，为中国"战略的深化。
This investment reflects intensifying "localization competition" among multinational pharma companies—amid global demand surge for GLP-1 drugs, securing local production and supply chain stability in China offers advantages in pricing, supply assurance, and market access. Additionally, Eli Lilly's deep collaboration with domestic CDMOs signals rising international recognition of China's pharmaceutical manufacturing capabilities and the deepening "In China, For China" strategy by foreign firms. ---
