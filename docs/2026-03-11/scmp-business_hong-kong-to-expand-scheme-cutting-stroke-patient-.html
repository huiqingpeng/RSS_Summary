Hong Kong to expand scheme cutting stroke patient transfer time by 80 minutes

SCMP Business 2026-03-11
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

香港医管局于2023年12月推出一项中风患者分流试点计划，允许救护人员将严重中风患者直接送往具备手术能力的七家指定医院，而非最近的急诊医院。该计划目前仅覆盖新界地区，待数据收集充足后将扩展至全港。
Hong Kong's Hospital Authority launched a pilot diversion programme in December 2023 that allows paramedics to transport serious stroke patients directly to seven designated hospitals with surgical capabilities, rather than the nearest A&E hospital. Currently limited to the New Territories, the programme will expand citywide after sufficient data collection.

为什么重要 / Why it matters

该计划可将患者转院时间缩短80分钟，对中风这种"时间就是大脑"的急症至关重要；此前18家急诊医院中仅7家能处理严重中风，患者常被送至无能力救治的医院后再二次转院，延误黄金救治窗口。这体现了医疗系统通过流程优化而非单纯增加资源来提升急救效率的趋势。
This programme can reduce transfer time by 80 minutes, critical for stroke where "time is brain"; previously only 7 of 18 A&E hospitals could treat serious strokes, causing dangerous delays from secondary transfers. It demonstrates a trend of healthcare systems improving emergency efficiency through process optimization rather than solely adding resources.
