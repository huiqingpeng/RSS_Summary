是什么 / What it is

这是一篇介绍Moeya 3D Designs团队用3D打印技术复刻老式旋转拨号电话拨盘的文章。该拨盘本质上是一个机械脉冲链发生器，包含棘轮、弹簧、齿轮组和离心调速器等完整机械结构，虽不如原版紧凑但功能齐全。
This article introduces a fully 3D-printed replica of vintage rotary telephone dial created by Moeya 3D Designs. The dial, essentially a mechanical pulse chain generator, features complete mechanical components including a ratchet, spring, gear train, and centrifugal speed governor—functional though less compact than the original. ---

As rotary phones fade into history, younger generations struggle to understand their mechanical principles; this project provides a tangible learning medium for technical heritage preservation. 3D printing enables low-cost replication of complex mechanical devices, lowering barriers for retro-tech education and maker experimentation while demonstrating digital manufacturing's potential in safeguarding industrial heritage. ---
