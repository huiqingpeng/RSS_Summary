DR-DOS Is Back, But Not Quite as We Knew It

是什么 / What it is

一位名为 CheeseWeezel 的开发者通过净室实现（clean-room implementation）重新打造了 DR-DOS 操作系统，并购买了商标使其成为官方版本，目前 DR-DOS 9.0 处于测试阶段。由于该操作系统知识产权历史的复杂性——历经 Digital Research、Novell、Caldera 等多家公司转手，且曾出现 GPL 违规事件——开发者选择完全重写代码而不开源。 A developer known as CheeseWeezel has recreated the DR-DOS operating system through clean-room implementation and purchased the trademark to make it official, with DR-DOS 9.0 currently in beta. Due to the tortured history of the IP—passing through Digital Research, Novell, Caldera, and others, with past GPL violations—the developer chose to rewrite the code from scratch without open-sourcing it.

为什么重要 / Why it matters

DR-DOS 曾是 MS-DOS 的强劲竞争对手，在技术上有诸多优势，此次"复活"让 20 世纪 90 年代中断的 DOS 血统得以延续，对复古计算爱好者和操作系统历史保护具有特殊意义。这也反映了开源/闭源许可争议对软件遗产传承的深远影响，以及社区驱动项目在保护技术历史方面的价值。 This revival continues the lineage of DOS that was interrupted in the 1990s, holding special significance for retrocomputing enthusiasts and operating system preservation. It also illustrates how licensing controversies affect software heritage, and demonstrates the value of community-driven projects in protecting technological history.
