This article introduces the Clura open-source smart 3D printer enclosure. Designed for open-concept printers, it features HEPA and activated carbon filtration systems to capture toxic volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and plastic particles released during printing, along with smart capabilities like filament weighing and fume detection.
