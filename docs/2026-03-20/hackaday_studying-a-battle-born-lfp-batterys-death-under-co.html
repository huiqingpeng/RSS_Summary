是什么 / What it is

这是一篇关于Battle Born品牌磷酸铁锂（LiFePO4/LFP）电池故障问题的技术测试报告。技术博主Will Prowse通过受控实验，对一块75Ah的Battle Born LFP电池进行充放电循环测试，以验证近期大量用户报告的电池过热、塑料外壳熔化及BMS（电池管理系统）异常等问题。
This is a technical test report on the failure issues of Battle Born brand LiFePO4 (LFP) batteries. Tech blogger Will Prowse conducted controlled charge-discharge cycle tests on a 75Ah Battle Born LFP battery to verify widespread user reports of overheating, melted plastic enclosures, and BMS (Battery Management System) malfunctions. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

该测试揭示了Battle Born LFP电池存在严重的安全隐患：即使在远低于额定电流（49A vs 100A）的条件下，仅14个循环后BMS就频繁断开，且未能进入安全保护模式，导致持续的热损伤和塑料熔化。更关键的是，制造商完全否认这些问题，拒绝召回，使已安装用户面临潜在火灾风险。
This test reveals serious safety flaws in Battle Born LFP batteries: even at well below rated current (49A vs 100A), the BMS began frequent disconnections after merely 14 cycles and failed to enter safe protection mode, causing continuous thermal damage and plastic melting. Critically, the manufacturer completely denies these issues and refuses to issue recalls, leaving installed users at potential fire risk. ---

我能用什么 / How I can use it

如果你拥有Battle Born LFP电池，应立即进行带载测试（load test）而非仅测量空载电压，以检测BMS是否频繁断开；若发现异常，建议更换为其他可靠品牌。对于电池选购，此案例警示需关注BMS的安全保护机制完整性，优先选择有第三方安全认证和负责任售后政策的厂商。
If you own Battle Born LFP batteries, immediately perform load testing (not just no-load voltage measurement) to detect frequent BMS disconnections; replace with reliable alternatives if anomalies are found. For battery purchasing, this case highlights the importance of verifying BMS safety protection integrity and prioritizing manufacturers with third-party safety certifications and responsible after-sales policies.
