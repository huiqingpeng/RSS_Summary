是什么 / What it is

Seeed Studio推出的reTerminal D1001是一款8英寸电容触摸屏HMI（人机界面）开发套件，基于ESP32-P4 RISC-V微控制器，并集成ESP32-C6无线模块。该设备配备摄像头、双麦克风阵列、扬声器，并可选配4G LTE和LoRaWAN扩展功能，主要面向控制面板、视觉物联网终端、视频对讲和智能仪表盘等应用场景。
The reTerminal D1001 is an 8-inch capacitive touch HMI (Human-Machine Interface) development kit from Seeed Studio, powered by an ESP32-P4 RISC-V microcontroller with an integrated ESP32-C6 wireless module. It features a camera, dual-microphone array, speaker, and optional 4G LTE and LoRaWAN expansion capabilities, targeting applications such as control panels, vision-enabled IoT terminals, video intercoms, and smart dashboards. ---

This device represents a significant evolution in low-cost edge AI and IoT HMI solutions, consolidating high-performance RISC-V processing, multi-protocol wireless connectivity (WiFi 6/Bluetooth 5.x/802.15.4), and optional cellular/LoRaWAN wide-area communications on a single platform.
