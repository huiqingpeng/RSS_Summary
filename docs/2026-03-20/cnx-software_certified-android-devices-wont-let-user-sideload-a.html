Certified Android devices won’t let user sideload APK app files anymore, or at least it won’t be straightforward

CNX Software 2026-03-20
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

Google 宣布自 2026 年 9 月起，将对认证 Android 设备的 APK 侧载（sideloading）实施新限制，要求开发者完成身份验证流程，而用户安装未验证开发者的应用则需通过复杂的"高级流程"。
Google announced that starting September 2026, certified Android devices will impose new restrictions on APK sideloading, requiring developers to complete a verification process, while users installing apps from unverified developers must go through a complex "advanced flow."

为什么重要 / Why it matters

这一变化标志着 Android 开放生态的重大转向，在提升安全防护（如防范诈骗）的同时，也增加了独立开发者和隐私敏感用户的门槛，可能削弱 Android 相对于 iOS 的开放性优势。
This shift marks a significant turn in Android's open ecosystem—while enhancing security protections (such as fraud prevention), it also raises barriers for independent developers and privacy-conscious users, potentially weakening Android's openness advantage over iOS.

我能用什么 / How I can use it

开发者可评估是否加入 Google 的验证计划或申请免费受限分发账户（限 20 台设备）；用户需提前了解"高级流程"步骤，或考虑非认证设备（如部分中国厂商设备）以保持侧载灵活性；关注 2026 年前政策可能的调整。
Developers can evaluate joining Google's verification program or applying for free limited distribution accounts (capped at 20 devices); users should familiarize themselves with the "advanced flow" steps in advance, or consider non-certified devices (such as certain Chinese OEM devices) to retain sideloading flexibility; monitor potential policy adjustments before 2026.
