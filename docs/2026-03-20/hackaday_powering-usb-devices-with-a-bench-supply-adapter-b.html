Powering USB Devices with a Bench Supply Adapter Board

Hackaday 2026-03-20
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

这是一个开源的USB供电适配器电路板项目，由KS-Elektronikdesign设计，用于将实验室常用的可调直流稳压电源（bench supply）转换为可为USB-A和USB-C设备供电的接口。该板卡集成了TUSB319芯片来处理USB电源协商协议，并具备极性保护和过压保护功能，支持最高35W的输出功率。
This is an open-source USB power adapter board project designed by KS-Elektronikdesign, which converts a common laboratory adjustable DC bench power supply into an interface capable of powering USB-A and USB-C devices. The board integrates a TUSB319 chip to handle USB power negotiation protocols, features polarity and over-voltage protection, and supports output power up to 35W. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

在硬件开发和测试场景中，工程师经常需要精确控制供电电压和电流，而普通USB充电器无法提供这种灵活性；同时，现代USB设备（尤其是USB-C）需要复杂的电源协商才能正常工作，简单直连可能导致设备拒绝充电或损坏。该工具填补了实验室精密电源与现代USB设备之间的鸿沟，既保留了可调电源的灵活性，又确保了兼容性和安全性。
In hardware development and testing scenarios, engineers often need precise control over voltage and current, which standard USB chargers cannot provide; meanwhile, modern USB devices (especially USB-C) require complex power negotiation to function properly, and simple direct connections may cause devices to refuse charging or become damaged. This tool bridges the gap between laboratory precision power supplies and modern USB devices, preserving the flexibility of adjustable supplies while ensuring compatibility and safety. ---

我能用什么 / How I can use it

如果你是电子工程师或硬件爱好者，可以复刻此设计或购买成品用于实验室，在调试IoT设备、传感器模块或小型开发板时获得精确的电压/电流监控能力；可进一步学习USB PD（Power Delivery）和USB PPS（Programmable Power Supply）协议，开发支持更高功率（如100W）的自定义供电方案；也可将此设计思路迁移到其他接口转换场景，例如为其他专用连接器制作类似的协商协议适配板。
If you are an electronics engineer or hardware hobbyist, you can replicate this design or purchase a ready-made unit for your lab to gain precise voltage/current monitoring capabilities when debugging IoT devices, sensor modules, or small development boards; you can further study USB PD (Power Delivery) and USB PPS (Programmable Power Supply) protocols to develop custom power solutions supporting higher power (e.g., 100W); you may also adapt this design concept to other interface conversion scenarios, such as creating similar negotiation protocol adapter boards for other specialized connectors.
