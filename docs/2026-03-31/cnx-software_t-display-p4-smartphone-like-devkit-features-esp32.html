是什么 / What it is

LILYGO T-Display-P4 是一款采用智能手机外形设计的 ESP32-P4 开发套件，集成了 ESP32-C6 无线 SoC 和 SX1262/LR2021 LoRa 收发器，支持 WiFi 6、蓝牙 5.x、LoRaWAN、GPS 和以太网等多种连接方式，配备 4.05 英寸 IPS 或 4.1 英寸 AMOLED 触摸屏、摄像头、音频接口和丰富的扩展接口。
The LILYGO T-Display-P4 is a smartphone-like ESP32-P4 development kit featuring an ESP32-C6 wireless SoC and SX1262/LR2021 LoRa transceiver, supporting WiFi 6, Bluetooth 5.x, LoRaWAN, GPS, and Ethernet connectivity, along with a 4.05" IPS or 4.1" AMOLED touchscreen, camera, audio interfaces, and extensive expansion options. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

This devkit represents a significant trend toward "smartphone-like" form factors in embedded devices—combining high-performance MCU (360MHz dual-core RISC-V with AI extensions), professional multimedia capabilities (H.264/JPEG codecs, 2D GPU), and multi-protocol wireless connectivity (WiFi 6 + LoRaWAN + cellular-ready) in a portable package, providing a complete hardware platform for edge AI, outdoor IoT, and decentralized communication networks like Meshtastic/MeshCore. ---
