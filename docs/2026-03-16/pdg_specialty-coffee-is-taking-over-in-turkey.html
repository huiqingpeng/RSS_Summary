Specialty coffee is taking over in Turkey

是什么 / What it is

土耳其拥有500年历史的传统咖啡文化正在经历深刻变革，精品咖啡（specialty coffee）正在伊斯坦布尔以外的安卡拉、伊兹密尔等城市快速崛起，同时本土品牌如Espressolab正积极向全球市场扩张。
Turkey's 500-year-old traditional coffee culture is undergoing a profound transformation, with specialty coffee rapidly rising beyond Istanbul to cities like Ankara and Izmir, while domestic brands such as Espressolab are aggressively expanding into global markets.

为什么重要 / Why it matters

这一趋势反映了年轻一代消费者对咖啡消费场景的重构——从单纯的日常饮品转向社交空间、个性表达和品质追求，同时土耳其正成为欧洲增长最快的品牌咖啡市场之一。
This trend reflects younger generations' reconstruction of coffee consumption—from mere daily beverage to social space, personal expression, and quality pursuit—while Turkey is becoming one of Europe's fastest-growing branded coffee markets.

对于咖啡从业者，可关注土耳其精品咖啡的"第三波"转型路径及本土品牌出海策略；对于消费者，可尝试将传统cezve冲泡法与现代精品咖啡理念结合，探索不同场景下的多元咖啡体验。
