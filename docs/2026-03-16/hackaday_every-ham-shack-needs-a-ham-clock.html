是什么 / What it is

Ham Clock CYD 是一款专为业余无线电（ham radio）操作员设计的独立时钟设备，基于廉价的 ESP32 彩色触摸屏（CYD）开发，配备温湿度传感器和电池备份的实时时钟模块。它无需互联网即可运行，但连接网络时可显示太阳活动预报和短波/VHF 频段传播条件等专业数据。 Ham Clock CYD is a standalone clock device designed for amateur radio operators, built around the affordable ESP32-based color touchscreen display (CYD), with additional temperature/humidity sensor and battery-backed RTC module. It functions without internet access but can display specialized data like solar forecasts and HF/VHF band conditions when connected.

对于依赖精确时间和传播条件信息的无线电操作员而言，这款设备解决了市售方案过度依赖网络、断网即失效的痛点，同时以极低的硬件成本实现了专业功能。它体现了开源硬件社区"自力更生"的精神，为应急通信和偏远地区操作提供了可靠工具。 For radio operators who rely on precise timing and propagation data, this device addresses the pain point of commercial solutions being too internet-dependent, while delivering professional features at minimal hardware cost. It embodies the open-source hardware community's self-reliance ethos and provides a reliable tool for emergency communications and remote operations.
