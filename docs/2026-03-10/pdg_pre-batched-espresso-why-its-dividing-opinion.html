Pre-batched espresso: Why it’s dividing opinion

Perfect Daily Grind 2026-03-10
Read Original Article →

是什么 /

What it is
预萃取浓缩咖啡（Pre-batched espresso）是一种提前萃取浓缩咖啡、冷藏保存、按订单出杯的新式服务方式。该方法通过消除研磨、布粉、压粉等步骤，可将奶咖出品时间缩短至10-20秒，目前已在澳大利亚Project
Zero Coffee等门店应用。 Pre-batched espresso is a novel service method where espresso shots are extracted in advance, chilled for storage, and poured to order. By eliminating steps like grinding, dosing, distributing, and tamping, it reduces milk-based drink preparation time to 10-20 seconds, and has been adopted by cafés such as Project Zero Coffee in Australia. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

这一创新直击精品咖啡行业的核心矛盾：在人力成本高、利润率低（澳大利亚咖啡馆仅7.6%）的高压环境下，如何平衡效率与品质。它挑战了"浓缩咖啡必须在数秒内饮用"的传统科学认知，同时也引发了关于咖啡技艺价值、新鲜度标准与顾客体验本质的行业大讨论。
This innovation strikes at the heart of specialty coffee's central tension: how to balance efficiency and quality amid high labor costs and low profit margins (Australian cafés average only 7.6%). It challenges the scientific consensus that espresso must be consumed within seconds of extraction, sparking industry-wide debate about the value of craft, freshness standards, and the essence of customer experience. ---

我能用什么 / How I can use it

若经营高流量、以奶咖为主的咖啡馆，可试验预萃取方案：选择果香浓郁、经共发酵处理的拼配豆，将TDS控制在12-13%并冷藏于3-5°C；同时保留传统萃取方式供黑咖啡与精品单品使用。关键是以盲测验证品质，并确保团队认同这一流程变革，而非盲目跟风。
For high-volume, milk-drink-focused cafés, consider piloting pre-batching: select co-fermented, fruit-forward blends, target 12-13% TDS, and refrigerate at 3-5°C; maintain traditional extraction for black coffees and premium single origins. Validate quality through blind tasting, and ensure team buy-in rather than adopting trends uncritically.
