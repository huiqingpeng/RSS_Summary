E-payments to attract more riders, cut fare disputes: Hong Kong taxi industry

SCMP Business 2026-03-10
Read Original Article →
【是什么 / What it is】 香港出租车行业正为4月1日实施的强制电子支付规定做准备，要求所有出租车至少提供两种电子支付方式（一种二维码、一种非二维码）。目前约90%的司机已完成电子支付平台注册，香港出租车协会主席表示行业已准备就绪。 Hong Kong's taxi industry is preparing for a mandatory e-payment requirement effective April 1, which requires all taxis to offer at least two e-payment options (one QR code-based and one non-QR method). About 90% of drivers have already registered with e-payment platforms, and the chairman of the Hong Kong Taxi Council stated the industry is ready. 【为什么重要 / Why it matters】 电子支付普及将减少现金找零纠纷，提升乘客体验，同时吸引习惯无现金支付的本地居民和游客重新选择出租车服务。此举反映了全球公共交通数字化趋势，也是香港追赶邻近地区支付便利性的重要一步，违规者将面临5000港元罚款及6个月监禁的严厉处罚。 The widespread adoption of e-payments will reduce fare disputes over change, improve passenger experience, and attract cashless-accustomed residents and tourists back to taxi services. This reflects the global digitalization trend in public transport and represents Hong Kong's crucial step to catch up with neighboring regions' payment convenience, with violators facing severe penalties of HK$5,000 fine and six months' imprisonment. 【我能用什么 / How I can use it】 出行者可利用此政策更便利地选择出租车服务，无需担心现金不足或找零问题；商家和开发者可关注出租车电子支付系统集成机会；其他城市可借鉴香港"双轨制"（二维码+非二维码）的强制规范模式，平衡技术包容性与支付安全性。 Travelers can leverage this policy for more convenient taxi services without worrying about insufficient cash or change issues; merchants and developers can explore opportunities in taxi e-payment system integration; other cities can learn from Hong Kong's "dual-track" mandatory model (QR code + non-QR) to balance technological inclusivity with payment security.
