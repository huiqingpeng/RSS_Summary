Hong Kong to tighten moneylending rules to tackle debt collector harassment

SCMP Business 2026-03-14
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

香港将于8月起实施更严格的放债业监管新规，包括为低收入借款人设置债务偿还比率上限（月入6,000港元以下者还款上限为收入的35%，6,001-12,000港元者为40%），并全面禁止"贷款担保人"制度。此举旨在解决债务催收骚扰问题，尤其针对外籍家庭佣工在合约期满前大额借贷后消失的现象。
Hong Kong will implement stricter moneylending regulations starting August, including debt-servicing ratio caps for low-income borrowers (35% of income for those earning HK$6,000 or below, 40% for HK$6,001-12,000) and a complete ban on "loan referees." These measures target debt collection harassment, particularly addressing cases where foreign domestic helpers take large loans before contract expiry and then disappear.

为什么重要 / Why it matters

新规回应了香港放债市场长期存在的结构性漏洞——低收入群体过度借贷及随之而来的暴力催收问题，体现了政府对金融弱势群体保护的强化。 phased implementation（分阶段实施）也显示出政策制定者平衡市场稳定与消费者保护的审慎态度。
The regulations address long-standing structural gaps in Hong Kong's moneylending market—excessive borrowing by low-income groups and subsequent aggressive debt collection—reflecting strengthened government protection of financially vulnerable populations. The phased implementation also demonstrates policymakers' prudent approach to balancing market stability with consumer protection.
