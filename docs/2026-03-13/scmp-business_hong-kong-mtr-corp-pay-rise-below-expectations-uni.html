Hong Kong MTR Corp pay rise below expectations: union

SCMP Business 2026-03-13
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

香港铁路有限公司（MTR Corp）宣布2026-27财年非管理层员工加薪幅度为1.6%至3.84%，低于去年1.8%至5.4%的调整幅度，同时员工将获得1.4至2.07个月的一次性绩效奖金。工会对此表示失望，认为加薪幅度低于预期。
Hong Kong's MTR Corporation announced pay increases of 1.6% to 3.84% for non-managerial staff for the 2026-27 financial year, down from last year's range of 1.8% to 5.4%, alongside one-off performance bonuses of 1.4 to 2.07 months' salary. Trade unions expressed disappointment, stating the raises fell below expectations.

为什么重要 / Why it matters

这一加薪幅度反映了香港当前经济环境下企业薪酬增长放缓的趋势，尽管MTR录得146亿港元净利润，但薪酬调整趋于保守，可能预示本地就业市场薪资压力增大。同时，工会与企业在薪酬预期上的分歧也凸显了劳资关系中的张力。
The modest pay adjustments reflect a broader trend of slowing salary growth in Hong Kong's current economic climate, with MTR taking a conservative approach despite reporting HK$14.6 billion in net profit—potentially signaling increased wage pressure in the local job market. The gap between union expectations and corporate decisions also highlights ongoing tensions in labor relations.
