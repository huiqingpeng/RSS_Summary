HKEX opens door for diverse innovative firms to list, expand confidential filings, with biggest reforms since 2018

SCMP Business 2026-03-13
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

香港交易所（HKEX）宣布了自2018年以来最大规模的上市制度改革，包括降低同股不同权（WVR）公司的上市门槛至200亿港元（约为原来的一半），并将保密提交上市申请的选项扩展至所有新上市申请人，此前该选项仅适用于二次上市或特定科技企业。
HKEX has announced its biggest listing reforms since 2018, including lowering the minimum valuation threshold for weighted voting rights (WVR) companies to HK$20 billion (half of the previous requirement) and expanding the confidential filing option to all new listing applicants, which was previously limited to secondary listings or certain technology companies.

为什么重要 / Why it matters

此次改革旨在应对美国、英国、新加坡和日本等竞争对手交易所的吸引IPO政策，防止香港因上市门槛过高而流失潜在的创新型企业。这反映了全球资本市场对科技和创新企业的激烈争夺，以及香港巩固其国际金融中心地位的战略调整。
These reforms aim to counter competitive measures from rival exchanges in the US, UK, Singapore and Japan to attract IPOs, preventing Hong Kong from losing potential innovative firms due to high listing barriers. This reflects the global capital markets' intense competition for technology and innovation companies, as well as Hong Kong's strategic adjustment to consolidate its position as an international financial center.

我能用什么 / How I can use it

对于投资者，可关注即将登陆港股的中小型创新企业投资机会，尤其是估值在200-400亿港元区间的科技公司；对于创业者或企业高管，可重新评估香港作为上市地的可行性，特别是考虑保密提交选项带来的战略灵活性；对于金融从业者，需深入研究新规细则，为客户提供上市路径规划的专业建议。
For investors, watch for opportunities in smaller innovative companies, especially tech firms valued between HK$20-40 billion, that may now qualify for HKEX listing. For entrepreneurs or executives, reassess Hong Kong's feasibility as a listing venue, particularly considering the strategic flexibility offered by confidential filing. For finance professionals, study the new rules in detail to provide clients with professional guidance on listing pathway planning.
