This is a project report about maker Simone Giertz's design of a shape-changing fruit bowl. The project uses an iris mechanism similar to a camera aperture, allowing the bowl's base to expand radially and automatically adjust its capacity based on the amount of fruit, solving the common problem of traditional fruit bowls being either too empty or too full.
