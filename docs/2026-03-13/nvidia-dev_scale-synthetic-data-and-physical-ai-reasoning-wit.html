Scale Synthetic Data and Physical AI Reasoning with NVIDIA Cosmos World Foundation Models

NVIDIA Developer Blog 2026-03-13
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

NVIDIA Cosmos 是一套世界基础模型（World Foundation Models, WFM）平台，旨在通过生成式 AI 加速物理 AI 系统的开发。它包含三大核心组件：Cosmos Transfer（从结构化输入生成逼真视频）、Cosmos Predict（预测未来世界状态）和 Cosmos Reason（物理感知推理），专门用于解决机器人（如人形机器人）和自动驾驶汽车等领域中真实数据收集昂贵、稀缺的问题。
NVIDIA Cosmos is a World Foundation Models (WFM) platform that uses generative AI to accelerate the development of physical AI systems. It comprises three core components: Cosmos Transfer (generating photorealistic videos from structured inputs), Cosmos Predict (forecasting future world states), and Cosmos Reason (physics-grounded reasoning), specifically designed to address the challenges of expensive and scarce real-world data collection in robotics (e.g., humanoids) and autonomous vehicles. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

物理 AI 系统的训练瓶颈在于高质量、多样化数据的获取——真实世界数据采集成本高昂且耗时，而数据不足会导致模型泛化能力差、边缘案例处理不可预测。
Cosmos 通过合成数据生成和物理感知推理，将数据生成成本大幅降低，同时提升模型在长尾场景和长时序预测中的准确性，是推动具身智能（Embodied AI）从实验室走向规模化部署的关键基础设施。 The training bottleneck for physical AI systems lies in acquiring high-quality, diverse data—real-world collection is costly and time-consuming, while insufficient data leads to poor generalization and unpredictable edge-case handling. By enabling synthetic data generation and physics-aware reasoning, Cosmos dramatically reduces data generation costs and improves model accuracy in long-tail scenarios and long-horizon predictions, serving as critical infrastructure for scaling Embodied AI from labs to real-world deployment. ---

我能用什么 / How I can use it

开发者可将 Cosmos Transfer 与 NVIDIA Omniverse 结合，从仿真环境生成带标注的逼真训练数据，用于机器人操作或自动驾驶感知模型的后训练；利用 Cosmos Predict 的 30 秒长序列生成能力，构建世界模型以支持策略学习；或通过 Cosmos Reason 的链式思维推理，开发能理解物理交互、预测最优动作的感知 AI 或具身智能体。具体可参考 NVIDIA Cosmos Cookbook 中的推理指南和后训练指南快速上手。
Developers can combine Cosmos Transfer with NVIDIA Omniverse to generate annotated, photorealistic training data from simulations for post-training robot manipulation or autonomous vehicle perception models; leverage Cosmos Predict's 30-second long-sequence generation to build world models for policy learning; or use Cosmos Reason's chain-of-thought reasoning to develop perception AI or embodied agents that understand physical interactions and predict optimal actions. Quick-start resources include the inference and post-training guides in the NVIDIA Cosmos Cookbook.
