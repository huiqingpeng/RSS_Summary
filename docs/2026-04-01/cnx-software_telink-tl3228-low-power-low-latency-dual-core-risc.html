Telink TL3228 – Low-power, low-latency dual-core RISC-V wireless MCU supports Bluetooth 6.0, 802.15.4, and 2.4 GHz proprietary

是什么 / What it is

泰凌微电子（Telink）发布了 TL322x 系列无线 MCU 的首款产品 TL3228，这是一款基于双核 RISC-V 架构的低功耗、低延迟无线微控制器，支持蓝牙 6.0、Matter、Thread、Zigbee、RF4CE 以及 2.4GHz 专有协议，面向智能家居、资产追踪和高性能游戏外设等应用场景。
Telink has launched the TL3228, the first product in its TL322x wireless MCU family, featuring a dual-core RISC-V architecture with low power consumption and low latency, supporting Bluetooth 6.0, Matter, Thread, Zigbee, RF4CE, and proprietary 2.4GHz protocols for smart home, asset tracking, and high-performance gaming accessories. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

TL3228 集成了最新的蓝牙 6.0 关键特性，包括信道探测（Channel Sounding）用于高精度测距、AoA/AoD 定位服务以及 LE Audio，同时通过专有 HDT 技术实现高达 6 Mbps 的数据速率和超低延迟，使其在定位精度和实时响应方面具有显著优势。此外，其多协议支持和丰富的外设接口（USB-HS、CAN-FD、I3C 等）使其成为物联网边缘设备的理想选择，有望推动智能家居、工业传感和新一代人机交互设备的创新。
The TL3228 integrates key Bluetooth 6.0 features including Channel Sounding for high-precision ranging, AoA/AoD location services, and LE Audio, while delivering up to 6 Mbps data rate and ultra-low latency through proprietary HDT technology, giving it significant advantages in positioning accuracy and real-time responsiveness. Its multi-protocol support and rich peripheral interfaces (USB-HS, CAN-FD, I3C, etc.) make it an ideal choice for IoT edge devices, potentially driving innovation in smart home, industrial sensing, and next-generation human-machine interaction devices. ---

我能用什么 / How I can use it

开发者可以利用 Telink 开源的 GitHub SDK 和 Telink IoT Studio IDE（支持 Windows/Linux）快速启动项目，结合约 70 美元的 AIOT-DK1 开发套件进行原型验证；其多连接能力（4 主 4 从）和丰富接口特别适合开发复杂的智能家居中枢、低延迟游戏手柄或键盘，以及需要精确定位的资产追踪标签。对于需要高集成度和长续航的电池供电设备，可重点评估其深度睡眠 0.7 µA 的功耗表现和多种封装选项（QFN40/56/80、WLCSP59）的灵活性。
Developers can leverage Telink's open-source GitHub SDKs and Telink IoT Studio IDE (Windows/Linux supported) for rapid project initiation, using the approximately $70 AIOT-DK1 development kit for prototyping; its multi-connection capability (4 central, 4 peripheral) and rich interfaces are particularly suitable for complex smart home hubs, low-latency gaming controllers or keyboards, and asset tracking tags requiring precise positioning. For battery-powered devices requiring high integration and long endurance, evaluate its 0.7 µA deep sleep power consumption and flexible packaging options (QFN40/56/80, WLCSP59).
