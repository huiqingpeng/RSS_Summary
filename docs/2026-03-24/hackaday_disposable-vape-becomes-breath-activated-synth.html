Disposable Vape Becomes Breath-Activated Synth

Hackaday 2026-03-24
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

这篇文章介绍了一个将一次性电子烟（ElfBars BC5000）改造为呼吸激活合成器的硬件黑客项目。制作者Becky Stern拆除了电子烟的外壳，保留了锂电池、USB充电板和低压传感器，并将其与一个基于555定时器的合成器电路结合，通过吸气触发声音，利用光敏电阻控制音高。
This article showcases a hardware hacking project that repurposes a disposable vape (ElfBars BC5000) into a breath-activated synthesizer. Creator Becky Stern gutted the vape, retaining the LiPo battery, USB charging board, and low-pressure sensor, then combined these with a 555 timer-based synthesizer circuit triggered by inhalation, with pitch controlled by photoresistors.

这个项目体现了电子废弃物再利用的创意潜力，将原本被丢弃的一次性电子烟转化为有趣的音乐装置，符合当下创客文化中的可持续设计理念。同时，它也展示了低成本传感器（如低压传感器）在互动艺术和DIY电子项目中的创新应用价值。
This project demonstrates the creative potential of e-waste upcycling, transforming discarded disposable vapes into an intriguing musical device—aligning with sustainable design principles in maker culture. It also highlights innovative applications of low-cost sensors (like low-pressure sensors) in interactive art and DIY electronics projects.
