是什么 / What it is

这篇文章介绍了一款名为"Sirenotron"的模拟合成器，它基于经典的555定时器芯片和LM358运算放大器构建，专门用于产生dub音乐中标志性的警笛音效。该设备具备LFO（低频振荡器）功能，支持三角波和方波模式，以及一个通过添加单个电容实现的"酸性模式"，可通过脚踏开关控制，方便音乐人在现场演出时使用。
This article introduces an analog synthesizer called "Sirenotron" built around the classic 555 timer IC and LM358 op-amp, designed specifically to produce the iconic siren sounds found in dub music. The device features an LFO (low-frequency oscillator) with triangle and square wave modes, plus an "acid mode" achieved by adding a single capacitor, and can be controlled via a footswitch for live performances. ---
