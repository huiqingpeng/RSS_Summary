Modular 18650 Packs, No Spot Welding Required

是什么 / What it is

Cell-Lock 是一种免焊接的模块化18650电池组装系统，通过旋转式锁扣端盖和连接件实现机械固定与电气连接，灵感源自家具组装中的凸轮结构。
Cell-Lock is a spot-welding-free modular battery assembly system for 18650 cells that uses rotating locking cam end caps and connection pieces for both mechanical fixation and electrical connections, inspired by cam systems used in furniture assembly.

为什么重要 / Why it matters

该系统消除了传统点焊所需的高温操作，降低了电池热损伤风险，同时支持灵活配置不同电池组布局；若连接可靠性和载流能力得到验证，可为DIY爱好者和小批量生产提供更安全的替代方案。
This system eliminates high-temperature operations required by traditional spot welding, reducing thermal damage risks to cells while enabling flexible configurations; if connection reliability and current-carrying capacity are validated, it could offer a safer alternative for DIY enthusiasts and small-scale production.

我能用什么 / How I can use it
