是什么 / What it is

AWOL Vision Aetherion 是一款基于联发科 MT9655 芯片的 4K 超短焦 RGB 激光投影仪，配备 Android TV 14 系统、8GB 内存和 128GB 存储空间，支持 2.5GbE 网络传输。该设备采用德州仪器 0.47 英寸 DLP 芯片配合 e-shift 像素位移技术实现真 4K 分辨率，提供 Pro 和 Max 两种亮度版本（分别为 2600 和 3300 ISO 流明），目前正在 Kickstarter 众筹，预计 2026 年 4 月发货。
The AWOL Vision Aetherion is a 4K Ultra Short Throw (UST) RGB laser projector powered by the MediaTek MT9655 SoC with Android TV 14, featuring 8GB RAM, 128GB storage, and 2.5GbE networking. It uses a Texas Instruments 0.47-inch DLP chip with e-shift pixel-shifting technology to achieve true 4K resolution, available in Pro and Max variants (2,600 and 3,300 ISO lumens respectively), currently crowdfunding on Kickstarter with shipping expected in April 2026. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

这款投影仪代表了家用投影技术的重大进步：它将 RGB 三色激光光源、110% Rec.2020 广色域、VRR 可变刷新率等高端特性整合到消费级设备中，同时通过 PixelLock 光学技术解决了超短焦投影常见的边缘模糊问题。其众筹金额已突破 1800 万美元，显示出市场对高性能一体化家庭影院解决方案的强烈需求，也预示着激光投影正在从专业影院向客厅娱乐场景加速普及。
This projector represents a significant advancement in home projection technology by integrating premium features—RGB triple-laser light source, 110% Rec.2020 color gamut, and Variable Refresh Rate—into a consumer-grade device while solving edge-blur issues common in UST optics through PixelLock technology. Its $18 million+ crowdfunding success demonstrates strong market demand for high-performance all-in-one home theater solutions, signaling that laser projection is accelerating from professional cinemas into living room entertainment. ---
