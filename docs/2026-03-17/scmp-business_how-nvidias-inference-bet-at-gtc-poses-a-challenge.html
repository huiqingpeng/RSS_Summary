是什么 / What it is

Nvidia unveiled its Groq 3 Language Processing Unit (LPU) at GTC 2026, integrating it into the Vera Rubin platform to shift from selling individual chips to "AI factories"—complete rack systems designed for agentic AI inference workloads. This move intensifies the competitive gap with China while simultaneously creating niche market opportunities for Chinese semiconductor firms. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

中美AI芯片差距已从单一硬件性能扩展到整个AI生产流程的标准化和系统级主导地位，这对中国半导体产业的自主可控发展构成严峻挑战。与此同时，AI推理市场的碎片化特性——并非所有工作负载都运行在数据中心——为中国厂商提供了差异化竞争的突破口。
The China-US AI chip gap has evolved from individual hardware performance to system-level dominance and standardization of the entire AI production pipeline, posing significant challenges to China's semiconductor self-sufficiency efforts. Meanwhile, the fragmentation of the AI inference market—where not all workloads run in data centers—offers Chinese players potential entry points for differentiated competition. ---
