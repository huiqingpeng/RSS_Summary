Mini review of the ThinkNode M6 “outdoor solar power for Meshtastic”

CNX Software 2026-03-17
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

这是一篇对 Elecrow ThinkNode M6 太阳能户外 Meshtastic 设备的评测文章。作者 Jean-Luc 在九个月前评测过同系列的 M1 和 M2 节点后，此次测试这款配备 6W 太阳能板和 7000mAh 电池的新型户外路由器，主要目的是验证其作为中继节点扩展通信范围的能力。
This is a review of the Elecrow ThinkNode M6, a solar-powered outdoor Meshtastic device. Author Jean-Luc, who previously reviewed the M1 and M2 nodes nine months ago, tests this new model featuring a 6W solar panel and 7000mAh battery, primarily to verify its capability as a relay node for extending communication range. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

评测结果显示 M6 作为路由器可将通信范围从原来的 550 米点对点扩展到约 3.2 公里直径覆盖，提升约 6 倍，证明了中继节点对 LoRa 网状网络的关键价值。这也反映出 Meshtastic 生态面临的现实挑战：设备成本高于入门级智能手机、功能受限且应用体验不佳，导致即使在泰国第二大城市也难以形成用户社区。
The test results demonstrate that the M6 as a router can extend communication range from 550 meters point-to-point to approximately 3.2km diameter coverage—about 6× improvement—proving the critical value of relay nodes for LoRa mesh networks. This also reflects real challenges facing the Meshtastic ecosystem: hardware costs exceeding entry-level smartphones, limited functionality, and poor app usability, which prevent community formation even in Thailand's second-largest city. ---

我能用什么 / How I can use it

对于需要构建低成本、离网通信网络的场景，可考虑部署太阳能 LoRa 路由器作为固定中继节点，显著扩展覆盖范围；建议优先选择预装 Meshtastic 固件的版本以简化配置，并注意统一频率槽位等参数避免连接问题。此外，可探索 UART/I2C 接口扩展传感器应用，或推动社区降低设备成本、改善软件体验以促进技术普及。
For building low-cost, off-grid communication networks, consider deploying solar LoRa routers as fixed relay nodes to significantly extend coverage; prioritize pre-flashed Meshtastic versions to simplify setup, and ensure unified parameters like frequency slots to avoid connectivity issues. Additionally, explore UART/I2C interfaces for sensor integration, or advocate for lower hardware costs and improved software experience to drive technology adoption.
