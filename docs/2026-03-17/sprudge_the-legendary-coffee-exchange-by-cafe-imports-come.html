The Legendary Coffee Exchange By Cafe Imports Comes To World Of Coffee San Diego

是什么 / What it is

传奇咖啡交换（Legendary Coffee Exchange）是由咖啡生豆进口商Cafe Imports主办的一项咖啡交流活动，起源于2021年疫情期间，旨在让烘焙师们互相品尝对方的咖啡。该活动现已发展为第五届线下活动，将在2025年4月圣地亚哥世界咖啡展（World of Coffee San Diego）期间举行。
The Legendary Coffee Exchange is a coffee swapping event hosted by green coffee importer Cafe Imports, which originated in 2021 during the pandemic to help roasters connect and taste each other's coffee. Now in its fifth in-person edition, it will take place during the World of Coffee San Diego in April 2025.

为什么重要 / Why it matters

这一活动体现了 Specialty Coffee 社区在疫情后时代对人际连接和知识共享的持续重视，超越了单纯的商业交易。它通过"以咖啡换咖啡"的简单机制，促进了烘焙师之间的深度交流，同时借助社交媒体抽奖（Fellow意式咖啡机）扩大了活动影响力，成为行业展会上兼具趣味性和专业性的标志性活动。
This event reflects the specialty coffee community's continued emphasis on human connection and knowledge sharing in the post-pandemic era, going beyond pure commercial transactions. Its simple "coffee-for-coffee" mechanism fosters deeper exchanges among roasters, while the social media giveaway (Fellow espresso machine) amplifies its reach, making it a signature event that balances fun and professionalism at industry trade shows.
