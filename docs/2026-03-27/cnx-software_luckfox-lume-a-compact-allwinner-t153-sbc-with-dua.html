是什么 / What it is

Luckfox Lume 是一款面向工业 HMI（人机界面）应用的紧凑型单板计算机，搭载全志 T153 四核 Cortex-A7 SoC（集成低功耗 RISC-V 协处理器），配备 128MB DDR3 内存、256MB SPI NAND 闪存、双千兆网口、MIPI 显示/摄像头接口及 40 针 GPIO 扩展槽。
Luckfox Lume is a compact industrial SBC for HMI applications, featuring an Allwinner T153 quad-core Cortex-A7 SoC with a low-power RISC-V co-processor, 128MB DDR3, 256MB SPI NAND flash, dual GbE ports, MIPI DSI/CSI interfaces, and a 40-pin GPIO header.

The board bridges low-cost edge computing and industrial automation by offering industrial-grade networking (dual GbE with optional PoE) and multimedia interfaces at an extremely low price point ($20-30), making it ideal for budget-constrained IoT gateways and touchscreen control panels.
