Understand Your Printer Better With The Interactive Inkjet Simulator

Hackaday 2026-03-27
Read Original Article →

是什么 / What it is

Dennis Kuppens 开发了一个开源的交互式喷墨打印模拟器，专注于功能性打印（如导电油墨、光掩模等），而非图形打印。该工具允许用户手动调整喷头参数或选择预配置的工业打印机，实时观察墨滴沉积过程，并以 AGPL-3.0 许可证发布。
Dennis Kuppens developed an open-source interactive inkjet printing simulator focused on functional printing (e.g., conductive ink, light masks) rather than graphics printing. The tool allows users to manually adjust nozzle parameters or select pre-configured industrial printers, observe droplet deposition in real-time, and is released under the AGPL-3.0 license. ---

为什么重要 / Why it matters

功能性打印对精度要求极高，单个墨滴错位都可能导致产品失效，这与容错性较高的图形打印有本质区别。该模拟器填补了教育与实践之间的空白，帮助用户直观理解喷墨物理机制；同时其开源特性为社区进一步开发工业级打印优化工具提供了基础。
Functional printing demands extreme precision where a single misplaced droplet can cause failure, fundamentally differing from fault-tolerant graphics printing. This simulator bridges the gap between education and practice, helping users intuitively understand inkjet physics; its open-source nature also provides a foundation for community development of industrial-grade printing optimization tools. ---

我能用什么 / How I can use it

电子制造或材料科学领域的工程师可用它来优化导电油墨打印参数，减少实际试错的材料成本；教育工作者可将其作为可视化教学工具讲解微液滴动力学；开发者可基于 AGPL-3.0 许可证扩展功能，如添加多材料模拟或机器学习驱动的参数优化模块。
Engineers in electronics manufacturing or materials science can use it to optimize conductive ink printing parameters and reduce material costs from physical trial-and-error; educators can employ it as a visualization tool for teaching micro-droplet dynamics; developers can extend functionality under the AGPL-3.0 license, such as adding multi-material simulation or ML-driven parameter optimization modules.
